DTC Troubleshooting: B1389
DTC B1389:
 Power Tailgate Drive Motor Clutch Circuit Malfunction
  1. Open the tailgate with the tailgate outer handle.
  1. Close the tailgate automatically with the inside tailgate switch.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0) and then back to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC B1389 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for loose or poor connections.■
  1. Check the No. 3-2 (PTG MTR) (40 A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Replace the blown fuse and recheck. If the fuse is blown again, check for a short to ground in the wire.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect power tailgate control unit connector A (5P).
  1. Measure the voltage between power tailgate control unit connector A (5P) No. 2 terminal and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
An open in the wire.■
  1. Disconnect power tailgate control unit connector B (20P).
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and power tailgate control unit connector B (20P) No. 1 and No. 2 terminals.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 12 .
NO -
Go to Step 14 .
  1. Disconnect power tailgate drive unit connector B (8P).
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and power tailgate control unit connector B (20P) No. 1 and No. 2 terminals.
Is there continuity?
YES -
A short to ground in the wire.■
NO -
Replace the power tailgate drive unit. ■
  1. Disconnect power tailgate drive unit connector B (8P).
  1. Check for continuity between power tailgate control unit connector B (20P) No. 1 and No. 2 terminals and power tailgate drive unit connector B (8P) No. 1 and No. 3 terminals.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 16 .
NO -
An open in the wire.■
  1. Check for continuity between power tailgate drive unit connector B (8P) No. 1 and No. 3 terminals.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Replace the power tailgate control unit. ■
NO -
Replace the power tailgate drive unit. ■
