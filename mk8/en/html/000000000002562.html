DTC Troubleshooting: P0980 (22)
DTC P0980:
 Open in Shift Solenoid Valve C Circuit
NOTE:
  • Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and any on-board snapshot with the HDS, and review General Troubleshooting Information.
  • This code is caused by an electrical circuit problem and cannot be caused by a mechanical problem in the transmission.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine in P, and wait for at least 1 second.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0980 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Go to Step 5 .
  1. Select Shift Solenoid Valve C in the Miscellaneous Test Menu, and test shift solenoid valve C with the HDS.
Is a clicking sound heard?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Go to Step 8 .
  1. Start the engine in P, and wait for at least 1 second.
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for P0980 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the HDS indicate FAILED?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
If the HDS indicates PASSED, intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals between shift solenoid valve C and the TCM. If the HDS indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 5 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect the TCM connector.
  1. Check for continuity between TCM connector terminals No. 52 and body ground, and between No. 59 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 11 .
NO -
Repair open in the wires between TCM connector terminals No. 52, No. 59 and body ground (G151), or repair poor body ground (G151), then go to Step 11 .
  1. Measure the resistance between TCM connector terminals No. 15 and No. 52 or No. 59.
Is there 12−25 Ω?
YES -
Go to Step 22 .
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Disconnect the shift solenoid wire harness connector.
  1. Check for continuity between TCM connector terminal No. 15 and shift solenoid wire harness connector terminal No. 2.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 14 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between TCM connector terminal No. 15 and the shift solenoid wire harness connector, then go to Step 16 .
  1. Remove the shift solenoid wire harness.
  1. Check for continuity between shift solenoid wire harness connector terminal No. 2 and the shift solenoid valve C connector terminal.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Replace shift solenoid valve C, then go to Step 19 .
NO -
Replace the shift solenoid wire harness, then go to Step 19 .
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine in P, and wait for at least 1 second, then test-drive the vehicle in D. Drive the vehicle in 1st gear for at least 1 second, then slow down to a stop.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0980 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals between shift solenoid valve C and the TCM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Go to Step 21 .
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for P0980 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the HDS indicate PASSED?
YES -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs were indicated in Step 20 , go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
NO -
If the HDS indicates FAILED, check for poor connections or loose terminals between shift solenoid valve C and the TCM, then go to Step 1 . If the HDS indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 19 .
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Update the TCM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good TCM.
  1. Start the engine in P, and wait for at least 1 second, then test-drive the vehicle in D. Drive the vehicle in 1st gear for at least 1 second, then slow down to a stop.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0980 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals between shift solenoid valve C and the TCM. If the TCM was updated, substitute a known-good TCM, then go to Step 24 . If the TCM was substituted, go to Step 1 .
NO -
Go to Step 26 .
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for P0980 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the HDS indicate PASSED?
YES -
If the TCM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the TCM was substituted, replace the original TCM. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs were indicated in Step 25 , go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
NO -
If the HDS indicates FAILED, check for poor connections or loose terminals between shift solenoid valve C and the TCM. If the TCM was updated, substitute a known-good TCM, then go to Step 24 . If the TCM was substituted, go to Step 1 . If the HDS indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 24 .
