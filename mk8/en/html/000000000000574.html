Rear Power Window Motor Test - Without All Power Windows AUTO UP/DOWN Function
Without all power windows AUTO UP/AUTO DOWN function
  1. Remove the rear power window switch.
  1. Test the motor in each direction by connecting battery power and ground to the rear power window switch 14P connector (A) according to the table.
  1. If the motor does not run or fails to run smoothly, go to Step 4 .
  1. Remove the door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 2P connector (A) from the rear power window motor (B).
  1. Check for continuity between the rear power window switch 14P connector No. 1 and No. 7 terminals and the rear power window motor 2P connector No. 1 and No. 2 terminals respectively. There should be continuity.

    No. 1 terminal
    No. 7 terminal
    No. 2 terminal

  1. If the wire harness is OK, replace the rear power window motor.
