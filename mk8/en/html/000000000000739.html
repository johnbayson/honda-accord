DTC Troubleshooting: B1126
DTC B1126:
 Driver's Power Window Motor Pulse B Error
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0) and then back to ON (II).
  1. Open and close the driver's power window by using the power window master switch manually.
