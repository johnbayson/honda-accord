Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) DTC Troubleshooting: 105
DTC 105:
 Dust or Dirt on the Millimeter Wave Radar
NOTE:
  • When driving on a road with little traffic and minimal reflecting objects for the millimeter wave radar, DTC 105 is stored temporally. Also DTC 105 is sometimes stored near overpasses, because, and bodies of water. Check for dirt on the front surface of the millimeter wave radar.
  • When the vehicle is towing a trailer, and the front of the vehicle is pointed higher than normal, DTC 105 is stored temporarily.
  1. Check for dust or dirt on the millimeter wave radar.
Is the surface dirty?
YES -
Clean the millimeter wave radar, then recheck the system.■
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Remove the front grille cover.
  1. Check the installation of the emblem.
Is the emblem installed correctly?
YES -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
NO -
Reinstall the emblem or the front grille cover grille correctly. If damaged, replace it.■
