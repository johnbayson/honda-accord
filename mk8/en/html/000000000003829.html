Fuel Filter Removal/Installation
M/T model
  1. Do the battery terminal disconnection procedure.
  1. Remove the strut brace.
  1. Remove the cover (A), then remove the two positive (+) terminals (B) from the under-hood fuse/relay box.
  1. Remove the under-hood fuse/relay box from the bracket.
  1. Disconnect the fuel heater connector (A), and the fuel filter water level switch connector (B), then remove the harness clamp (C).
  1. Remove the fuel feed hoses (D), then remove the fuel filter assembly (E).
    NOTE: When installing the fuel feed hoses, use the new O-rings (F).
  1. Remove the harness bracket (A) and the fuel filter bracket (B).
  1. Remove the fuel filter protector (C) and the fuel filter mounting bracket (D) from the fuel filter.
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removel with new O-rings.
    NOTE: Coat the new O-ring with clean fuel.
  1. Do the battery terminal reconnection procedure.
