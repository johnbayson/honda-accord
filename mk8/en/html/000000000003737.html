Audio System Self-diagnostic Function - Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) System
How to Check the Active Noise Cancellation System in this Check Mode
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II). Turn the audio unit off.
  1. Push and hold the No. 1 and No. 6 buttons. While holding the buttons, push the VOL knob to on. Release the buttons and the self-diagnostic function begins.
  1. Press the No. 1 button to enter the active noise cancellation (ANC) system check mode ANC on appears on the display. If the hum sound for a second, check for an open in the wire between the active noise control unit and the microphone.
    NOTE: The active noise cancellation system is cancelled when any door is opened.
  1. The self-diagnostic function ends when you turn the audio unit off, or turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
