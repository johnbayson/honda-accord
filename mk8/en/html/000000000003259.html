Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) DTC Troubleshooting: 3
LKAS Control Unit DTC 3:
 A Problem with the Steering Angle Sensor A/B Phases
NOTE: Information marked with an asterisk ( *1 ) applies to LHD model, an asterisk ( *2 ) applies to RHD model.
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), then turn it to ON (II).
  1. Turn the steering wheel 90 degrees or more to the right, and wait 20 seconds or more.
  1. Watch the LKAS indicator.
Does the LKAS indicator come on?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC 3 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Troubleshoot the indicated DTCs.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between body ground and steering angle sensor 5P connector No. 2, No. 3 and No. 4 terminals individually.
Is there 5 V or more?
YES -
Repair short to power in the wire between the LKAS control unit and the steering angle sensor.■
NO -
Go to Step 8 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect the steering angle sensor 5P connector, VSA modulator-control unit 47P connector and LKAS control unit 26P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and steering angle sensor 5P connector No. 2, No. 3, and No. 4 terminals individually.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to body ground in the wire between the LKAS control unit and the steering angle sensor.■
NO -
Go to Step 11 .
  1. Check for continuity between the following terminals of the LKAS control unit 26P connector and steering angle sensor 5P connector.
    26P:
    5P:
    No. 5
    No. 4
    No. 18
    No. 2

Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 12 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the LKAS control unit and the steering angle sensor.■
  1. Check for continuity between LKAS control unit 26P connector No. 6 terminal and steering angle sensor 5P connector No. 3 terminal.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 13 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the LKAS control unit and the steering angle sensor.■
  1. Check for VSA modulator-control unit DTCs with HDS.
Are any DTCs indicated?
YES -
Troubleshoot the VSA modulator-control unit DTCs.■
NO -
Check for loose terminals in the LKAS control unit connectors, and repair if necessary. If all connections are OK, replace the LKAS control unit. ■
