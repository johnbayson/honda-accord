M/T Assembly Installation
Special Tools Required

Subhanger stay
 07MAK-PY30100

Subframe alignment pin
 070AG-SJAA10S

Engine support hanger
 AAR-T1256-J00
*
*: This special tool is available from Snap-on Tools    International, LLC.
NOTE: Use fender covers to avoid damaging painted surfaces.
  1. Make sure the two dowel pins (A) are installed prescribed hole on the engine block as shown.
  1. Check the release bearing, and reinstall the release bearing and the release fork with the appropriate grease.
  1. Place the transmission on the transmission jack, and raise it to engine level.
  1. Align the transmission mainshaft and the clutch pressure plate, then move the transmission inward until there is no gap between the transmission housing and the engine block.
  1. Install the lower transmission mounting bolts (A).
Front side
Rear side
  1. Install the DPF converter mounting bracket (A).
  1. Install the clutch case cover (A).
  1. Install the intermediate shaft.
  1. Install both driveshafts.
  1. Support the front subframe with a piece of wood and a jack.
  1. Position the steering gearbox washers (A) on the front subframe (B), and lift up the subframe until it is about 10 mm (0.4 in.) away from the frame.
  1. Loosely install new four front subframe mounting bolts with the stiffeners, new rear stiffener mounting bolts (C), and the front stiffener mounting bolts (D).
  1. Align the front subframe using the subframe alignment pin (A). Vertically install the subframe alignment pin, and align the right-rear corner of the subframe and vehicle frame holes, then loosely tighten the subframe mounting bolt (B) until the front subframe contacts the body frame.
  1. Loosely tighten the left-rear subframe mounting bolt using the same procedure as the right-rear with the subframe alignment pin.
  1. Partially tighten the right and left front subframe mounting bolts.
  1. Tighten the right-rear subframe mounting bolt to the specified torque with the subframe alignment pin in the positioning hole.
  1. Tighten the left-rear subframe mounting bolt to the specified torque with the subframe alignment pin in the positioning hole.
  1. Tighten the right and left front subframe mounting bolts (A) to the specified torque.
  1. Tighten the rear and front stiffener mounting bolts to the specified torque.
  1. Retorque all of the front subframe mounting bolts.
  1. Remove the jack and wood piece.
  1. Connect the EPS ground subharness connector at the front of the front subframe.
  1. Install both sides of the front subframe mid mount (A) with new bolts.
  1. Install new lower torque rod connecting bolts (A) to the bracket on the engine block.
  1. Install exhaust pipe A with the gaskets.
  1. Refill the MTF to the proper level.
  1. Connect the knuckle ball joint onto the lower arm.
  1. Install the damper fork.
  1. With HID lamp model: Connect the front suspension stroke sensor connector.
  1. Clean the mating surfaces of the brake disc and the inside of the wheel, then install the front wheels.
  1. Lower the vehicle on the lift.
  1. Install the upper transmission mounting bolts (A) with the harness bracket (B).
  1. Install the engine harness clamps (C) to the harness bracket.
  1. Connect the reverse lockout solenoid connector (A).
  1. Position the transmission mount (A), and loosely install new transmission mount bolts (B) to the body frame.
  1. Loosely install new transmission mount bolt (A) and nuts (B) to the transmission housing.
  1. Install the ground cable (C) to the transmission mount.
  1. Remove the engine support hanger (AAR-T1256-J00) and the subhanger stay (07MAK-PY30100).
  1. Tighten the transmission mount bolts and nuts to the specified torque.
  1. Install the engine cover.
  1. Install the engine harness clamps (A) to the side of the engine room.
  1. Set the steering gearbox into position, then loosely install the front side steering gearbox mounting bolts (A) with the washers (B).
Driver's side
Passenger's side
  1. Position the stiffener (A), and loosely install the rear side steering gearbox mounting bolts (B) and new stiffener mounting bolt (C).
    NOTE: Make sure the lower washers (D) placed in Step 11 are correctly positioned before installing the bolts.
  1. Tighten the front/driver's side of the steering gearbox mounting bolt (A) to the specified torque.
  1. Tighten the front/passenger's side of the steering gearbox mounting bolt (B) to the specified torque.
  1. Tighten the rear/driver's side of the steering gearbox mounting bolt (C) to the specified torque.
  1. Tighten the rear/passenger's side of the steering gearbox mounting bolt (D) to the specified torque.
  1. Tighten the stiffener mounting bolt (E) to the specified torque.
  1. Retorque all of the steering gearbox mounting bolts and the stiffener mounting bolt as same sequence that they were tightened.
  1. Install transmission hanger D to the transmission housing, and connect the back-up light switch connector (A).
  1. Connect the output shaft (countershaft) speed sensor connector (B).
  1. Apply a light coat of silicone grease to the cable ends (A), and connect the cable end to the select lever (B) and the change lever (C), then install the shift cable bracket (D) and the lock pins (E). Do not bend or damage the shift cables.
    NOTE: Make sure not to get any silicone grease on the terminal part of the connectors and switches, especially if you have silicone grease on your hands or gloves.
  1. Install the under-hood fuse/relay box (A).
  1. Apply a light coat of molybdenum grease to the end of the slave cylinder rod (A).
  1. Install the clutch hose bracket (B), then install the slave cylinder (C). Do not bend or damage the clutch line.
  1. Install the condenser fan shroud assembly and the radiator fan shroud assembly.
  1. Install the splash shield.
  1. Install the ECM, then connect the ECM connectors.
  1. Install the strut brace.
  1. Install the front grille cover.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing.
  1. Install the intake air duct.
  1. Do the battery installation procedure.
  1. Check the wheel alignment.
  1. Check the shift lever and the clutch operation.
  1. Test-drive the vehicle.
