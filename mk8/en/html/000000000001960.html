EGR Valve Replacement
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Remove the strut brace.
  1. Disconnect and plug the water bypass hoses (A).
  1. Disconnect the EGR valve connector (B).
  1. Remove the bolts (C) and nuts (D).
  1. Lift the vehicle, and support it with jackstands.
  1. Remove the bolt (A).
  1. Lower the vehicle.
  1. Remove the EGR valve (A) and the EGR pipe B.
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal with the new gaskets (C).
  1. Refill the radiator with engine coolant.
