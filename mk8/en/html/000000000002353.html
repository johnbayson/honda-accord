Fuel Filter Element Removal and Installation
Special Tools Required

Fuel filter wrench
 070AA-RL00200
Removal (A/T model)
  1. Raise the vehicle on a lift.
  1. Remove the fuel filter cover (A).
  1. Loosen the drain plug (B) by a wrench.
  1. Loosen the drain plug until about 3−5 mm by a hand.
  1. Connect the hose (piping diameter 6 mm), and place a suitable container under the hose to catch up the water and/or fuel.
  1. Remove the fuel filter.
  1. Remove the outlet joint (A).
  1. Hold the fuel filter housing.
  1. Attach the fuel filter wrench, and loosen the cap (A).
  1. Remove the fuel filter element (B).
    NOTE: Clean inside of the fuel filter housing, if needed.
Installation (A/T model)
  1. Install a new O-ring (A), and the new filter element (B) to the fuel filter cap (C).
    NOTE: Coat the new O-ring with clean fuel.
  1. Insert the fuel cap to the fuel filter housing. Using the special tool, tighten the cap to the specified torque.
    NOTE: After tightening, make sure the marks (D) are still aligned.
  1. Coat the new O-rings (A) with clean fuel, then install the drain plug (B) and inlet joint (C) with the O-rings.
  1. Install the fuel filter.
  1. Wipe off the fuel on the floor.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II), and wait about 40 seconds, then check for fuel leakage.
  1. Confirm the engine can be started.
