Cam Chain Sprocket Replacement
Removal
NOTE: Keep the cam chain away from magnetic fields.
  1. Remove the cylinder head cover.
  1. Remove the camshaft position (CMP) sensor.
  1. Set the No. 1 piston at top dead center (TDC). Align the punch mark on the intake camshaft gear in the center of the two punch marks on the exhaust camshaft gear.
  1. Make reference marks (A) across the cam chain sprocket and cam chain.
  1. Remove the auto-tensioner.
  1. Hold the camshaft with an open-end wrench, then loosen the cam chain sprocket mounting bolt.
  1. Remove the cam chain sprocket mounting bolt (A), then remove the cam chain sprocket (B).
Installation
NOTE: Keep the cam chain away from magnetic fields.
  1. Install the cam chain to the cam chain sprocket by alignment the reference marks (A), then install the cam chain sprocket on the intake camshaft.
  1. Apply new engine oil to the threads and flange of the cam chain sprocket mounting bolt, and install it. Hold the intake camshaft with an open-end wrench, then tighten the bolt.
    Specified Torque:
    12 x 1.25 mm
    118 N·m (12.0 kgf·m, 86.8 lbf·ft)

  1. Install the auto-tensioner.
  1. Install the CMP sensor.
  1. Install the cylinder head cover.
