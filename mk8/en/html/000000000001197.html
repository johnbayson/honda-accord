Injector Replacement
  1. Relieve fuel pressure.
  1. Remove the fuel pipe cover (A).
  1. Disconnect the injector connectors (B), the MAP sensor connector (C) and the rocker arm oil control valve connector (D).
  1. Remove the quick-connect fitting cover (E), then disconnect the quick-connect fitting (F).
  1. Remove the fuel rail mounting nuts (G) from the fuel rail (H).
  1. Remove the fuel rail and the injectors from the cylinder head (I).
  1. Remove the injector clips (J) from the fuel rail.
  1. Remove the injectors from the fuel rail.
