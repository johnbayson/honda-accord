Front Seat Frame Replacement
Driver's Seat
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • When removing components, use the trim tools or equivalent.
  • If the side airbag has deployed, replace the seat frame and related parts with new ones.
  • LHD model is shown; RHD model is symmetrical.
  1. Remove the front seat.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Front seat-back cover/pad
    • Front seat cushion cover/pad
    • Seat belt buckle
    • Side airbag
  1. Except manual seat: Detach the clips (A, B), then remove the upper rail outer cover (C).
8-way power seat
Manual height adjustable seat
  1. Except manual seat: Detach the clip, then remove the upper rail inner cover (A).
8-way power seat
Manual height adjustable seat
  1. Remove the recline inner cover (A) and the center inner cover (B) from the seat frame (C).
  1. Install the new seat frame in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, replace them with new ones.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
8-way power seat
Manual height adjustable seat/manual seat
Passenger's Seat
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • When removing components, use the trim tools or equivalent.
  • If the side airbag has deployed, replace the seat frame and related parts with new ones.

  1. Remove the front seat.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Front seat-back cover/pad
    • Front seat cushion cover/pad
    • OPDS unit (for some models)
    • Seat belt buckle
    • Side airbag
  1. Remove the recline inner cover (A) and the center inner cover (B) from the seat frame (C).
  1. Install the new seat frame in the reverse order of removal.
