Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) DTC Troubleshooting: 63
DTC 63:
 Millimeter Wave Radar Aiming Error
  1. Watch the ACC indicator and CMBS indicator.
Do the indicator come on?
YES -
Go to Step 2 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC 63 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Troubleshoot the indicated DTC(s). If there are no DTCs, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. Check the installation of the millimeter wave radar and the main bracket.
Is the installation OK?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Reinstall or replace the part that has a problem, then go to Step 5 .
  1. Do the millimeter wave radar aiming procedure.
  1. Watch the ACC indicator and CMBS indicator.
Do the indicator come on?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC 63 indicated?
YES -
Check for loose or poor connections. If the connections are OK, replace the millimeter wave radar. After replacement, do the millimeter wave radar aiming. ■
NO -
Troubleshoot the indicated DTC(s). If there are no DTCs, the system is OK at this time.■
