Power Tailgate Control Unit Input Test/Replacement
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Remove the left cargo area side trim panel.
  1. Disconnect power tailgate connector (A) from the power tailgate control unit (B).
  1. Disconnect power tailgate connector (C) from the power tailgate control unit.
  1. Disconnect power tailgate connector (D) from the power tailgate control unit.
  1. Inspect the connector and socket terminals to be sure they are all making good contact.
    • If the terminals are bent, loose, or corroded, repair them as necessary and recheck the system.
    • If the terminals look OK, go to Step 7 .
  1. With the connectors still disconnected, make these input tests at the following connectors.
    • If any test indicates a problem, find and correct the cause, then recheck the system.
    • If all the input tests prove OK, replace the power tailgate control unit.
    NOTE: After replacement, open and close the tailgate by auto operation and wait for 5 seconds. Repeat this procedure 5 times or more for proper operation.
