Audio Switch Panel Removal/Installation
Without Navigation System
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • Lay a workshop towel under the parts when working on them to protect the face panel from scratches or other damage.
  • Do not work in a dusty or dirty place.
  • Discharge static electricity from your body before and during the work.
  • Do not touch the circuit board with your bare hands.
  • Do not work with dirty hands.
  • Be careful not no fold the flat plate cable.
  • Do not touch the terminal connector of the flat plate cable with your bare hands. (If you have touched it, wipe it off thoroughly.)
  1. Remove these items:
    • Driver's center trim
    • Passenger's dashboard trim
    • Passenger's center trim
    • Center console panel
    • Climate control unit
    • Dashboard center pocket
    • Dashboard center vent
    • Audio unit
    • Hazard switch
  1. Remove the audio switch panel (A).
  1. Install the audio switch panel in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure all the connectors and the antenna lead are secure.
    • Enter the anti-theft codes for the audio system.
  1. Give the new anti-theft codes to the customer.
