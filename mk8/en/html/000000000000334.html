Hood Release Handle Replacement
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to scratch the body.
  • LHD model is shown; RHD model is symmetrical.
  1. Remove the driver's kick panel.
  1. Remove the bolts, then remove the hood release handle (A).
  1. Disconnect the hood opener cable (B) from the hood release handle. Take care not to kink the cable.
  1. Install the hood release handle in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the hood opener cable is connected properly.
    • Make sure the hood opens properly.
