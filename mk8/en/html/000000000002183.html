DTC Troubleshooting: P228E, P228F
DTC P228E:
 FRP Regulator Incorrect Learned Value
DTC P228F:
 FRP Regulator Incorrect Learned Value
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and review the general troubleshooting information.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Do the FRP REGULATOR ON/OFF in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
Is DTC P228E and/or P228F indicated?
YES -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the FRP regulator and the ECM.■
NO -
Go to Step 4 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Replace the fuel rail.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Do the FRP REGULATOR ON/OFF in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P228E and/or P228F indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the FRP regulator and the ECM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
