Rear Gutter Trim Replacement
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the body.
  • When removing components, use the trim tools or equivalent.
  1. Remove the rear gutter trim (A).
    -1.
    Pry up the notch (B), then detach the clip.
    -2.
    Pull the upper portion of the trim out.
    -3.
    Pull up the trim to release the hooks (C).
  1. Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the clip is damaged or stress-whitened, replace it with a new one.
    • Push the clip into place securely.
