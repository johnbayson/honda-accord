Headlight Leveling Switch Test/Replacement - Without HID
  1. Remove the driver's dashboard undercover, then remove the dashboard lower cover.
  1. Disconnect the 6P connector (A) from the headlight leveling switch (B), then push out the switch from the dashboard lower cover.
  1. Measure the resistance between terminals No. 2 and No. 4 and terminals No. 2 and No. 3 at positions 0, 1, 2, and 3 by moving the switch knob.
    Between terminals No. 2 and No. 4:
    About 4.7 kΩ

    Between terminals No. 2 and No. 3:

    Knob position
    0
    1
    2
    3
    Resistance [About (kΩ)]
    3.964
    3.299
    2.695
    2.49

  1. If the resistance is not as specified, replace the switch.
