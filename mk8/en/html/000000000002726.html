M/T Clutch Housing Mainshaft Bearing and Oil Seal Replacement
Special Tools Required

Oil seal driver, 65 mm
 07JAD-PL90100

Adjustable bearing puller, 20−40 mm
 07JAC-PH80100

Bearing remover shaft
 07JAC-PH80200

Bearing driver attachment, 42 x 47 mm
 07746-0010300

Driver handle, 15 x 135L
 07749-0010000

Slide hammer weight
 07741-0010201
  1. Remove the ball bearing (A) from the clutch housing (B) using the 20−40 mm adjustable bearing puller (C), slide hammer weight (D) and bearing remover shaft (E).
  1. Remove the oil seal (A) from the clutch housing. Be careful when removing the oil seal so the clutch housing is not damaged.
  1. Drive in the new oil seal from the transmission side using the 15 x 135L driver handle (A) and 42 x 47 mm bearing driver attachment (B).
  1. Drive in the new ball bearing (A) from the transmission side using the 65 mm oil seal driver (B).
