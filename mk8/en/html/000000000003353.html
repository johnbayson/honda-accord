Rear Door Glass Outer Weatherstrip Replacement
NOTE:
  • If you remove the door glass outer weatherstrip, replace it with a new one because its rear hook will be damaged during removal.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to scratch the door.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Door panel
    • Door panel bracket
    • Plastic cover, as needed
  1. Raise the glass fully.
  1. From the inside of the door, slide the clips (A) on the door glass outer weatherstrip (B) to release the hooks (C) from the flanges (D) on the door panel.
  1. Using a flat-tip screwdriver, push the front hook (A) from inside of the door, then pull up the front portion of the rear door glass outer weatherstrip (B).
  1. Pull up the weatherstrip to detach the rear hook (C), then remove the weatherstrip.
  1. Before installing the new weatherstrip, align the clips of the weatherstrip to the flanges where the clips should be engaged by sliding them.
  1. Push the clip and hook portions of the rear door glass outer weatherstrip into place securely.
  1. Reinstall all remaining removed parts.
    NOTE: When reinstalling the door panel, make sure the plastic cover is installed properly and sealed around its outside perimeter to seal out water.
