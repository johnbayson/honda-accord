Keyless/Power Door Locks/Security System Function Test
Ultrasonic sensor
  1. Open the driver's window.
  1. Close all doors.
  1. Lock the doors with the transmitter or the key cylinder.
  1. Wait 25 seconds for activate the security system.
    The hazard warning lights and the security indicator blink when the security system is armed.
  1. Put your hand into the cabin.
    • If the siren sounds and hazard warning lights blink, go to Step 6 .
    • If the system siren doesn't sound and the hazard warning lights don't blink, replace the ultrasonic sensor.
Internal battery of alarm control siren
  1. Open the hood.
  1. Disconnect the terminals from the battery.
    • If the siren sounds continuously, internal battery is OK.
    • If the siren turns off, replace the alarm control siren.
    NOTE: The alarm control siren is self-powered system with internal Litium battery back-up. Even if the voltage of vehicle's battery drops, the alarm control siren works properly.
