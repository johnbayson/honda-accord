Interior Trim Removal/Installation - Tailgate Area
For Some Models
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
  • When removing components, use the trim tools or equivalent.
  1. Remove the tailgate upper trim (A).
    -1.
    Detach the hooks (B) and tabs (C) from the left tailgate side trim (D) and the right tailgate side trim (E).
    -2.
    Pull the tailgate upper trim back by hand to detach the clips, then remove it.
  1. Remove the left tailgate side trim (A) and the right tailgate side trim (B).
    -1.
    Pull out the top of the side trim to detach the clips.
    -2.
    Detach the tabs (C) and hooks (D) from the tailgate lower trim panel (E).
  1. With power tailgate: Remove the inside tailgate switch.
  1. Remove the tailgate lower trim panel (A).
    -1.
    Remove the screws (B) from both pull pockets.
    -2.
    Detach the clips (C) beside the tailgate latch.
    -3.
    Pull down the trim panel to detach all of the clips (D).
    -4.
    Release both upper corner portions of the trim panel from the edge of the tailgate flange.
  1. Install the trim and panel in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • For some models: Make sure the inside tailgate switch connector is plugged in properly.
    • If the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, replace them with new ones.
    • Push the clips and the hooks into place securely.
Tailgate Hinge Trim
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the panel.
  • When removing components, use the trim tools or equivalent.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Tailgate weatherstrip
    • Tonneau cover and the cargo net, for some models
    • Under floor cover:
      • -
      With temporary repair kit (TRK)
      -
      With compact spare tyre/with normal spare tyre
    • Rear seat cushion
    • Rear seat belt lower anchor
    • Rear seat side bolster
    • Damper maintenance lid
    • Cargo net striker, for some models
    • Rear seat belt anchor wire
    • Cargo area side trim panel
    • Rear door opening seal, as needed
    • Roof end trim
    • Quarter pillar trim
    • Tailgate upper trim, for some models
    • Tailgate side trim, for some models
  1. Remove the tailgate hinge trim (A). The right tailgate hinge trim is shown; the left tailgate hinge trim is symmetrical, however, the rear window washer tube is routed on only right side.
    -1.
    Lower the trim to release three hooks (B) from convex portions (C) in the tailgate hinge arm (D).
    -2.
    Right side: Release the rear window washer tube (E) from the hooks (F) and the ribs (G).
  1. Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Right side: Align the tube joint (A) and the mark (B) on the rear window washer tube (C) with the hooks (D) as shown. Make sure the washer tube is routed in the trim properly without being pinched.
    • When installing the trim, fit the three inside hooks to concaved portions in the hinge arm, then gradually slide the hinge trim up to fit the hooks to the convex portions in the hinge arm.
    • Make sure the tailgate wire harness is routed in the trim properly.
