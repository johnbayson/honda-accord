Dashboard Center Vent Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and its related parts.
  • When removing components, use the trims tool or equivalent.
  • The left dashboard center vent is shown; the right dashboard center vent is symmetrical.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Center console panel
    • Driver's center trim
    • Passenger's dashboard trim
    • Passenger's center trim
    • Center holder
    • Climate control unit
    • Center pocket, without navigation system
    • Center panel, without audio system
    • Audio unit:
      • -
      Without navigation system
      -
      With navigation system
    • Audio/navigation switch panel, with navigation system
  1. Remove the screws, then remove the dashboard center vent (A) from the center panel (B) while releasing upper tabs (C) with the appropriate trim tool.
  1. Install the vent in the reverse order of removal.
