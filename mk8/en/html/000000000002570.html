DTC Troubleshooting: P179A (138)
DTC P179A:
 Transmission Control Module (TCM) Relay Stuck On
NOTE:
  • Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and any on-board snapshot with the HDS, and review General Troubleshooting Information.
  • This code is caused by an electrical circuit problem and cannot be caused by a mechanical problem in the transmission.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and wait for at least 5 seconds. Then turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P179A indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals between the TCM and the TCM relay 4P connector.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Remove the TCM relay.
  1. Measure the voltage between TCM relay 4P connector terminals No. 3 and No. 4 when the ignition switch is turned to ON (II), and when the ignition switch is turned to LOCK (0).
Is there about battery voltage when the ignition switch is turned to ON (II), and about 0 V when the ignition switch is turned to LOCK (0)?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Go to Step 9 .
  1. Test the TCM relay.
Is the TCM relay OK?
YES -
Repair short to power in the wire between the TCM relay 4P connector and TCM connector terminals No. 6, No. 8, and No. 55, then go to Step 11 .
NO -
Replace the TCM relay, then go to Step 11 .
  1. Disconnect the TCM connector.
  1. Check for continuity between TCM connector terminal No. 7 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to body ground in the wire between TCM connector terminal No. 7 and the TCM relay 4P connector, then go to Step 11 .
NO -
Go to Step 14 .
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and wait for at least 5 seconds. Then turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P179A indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals between the TCM and the TCM relay 4P connector, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs were indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Update the TCM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good TCM.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P179A indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals between the TCM and the TCM relay 4P connector. If the TCM was updated, substitute a known-good TCM, then recheck. If the TCM was substituted, go to Step 1 .
NO -
If the TCM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the TCM was substituted, replace the original TCM. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs were indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
