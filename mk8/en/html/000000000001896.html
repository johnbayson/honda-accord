Cam Chain Auto-tensioner Removal and Installation
Removal
  1. Remove the cylinder head cover.
  1. Remove the chain case cover.
  1. Hold the intake camshaft with an open-end wrench, then turn the crankshaft counterclockwise to compress the auto-tensioner.
  1. Compress the auto-tensioner fully, then insert the stopper (P/N 14511-RL0-003) or 2.5 mm (0.10 in.) diameter pin into the space between the plunger and housing. Turn the crankshaft clockwise to secure the pin.
  1. Remove the auto-tensioner.
Installation
  1. Install the auto-tensioner.
  1. Remove the stopper or pin from the auto-tensioner.
  1. Remove all of the old liquid gasket from the chain case cover mating surfaces, bolts, and bolt holes.
  1. Clean and dry the chain case cover mating surfaces.
  1. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M or 08C70-K0334M) to the chain case mating surface of the chain case cover.
    NOTE:
    • Apply liquid gasket about 2.5 mm (0.098 in.) diameter bead along the broken line (A).
    • Do not install components if 5 minutes or more have passed after applying liquid gasket. Instead, remove the old residue and reapply the liquid gasket.
  1. Install the chain case cover.
    NOTE:
    • Wait at least 30 minutes before filling the engine with oil.
    • Do not run the engine for at least 3 hours after installing the chain case cover.
  1. Install the cylinder head cover.
