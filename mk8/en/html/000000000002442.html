A/T Housing Secondary Shaft ATF Feed Pipe Cap, Feed Pipe Replacement
NOTE: The 1st-hold clutch ATF feed pipe/ATF feed cap is not available separately.
  1. Remove the ATF feed pipe cap from the transmission housing.
  1. Remove the snap ring (A) from the ATF feed pipe cap (B), then remove the feed pipe guide (C), the O-rings (D) (E), and the 1st clutch ATF feed pipe (F).
  1. Replace the 1st clutch ATF feed pipe or 1st-hold clutch ATF feed pipe/ATF feed pipe cap assembly.
  1. Install a new O-ring (D) over the 1st clutch ATF feed pipe, then install the feed pipe over the 1st-hold clutch ATF feed pipe (G) while aligning the feed pipe tabs (H) with the guide in the cap.
  1. Install a new O-ring (E) in the cap and the feed pipe guide, then secure the guide with the snap ring.
  1. Install the ATF feed pipe cap in the transmission housing.
