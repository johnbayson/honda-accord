DTC Troubleshooting: U0047, U1263
DTC U0047:
 P/T-CAN Malfunction (BUS-OFF (TCM))
DTC U1263:
 P/T-CAN Malfunction (ECM-TCM)
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and review the general troubleshooting information.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0047 and/or U1263 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the ECM and the TCM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II), and check the A/T gear position indicator.
Is the indicator does not flash?
YES -
Check the A/T system. ■
NO -
Go to Step 5 .
  1. Check for the A/T system DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0047 and/or U1262 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Go to Step 22 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and wait 2 minutes.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 2 (58P).
  1. Measure the resistance between ECM connector No. 2 terminals No. 50 and No. 51.
Is there about 108−132Ω?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
  • If the resistance is lower than 108Ω, go to Step 12 .
  • If the resistance is higher than 132Ω, go to Step 14 .
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and ECM connector No. 2 terminals No. 50 and No. 51 individually.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to ground in the wire between the TCM and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 50 (P-CANH line), and/or the TCM and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 51 (P-CANL line), then go to Step 17 .
NO -
Go to Step 10 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between body ground and ECM connector No. 2 terminals No. 50 and No. 51 individually.
Is there 5.0 V or more?
YES -
Repair short to power in the wire between the TCM and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 50 (P-CANH line), and/or the TCM and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 51 (P-CANL line), then go to Step 17 .
NO -
Go to Step 22 .
  1. Disconnect the TCM 60P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector No. 2 terminals No. 50 and No. 51.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to power in the wire between the TCM and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 50 (P-CANH line), and/or the TCM and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 51 (P-CANL line), then go to Step 17 .
NO -
Substitute a known-good TCM, then recheck. If DTC U0047 and/or U1263 is not indicated after substitution, replace the original TCM, then go to Step 17 .
  1. Disconnect the TCM 60P connector.
  1. Connect TCM 60P connector terminals No. 56 and No. 57 to body ground with jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and ECM connector No. 2 terminals No. 50 and No. 51 individually.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Substitute a known-good TCM, then recheck. If DTC U0047 and/or U1263 is not indicated after substitution, replace the original TCM, then go to Step 17 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the TCM and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 50 (P-CANH line), and/or the TCM and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 51 (P-CANL line), then go to Step 17 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0047 and/or U1263 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the ECM and the TCM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Update the ECM does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0047 and/or U1263 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the ECM and the TCM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
