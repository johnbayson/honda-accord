A/T Assembly Disassembly
Special Tools Required

Mainshaft holder
 07PAB-0010001

Snap ring pliers
 07LGC-0010100
  1. Remove the ATF dipstick (A) and the dipstick guide tube (B).
  1. Remove A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valves A and B (C) (six bolts), the ATF feed pipes (D) (three), the O-rings (three), and the gasket.
  1. Remove the solenoid valve cover (E) (seven bolts), the dowel pins (two), and the gasket.
  1. Disconnect the solenoid valve connectors, then remove the solenoid wire harness connector (F).
  1. Remove A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve C (G) (four bolts), the ATF feed pipes (H) (three), the O-rings (two), and the gasket.
  1. Pry the lock tab of the lock washer (I) on the selector control lever (J), and remove the nut, the lock washer, the spring washer, and the selector control lever.
  1. Pry the lock tabs of the lock washer (K) on the transmission range switch (L), hold the selector control shaft (M) with a 6.0 mm wrench, and loosen the locknut (N).
  1. Remove the locknut and the lock washer, then remove the transmission range switch (two bolts).
  1. Remove the input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor (O), the output shaft (countershaft) speed sensor (P), and the sensor washer (Q).
  1. Remove the ATF temperature sensor connector and the harness clamp from the clamp bracket on the ATF cooler line, then remove the ATF temperature sensor (R).
  1. Remove the 6.0 mm bolts (S), the line bolts (T), the ATF cooler lines (U), and the sealing washers (V).
  1. Remove the end cover (W) (three bolts), the dowel pins (two), the O-ring, the snap ring cap (X) (two bolts), the sealing plug (Y), and the washer (Z).
  1. Remove the 3rd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch (AA) and the sealing washer.
  1. Remove the 4th clutch transmission fluid pressure switch (BB) and the sealing washer.
  1. Remove the 2nd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch (CC) and the sealing washer.
  1. Remove the ATF warmer bracket (DD).
  1. Remove the ATF cooler pipe mounting bracket (EE) and the harness brackets (FF).
