Radiator and A/C Condenser Fan, Motor, Shroud Removal and Installation
Removal
  1. Disconnect the fan motor connectors (A), then remove the harness clamp (B).
  1. With A/C: Remove the condenser fan shroud assembly (C).
  1. Remove the radiator fan shroud assembly (D) from right side of the engine compartment.
  1. Disassemble the fan shrouds.
Installation
  1. Assemble the fan shrouds.
  1. Install the radiator fan shroud assembly (A).
  1. With A/C: Install the condenser fan shroud assembly (B).
  1. Connect the fan motor connectors (C), then install the harness clamp (D).
