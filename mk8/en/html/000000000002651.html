E-pretensioner Operation Check
  1. Shift to the P position and release the parking brake.
  1. Connect the HDS to the DLC.
  1. Sit in the driver's and front passenger's seat, and fasten the seat belts.
  1. Make sure the SRS indicator comes on for about 6 seconds after turning the ignition switch to ON (II), then goes off. If the SRS indicator does not come on, check for DTCs with the HDS.
    NOTE: If the DTC is indicated, do the indicated DTCs troubleshooting, then go to go to Step 5 .
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Make sure the HDS communicates with the vehicle and the SRS unit. If it does not, troubleshoot the DLC circuit:
    • R20A3 engine
    • K24Z3 engine
  1. Select the E-PRETENSIONER with the HDS, and do the E-PRETENSIONER OPERATION CHECK.
    Make sure the E-pretensioners retract both driver's and front passenger's seat belts several times.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF and make sure the SRS indicator goes off.
