Wheel Bearing End Play Inspection
  1. Raise the vehicle, and support it with safety stands in the proper locations.
  1. Remove the wheels.
  1. Install a suitable flat washers (A) and the wheel nuts. Tighten the nuts to the specified torque to hold the brake disc or the brake disc/drum securely against the hub.
  1. Attach the dial gauge. Place the dial gauge against the hub flange.
  1. Measure the bearing end play while moving the brake disc or the brake disc/drum inward and outward.

    Wheel bearing end play:

    Front/Rear:
    0−0.05 mm (0−0.002 in.)

  1. If the bearing end play measurement is more than the standard, replace the wheel bearing or the hub bearing unit.
