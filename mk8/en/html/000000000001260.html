Starter Removal and Installation
Removal
  1. Do the battery terminal disconnection procedure.
  1. Remove the harness holder mounting bolt (A), then remove the intake manifold bracket (B).
  1. Remove the harness clamps.
  1. Remove the two bolts securing the starter, then remove the starter from the engine.
  1. Disconnect the starter cable (A) from the B terminal and disconnect the connector (B) from the S terminal, then remove the starter.
Installation
  1. Connect the starter cable (A) and connector (B). Make sure the starter cable crimped side of the ring terminal faces away from the starter when you connect it.
  1. Install the starter, then loosely install the upper mounting bolt (A) and lower mounting bolt (B).
  1. Tighten the upper mounting bolt, then tighten the lower mounting bolt.
  1. Install the harness clamps.
  1. Install the intake manifold bracket (A), then install the harness holder mounting bolt (B).
  1. Do the battery terminal reconnection procedure.
  1. Start the engine to make sure the starter works properly.
