Rear Seat Side Bolster Cover Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  1. Remove the seat side bolster.
  1. Release all the hook strips (A), and fold back the seat side bolster cover (B), and release all the staples (C) fastening the cover.
    Staple:
    Height 5 mm (0.2 in.)
    Width 10 mm (0.39 in.)

  1. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • To prevent wrinkles when installing a side bolster cover, make sure the material is stretched evenly over the pad before securing the hook strips and the staples.
    • Fasten the cover with new staples securely.
