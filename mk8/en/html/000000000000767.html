DTC Troubleshooting: B1277
DTC B1277:
 Combination Light Switch AUTO Position Circuit Malfunction
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0) and then back to ON (II).
  1. Turn the combination light switch POSITION (SMALL), AUTO, and ON (low beam) positions for 6 seconds in each position, and then OFF position.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.

YES -
Go to Step 5 .
