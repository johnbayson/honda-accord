Emblem/Sticker Replacement
NOTE: When removing the emblems/sticker, take care not to scratch the body.
  1. Without millimeter wave radar unit: To remove the front ‘‘H'' emblem, remove these items:
    • Front grille cover
    • Front bumper
  1. With millimeter wave radar unit: To remove the front ‘‘H'' emblem, remove these items:
    • Front grille cover
    • Front bumper
    • Front grille
    • Front grille molding A
  1. Clean the body surface with a shop towel dampened in isopropyl alcohol. After cleaning, keep oil, grease, and water from getting on the surface.
  1. Apply the emblems/sticker where shown. When installing the i-DTEC sticker on the outside surface of the rear window glass, align the bottom edge of the application tape with the edge of the rear window glass, and center the sticker as shown, then press the sticker into place, and remove the application tape.
