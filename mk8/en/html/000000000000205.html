Front Seat Belt Replacement
Front Seat Belt
SRS components are located in this area. Review the SRS component locations and the precautions and procedures before doing repairs or service.
NOTE:
  • Check the front seat belts for damage, and replace them if necessary.
  • LHD model is shown; RHD model is symmetrical.
  1. Driver's side: Prepare a sheet of cardboard which is 2−2.5 mm (0.08−0.1 in.) thick.
    NOTE: If there is no suitable cardboard, put some seats of thin paper together until they will be required thick.
  1. Driver's side: Make a jig (A) from the prepared cardboard (B).
  1. Do the battery terminal disconnection procedure, then wait at least 3 minutes before beginning work.
  1. Slide the front seat forward fully.
Dimensions of the jig
  1. Driver's side: Slowly insert the jig (A) into holes (B) in the back of the lap connector cover (C).
    NOTE: Make sure the jig is perpendicular to the lap connector cover.
  1. Driver's side: With the jig (A) still inserted in the lap connector cover (B), carefully insert the tip of a small screwdriver (C) into the hole in the lap connector cover, and unlock the lap connector by pushing in on the screwdriver.
  1. Driver's side: Unbuckle the front seat belt lap connector (A).
    -1.
    While pulling the front seat belt (B) upward, remove the screwdriver (C). As soon as you remove the screwdriver from the lap connector (D), the anchor connector will come off the lap connector.
    -2.
    Remove the jig (E).
  1. Passenger's side: Pull the lower anchor cover (A) back, and remove the lower anchor bolt (B). Do not disassemble the lower anchor (C).
  1. Remove these items:
    • Front door sill trim
    • Rear door sill trim
    • Front door opening seal, as needed
    • Rear door opening seal, as needed
    • Center pillar lower trim
    • Center pillar upper trim
  1. Driver's side: Remove the seat belt outer lap tensioner (A).
    -1.
    Pull the carpet (B) back as needed.
    -2.
    Disconnect the seat belt outer lap tensioner connector (C).
    -3.
    Remove the lower anchor bolt (D).
  1. Remove the upper anchor bolt (A).
  1. Disconnect the seat belt tensioner connector (A) and the seat belt motor connector (B) (for some models). Remove the upper retractor mounting bolt (C) and the lower retractor bolt (D), then remove the front seat belt (E) and the retractor (F).
  1. Remove the shoulder anchor adjuster (A).
  1. Install the seat belt and the retractor in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Apply medium strength liquid thread lock to the anchor bolts before reinstallation.
    • Tighten the bolts by hand first, then tighten to the specified torque.
    • Check that the retractor locking mechanism functions.
    • Assemble the washer, the collars, and the bushing on the upper anchor bolt, the lower anchor bolt, and the retractor bolt as shown.
    • Before installing the anchor bolts, make sure there are no twists or kinks in the seat belt.
    • Make sure the seat belt tensioner connector and the seat belt motor connector (for some models) are plugged in properly.
    • Do the battery terminal reconnection procedure.
    • Check for any DTCs that may have been set during repairs, and clear them.
