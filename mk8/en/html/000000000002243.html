  1. Check the brake pedal position switch position. ■

YES -

Do the brake pedal position switch adjustment. ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Check the clutch pedal position switch A position. ■
Is a position proper?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Do the clutch pedal and clutch pedal position switch A adjustment. ■
  1. Connect the HDS to the DLC.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Check CLUTCH PEDAL POSITION SWITCH A in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is OFF indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Go to Step 25 .
  1. Press the clutch pedal.
  1. Check CLUTCH PEDAL POSITION SWITCH A in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is ON indicated?
YES -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the gauge control module, clutch pedal position switch A, and the ECM.■
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect the clutch pedal position switch A 2P connector.
  1. Connect clutch pedal position switch A 2P connector terminals No. 1 and No. 2 with a jumper wire.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Check CLUTCH PEDAL POSITION SWITCH A in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is OFF indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 17 .
NO -
Replace clutch pedal position switch A. ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Remove the jumper wire from the clutch pedal position switch A 2P connector.
  1. Connect clutch pedal position switch A 2P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Check CLUTCH PEDAL POSITION SWITCH A in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is ON indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 22 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the clutch pedal position switch A and body ground (LHD model: G302, RHD model: G251). ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and wait 2 minutes.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 2 (58P).
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 26 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM, then recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away with a known-good ECM, replace the original ECM. ■
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the clutch pedal position switch A and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 26.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect the clutch pedal position switch A 2P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Check CLUTCH PEDAL POSITION SWITCH A in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is OFF indicated?
YES -
Replace the clutch pedal position switch A. ■
NO -
Go to Step 29 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and wait 2 minutes.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 2 (58P).
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 26 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between clutch pedal position switch A and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 26.■
NO -
Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM, then recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away with a known-good ECM, replace the original ECM. ■
