M/T Reverse Lockout Solenoid Disassembly/Reassembly
NOTE: Use fender covers to avoid damaging painted surfaces.
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing.
  1. Support the under engine/transmission with a floor jack.
  1. Remove the engine harness clamps (A) from the side of the engine room.
  1. Remove the ground cable (A) from the transmission mount.
  1. Remove the transmission mount bolt and nuts from transmission housing.
    NOTE: Do not remove the TORX bolt (B) from the upper transmission mount. If the TORX bolt is removed, the upper transmission mount must be replace as an assembly.
  1. Remove the transmission mount bolts from the body frame, then remove the transmission mount (A).
    NOTE: Slightly lower the engine/transmission supported by the jack to remove the transmission mount if it necessary.
  1. Disconnect the reverse lockout solenoid 2P connector (A).
  1. Remove the reverse lockout solenoid (B), the select lock pin (C), and the select lock pin spring (D).
  1. Remove the roller (A), and the select lock cam (B).
  1. Install the components of the reverse lockout solenoid in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Clean any dirt and oil from the reverse lockout solenoid sealing surface.
  1. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M, 08C70-K0334M, or 08C70-X0331S) evenly to the transmission housing surface of the reverse lockout solenoid.
    NOTE: Do not install components if too much time has passed after applying the liquid gasket. Instead, remove the old residue and reapply the liquid gasket.
  1. Install the select lock pin sprig (A), the select lock pin (B), and the reverse lockout solenoid (C).
  1. Connect the reverse lockout solenoid 2P connector (D).
  1. Position the transmission mount (A), and loosely install the new transmission mount bolts (B) to the body frame.
  1. Loosely install the new transmission mount bolt (A) and nuts (B) to the transmission housing.
  1. Install the ground cable (C) to the transmission mount.
  1. Remove the jack under the engine/transmission.
  1. Tighten the transmission mount bolts and nuts to the specified torque value.
  1. Install the engine harness clamps (A) to the side of the engine room.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing.
