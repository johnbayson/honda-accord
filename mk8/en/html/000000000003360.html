Rear Undercover Replacement
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to scratch the rear bumper and the body.
  1. Remove the rear undercover (A).
    -1.
    Remove the bolts (B) and the clip (C) that secure the undercover to the body.
    -2.
    R20A3 engine model: Remove the clips (D) securing the undercover and the rear inner fender (E).
    -3.
    From under the rear bumper (F), remove the clips (G) securing the undercover and the bumper.
    -4.
    Lower the front portion of the undercover, and pull the undercover out from the bumper.
    R20A3 engine model: Pull the undercover out from the rear bumper and the left rear inner fender cover.
    NOTE: To remove the clips (D, G), pry the inner clip up at the edge near the line (H) on its head.
  1. Install the undercover in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, replace them with new ones.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
