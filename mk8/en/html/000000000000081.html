Steering Rack Guide Removal/Installation
NOTE: During removal/installation, do not allow dust, dirt, or other foreign materials to enter the steering gearbox.
  1. Loosen the locknut (A), then remove the rack guide screw (B), the spring (C) and rack guide (D).
  1. Apply steering grease to the sliding surface and the circumference of the rack guide, and install it onto the gearbox housing. Wipe the grease off the threaded section of the gearbox housing.
  1. Install the spring onto the gearbox housing.
  1. Remove the old sealant from the rack guide screw and apply new sealant (Three Bond 1215 or Loctite 5699) to the middle of the threads. Loosely install the rack guide screw on the steering gearbox.
    NOTE: If more than 5 minutes has passed after applying the sealant, remove the old sealant and residue, and reapply new sealant.
  1. Loosely install the locknut.
  1. Adjust the rack guide screw. After adjusting, check that the rack moves smoothly by sliding the rack right and left.
