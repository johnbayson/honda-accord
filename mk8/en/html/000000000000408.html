Headlight Replacement
  1. Remove the parts shown:
    • Front fender trim
    • Front bumper
    • Grille cover
  1. Remove the four bolts, and pull the headlight (A) out slightly.
  1. Remove the harness clip from the headlight housing, and disconnect the connectors from the bulb sockets.
  1. Remove the headlight with the corner upper beam (B).
    NOTE: Be careful not to scratch the headlight lens and the fender.
  1. Remove the bolt (C), and separate the corner upper beam and the headlight.
  1. Install the headlight in the reverse order of removal.
  1. After replacement, adjust the headlight.
