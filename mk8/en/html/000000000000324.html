Rear Inner Fender Replacement
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to scratch the rear bumper and the body.
  1. Remove the screws (A) securing the rear inner fender (B) and the rear bumper (C), and remove the screw (D) securing the rear inner fender and the body.
    Left side on R20A3 engine model: Remove the clips (E) securing the rear inner fender and the side rear undercover.
    NOTE: To remove the clips, pry the inner clip up at the edge near the line (F) on its head.
  1. Pull on the rear bumper (A) at the lower portion of the wheel arch area. Pull out the grommet (B) while pinching the tabs (C) on the end of the grommet, then remove it from the body, and remove the rear inner fender (D).
  1. Install the inner fender in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the grommet and the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, replace them with new ones.
    • Push the grommet and the clips into place securely.
