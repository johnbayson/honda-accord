Lane Keeping Assist System (LKAS) DTC Troubleshooting: 21
LKAS Control Unit DTC 21:
 VSA Modulator-control Unit Malfunction
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), then turn it to ON (II).
  1. Watch the VSA indicator.
Does the VSA indicator come on?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
