Rear Seat Armrest Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  1. Fold the left rear seat-back forward.
  1. Unzip the left rear seat-back cover (A), then pull back the seat-back cover and the pad (B) as needed. Remove the bolt from the armrest pivot.
  1. Raise the left rear seat-back up.
  1. Remove the clip (A) from the left portion of the armrest pivot (B).
  1. Slide the armrest (A) toward the driver's side of the vehicle, and remove the pivot shaft (B) from the collar (C) on the right side of the vehicle by pulling back on the armrest.
  1. Remove the pivot shaft (A) from the collar (B) on the left, then remove the armrest (C).
  1. Remove the collars (A) from the seat-back (B).
  1. Install the armrest in the reverse order of removal. Apply medium strength liquid thread lock to the pivot bolt before reinstallation.
