Turbocharger and Exhaust Manifold Removal and Installation

Exploded View
Removal
  1. Remove the diesel particulate filter (DPF).
  1. Remove the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) pipe A.
  1. Remove the oil bypass pipe (A) and turbocharger bracket (B).
  1. Disconnect the water bypass hoses (A), breather hose (B), and vacuum hose (C), then remove the turbocharger (D).
  1. Remove the exhaust manifold.
Installation
  1. Install the exhaust manifold (A) with a new gasket (B) and new self-locking nuts (C), and tighten the nuts in a crisscross pattern in three steps, beginning with the inner nut.
  1. Pour new engine oil into the turbocharger oil line.
  1. Install the turbocharger (A) with new gasket (B), then connect the water bypass hoses (C), breather hose (D), and vacuum hose (E).
  1. Check the bolt (A) and oil bypass pipe (B) for clogs. If they are clogged clean or replace the bolt and/or pipe.
  1. Install the oil bypass pipe with new gaskets (C) and new washers (D).
  1. Install the turbocharger bracket (E).
  1. Install the EGR pipe A.
  1. Install the DPF.
