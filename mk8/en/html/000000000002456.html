A/T Clutch Pressure Control Solenoid Valve C Replacement
  1. Do the battery removal procedure.
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing and the intake air duct.
  1. Disconnect the A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve C connector (A).
  1. Remove the harness clamp (B) from the harness bracket (D).
  1. Remove A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve C, the ATF joint pipes (E), the O-rings (F), the ATF pipe (G), and the gasket (H).
  1. Clean the mounting surfaces and the fluid passages of the solenoid valve body and the transmission housing.
  1. Install the 8 x 53 mm ATF joint pipe (A) with the filter end into its mounting hole (B).
  1. Check the height (A) of the 8 x 53 mm ATF joint pipe (B) between the top (C) of the pipe and the solenoid valve body mounting surface (D). The height is about 7.0 mm (0.3 in.). If the height is over 7.0 mm (0.3 in.), push the pipe until it bottoms in the accumulator body.
  1. Install a new gasket (A) on the transmission housing, and install the 8 x 34.5 mm ATF joint pipe (B) with the filter end into the transmission housing and the 8 x 25.2 mm ATF pipe (D).
  1. Install new O-rings (E) over the ATF joint pipes.
  1. Install the harness clamp (F) to the harness bracket (G).
  1. Install A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve C.
  1. Check the connector for rust, dirt, or oil, then connect the connector securely.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing and the intake air duct.
  1. Do the battery installation procedure.
