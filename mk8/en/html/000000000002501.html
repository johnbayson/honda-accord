A/T Intermediary Shaft Installation Height Inspection/Adjustment
  1. Install the intermediary shaft (A) in the torque converter housing, and install the selected 26.5 mm washer (B) on the intermediary shaft.
    Intermediary Shaft Installation Height
    Standard:
    137.285−137.385 mm (5.40491−5.40885 in.)

  1. Install a new gasket (C) on the torque converter housing.
  1. Measure the installed height (D) of the intermediary shaft between the surface (E) of the gasket and the surface of the 26.5 mm washer (F).
  1. If the measurement is out of standard, remove the 26.5 mm washer and measure its thickness.
  1. Select and install a new 26.5 mm washer, then recheck the installation height.
