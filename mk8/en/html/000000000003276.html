DTC Troubleshooting: B1384
DTC B1384:
 Tailgate Half Switch Input Circuit Malfunction
  1. Open the tailgate with the tailgate outer handle.
  1. Close the tailgate manually to the half latch position.
  1. Open the tailgate with the tailgate outer handle.
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC B1384 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for loose or poor connections.■
  1. Select POWER TAILGATE from the BODY ELECTRICAL system select menu, and enter DATA LIST.
  1. Check the ON/OFF information of the TAILGATE LOCK SWITCH RATCHET with the DATA LIST.
Is the information indicator OFF?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Go to Step 10 .
  1. Check the ON/OFF information of the TAILGATE LOCK SWITCH HALF LATCH with the DATA LIST.
Is the information indicator ON?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Go to Step 17 .
  1. Turn the tailgate latch to half latch position using a screwdriver and check the ON/OFF information of the TAILGATE LOCK SWITCH HALF LATCH with the DATA LIST, then turn the tailgate latch to full latch position using a screwdriver and check the ON/OFF information of the TAILGATE LOCK SWITCH HALF LATCH with the DATA LIST.
Are both information indicators OFF?
YES -
Do the steps Step 10 to 16, Step 17 to 23, and Step 24 to 29.
NO -
Go to Step 17 .

  1. Open the tailgate with the tailgate outer handle.
  1. Disconnect power tailgate control unit connector C (14P).
  1. Check for continuity between power tailgate control unit connector C (14P) No. 10 and No. 11 terminals.
Is there continuity?
YES -

NO -
