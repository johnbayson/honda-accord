Keyless/Power Door Locks/Security System Symptom Troubleshooting - Doors will not unlock (or lock) with the transmitter, but will unlock (or lock) with the door switch
Doors will not unlock (or lock) with the transmitter, but will unlock (lock) with the door switch
NOTE: Before troubleshooting, check the B-CAN DTCs. If any DTC is indicated, troubleshoot the indicated DTC first.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Remove the ignition key from the ignition switch.
  1. Close and lock the doors.
  1. Try to lock/unlock the doors with the keyless transmitter.
Do the door lock actuators work normally?
YES -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
NO -
Go to Step 5 .
  1. Open the driver's door.
Does the key-in reminder chime sound?
YES -
Faulty ignition key switch , or short to ground on the ignition switch wire. Repair as necessary.■
NO -
Go to Step 6 .
  1. Do the transmitter test.
Is the transmitter OK?
YES -
Substitute a known-good driver's under-dash fuse/relay box and recheck. If there is still a problem, substitute a known-good immobilizer-keyless control unit and recheck.■
NO -
Replace the transmitter.■
