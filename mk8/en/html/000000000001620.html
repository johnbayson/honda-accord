Air Cleaner Element Inspection/Replacement
  1. Open the air cleaner housing cover (A).
  1. Remove the air cleaner element (B) from the air cleaner housing (C).
  1. Check the air cleaner element for damage or clogging. If it is damage or clogged, replace it.
    NOTE: Do not use compressed air to clean the air cleaner element.
  1. Clean and remove any debris from inside the air cleaner housing.
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
    • If you did not replace the air cleaner element, this procedure is complete.
    • If you replace the air cleaner element, reset the ECM/PCM with the HDS, and do the ECM/PCM idle learn procedure.
  1. Connect the HDS to the data link connector (DLC) located under the driver's side of the dashboard.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Make sure the HDS communicates with the vehicle and the ECM/PCM. If it doesn't communicate, troubleshoot the DLC circuit.
  1. Select BODY ELECTRICAL with the HDS.
  1. Select ADJUSTMENT in GAUGE MENU with the HDS.
  1. Select SERVICE REMINDER in the EU MAINTENANCE MINDER with the HDS.
  1. Select SERVICE REMINDER ITEM 8 with the HDS.
