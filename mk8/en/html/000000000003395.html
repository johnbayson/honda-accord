Interior Trim Removal/Installation - Cargo Area
With Temporary Repair Kit (TRK)
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
  • When removing components, use the trim tools or equivalent.
  1. For some models: Remove the tonneau cover and the cargo net.
  1. Fold both rear seat-backs forward.
  1. Release the Velcro fasteners (A) from both rear seat-backs (B). Pull back the Velcro fasteners, then attach them on the rear floor mat (C) as shown. Pull back the front portion of the rear floor lid (D), and detach the clips, then turn the front section upside down to release the hooks (E) from both front lid supports (F).
  1. Remove the rear floor lid (A).
  1. Remove the tailgate weatherstrip near the rear trim panel, as needed.
  1. Detach the clips (A, B), and release the hooks (C) and the tabs (D) from the cargo area side trim panels (E) by pulling the rear trim panel (F) up, then remove it.
  1. Remove the clips, fold the rear wall of the rear under floor cover (A) forward, then remove cover.
  1. Remove the tool box spacer (A).
  1. Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, replace them with new ones.
    • Push the clips and the hooks into place securely.
With Compact Spare Tyre/With Normal Spare Tyre
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
  • When removing components, use the trim tools or equivalent.
  1. Remove the tonneau cover, and if equipped, remove the cargo net.
  1. Fold both rear seat-backs forward.
  1. Release the fasteners (A) from both rear seat-backs (B). Pull back the Velcro fastners, then attach them on the rear floor mat (C) as shown. Pull back the front portion of the rear floor lid (D) to detach the clips, then turn the front section upside down to release the hooks (E) from both front lid supports (F).
  1. Remove the rear floor lid (A).
  1. Remove the tool box spacer (A).
  1. Remove the tailgate weatherstrip near the rear trim panel, as needed.
With compact spare tyre
With normal spare tyre
  1. Detach the clips (A, B), and release the hooks (C) and the tabs (D) from the cargo area side trim panels (E) by pulling the rear trim panel (F) up, then remove it.
  1. Remove the clips, then remove the rear under floor cover (A).
  1. Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, replace them with new ones.
    • Push the clips and the hooks into place securely.
Rear Floor Lid Handle
  1. Remove the rear floor lid.
  1. Remove the screws, then remove the handle case (A) and the handle (B) from the rear floor lid.
  1. Install the handle in the reverse order of removal.
