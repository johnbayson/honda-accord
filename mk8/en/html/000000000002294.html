M/T Countershaft Inspection
  1. Inspect the oil passage, gear, and bearing contact areas for wear and damage, then measure the countershaft at points A, B, C, and D. If any part of the countershaft is less than the service limit, replace it.
    Standard:
    A Ball Bearing Contact Area (Transmission Housing Side):
    30.002−30.015 mm (1.1812−1.1817 in.)
    B 2nd Gear Distance Collar Contact Area:
    42.975−42.991 mm (1.6919−1.6926 in.)
    C Countershaft Reverse Gear Contact Area:
    45.934−45.950 mm (1.8084−1.8091 in.)
    D Needle Bearing Contact Area (Clutch Housing Side):
    43.974−43.990 mm (1.7313−1.7319 in.)
    Service Limit:
    A:
    29.95 mm (1.179 in.)
    B:
    42.92 mm (1.690 in.)
    C:
    45.88 mm (1.806 in.)
    D:
    43.92 mm (1.729 in.)


  1. Inspect the runout by supporting both ends of the countershaft. Then rotate the countershaft two complete turns while measuring with a dial gauge. If the runout is more than the service limit, replace the countershaft.
    Standard:
    0.02 mm (0.001 in.) max.
    Service Limit:
    0.05 mm (0.002 in.)
