Sunroof Sunshade Replacement
  1. Remove these items:
    • Sunroof glass
    • Drain channel
  1. Slide the sunshade (A) until you can see both sunshade slider spacers (B).
  1. Remove the screws, then remove both spacers.
  1. While lifting the front portion of the sunshade (A), move the sunshade forward until you can see both sunshade rear hooks (B). Do not damage the sunshade and the hooks.
  1. Remove the screws, then remove both hooks.
  1. Remove the sunshade (A).
  1. Remove both front sunshade base sliders (A) and both rear sunshade base sliders (B).
  1. Install the sunshade in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Check the glass position adjustment.
    • Reset the sunroof control unit.
  1. Check for water leaks. Let the water run freely from a hose without a nozzle. Do not use a high-pressure spray.
