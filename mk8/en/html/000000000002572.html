DTC Troubleshooting: U0047 (121), U1262 (121)
DTC U0047:
 P/T-CAN Malfunction (BUS-OFF (Transmission Control Module (TCM)))
DTC U1262:
 P/T-CAN Malfunction (Transmission Control Module (TCM) - Engine Control Module (ECM))
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and any on-board snapshot with the HDS, and review General Troubleshooting Information.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. With the ignition switch in ON (II), and wait for at least 1 second.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0047 or U1262 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs were indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and wait for at least 2 minutes.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 2 (58P).
  1. Measure the resistance between ECM connector No. 2 terminals No. 50 and No. 51.
Is there about 85−130 Ω?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
If the resistance is less than 1 Ω, go to Step 15 . If the indicated resistance is infinite, go to Step 17 .
  1. Disconnect the TCM connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between TCM connector terminal No. 56 and body ground.
Is there about 5.0 V or about battery voltage?
YES -
Repair short to power in the wire between TCM connector terminal No. 56 and the ECM, then go to Step 20 .
NO -
Go to Step 11 .
  1. Measure the voltage between TCM connector terminal No. 57 and body ground.
Is there about 5.0 V or about battery voltage?
YES -
Repair short to power in the wire between TCM connector terminal No. 57 and the ECM, then go to Step 20 .
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Connect ECM connector No. 2 (58P).
  1. Measure the resistance between TCM connector terminals No. 56 and No. 57.
Is there about 85−130 Ω?
YES -
Go to Step 26 .
NO -
Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM, then go to Step 20 .
  1. Disconnect the TCM connector.
  1. Check for continuity between TCM connector terminals No. 56 and No. 57.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between TCM connector terminals No. 56 and No. 57, then go to Step 20 .
NO -
Go to Step 26 .
  1. Disconnect the TCM connector.
  1. Check for continuity between TCM connector terminal No. 56 and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 50.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 19 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between TCM connector terminal No. 56 and the ECM, then go to Step 20 .
  1. Check for continuity between TCM connector terminal No. 57 and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 51.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 26 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between TCM connector terminal No. 57 and the ECM, then go to Step 20 .
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. With the ignition switch in ON (II), and wait for at least 1 second.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0047 or U1262 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals between the TCM and the ECM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Go to Step 25 .
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for U0047 or U1262 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the HDS indicate PASSED?
YES -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs were indicated in Step 24 , go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
NO -
If the HDS indicates FAILED, check for poor connections or loose terminals between the TCM and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If the HDS indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 23 .
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Update the TCM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good TCM.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II), and wait for at least 1 second.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0047 or U1262 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals between the TCM and the ECM. If the TCM was updated, substitute a known-good TCM, then go to Step 28 . If the TCM was substituted, go to Step 1 .
NO -
Go to Step 30 .
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for U0047 or U1262 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the HDS indicate PASSED?
YES -
If the TCM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the TCM was substituted, replace the original TCM. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs were indicated in Step 28 , go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
NO -
If the HDS indicates FAILED, check for poor connections or loose terminals between the TCM and the ECM. If the TCM was updated, substitute a known-good TCM, then go to Step 27 . If the TCM was substituted, go to Step 1 . If the HDS indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 27 .
