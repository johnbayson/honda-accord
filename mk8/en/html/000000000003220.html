Today's Destinations button is dim and not selectable in the Enter destination by screen (grayed-out)
The client has not entered a group of locations for Today's Destinations. This is normal. The button is only selectable if the customer is using this function.
See Owner's Manual
Previous Destinations button is dim and not selectable in the Enter destination by screen (grayed-out)
The vehicle may be new, or the customer deleted the destination. Without a stored previous destination, the system can't route to a previous destination. Enter a destination, and allow the system to route to it. After the trip, the Previous Destinations button will be selectable.
