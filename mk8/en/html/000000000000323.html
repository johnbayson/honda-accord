Front Inner Fender Replacement
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to scratch the body.
  1. For some models: Remove the side sill panel.
  1. Remove the front inner fender (A).
    -1.
    From under the front bumper (B), remove the clip (C) securing the front bumper, the splash shield (D), and the front inner fender, and remove the clips (E) securing the front bumper and the front inner fender.
    -2.
    From the wheel arch, remove the screw (F) securing the front bumper and the front inner fender.
    -3.
    From the wheel arch, remove the clips (G, H, I) that secure the front inner fender (and splash shield) to the body.
    NOTE: To remove the clips (C, E, G), pry the inner clip up at the edge near the line (J) on its head.
  1. Install the inner fender in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, replace them with new ones.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
