DTC Troubleshooting: P2199 (125)
DTC P2199:
 IAT Sensor 1/2 Incorrect Voltage Correlation
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and review the general troubleshooting information.
  1. Check for poor connections or blockage at the intake air duct.
Is the intake air duct OK?
YES -
Go to Step 2 .
NO -
Repair or replace the damaged intake air duct, then go to Step 12 .
  1. Check for damage at the air cleaner housing.
Is it OK?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Replace the air cleaner housing, then go to Step 12 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Remove the MAF sensor/IAT sensor 1, and the MAP sensor/IAT sensor 2.
  1. Allow the MAF sensor/IAT sensor 1 and the MAP sensor/IAT sensor 2 to cool below 25 °C (77 °F).
  1. Note the ambient temperature.
  1. Connect the MAF sensor/IAT sensor 1 to its 2P connector and the MAP sensor/IAT sensor 2 to its 2P connector, but do not install them into the air intake duct or the IMRC valve.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Note the value of IAT SENSOR 1 and IAT SENSOR 2 quickly in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
  1. Compare the value of IAT SENSOR 1 and the ambient temperature, and the value of IAT SENSOR 2 and the ambient temperature individually.
Does either sensor differ more than 35 °C (63 °F) from the ambient temperature?
YES -
Replace the sensor that differed more than 35 °C (63 °F) from the ambient temperature, then go to Step 11 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the MAF sensor/IAT sensor 1, the MAP sensor/IAT sensor 2, and the ECM.■
  1. Install the MAF sensor/IAT sensor 1, and the MAP sensor/IAT sensor 2.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine. Hold the engine speed at 2,000 rpm (min −1 ) without load (in neutral) until the radiator fan comes on, then let it idle.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P2199 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the MAF sensor/IAT sensor 1, the MAP sensor/IAT sensor 2, and the ECM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Go to Step 16 .
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for DTC P2199 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the screen indicate PASSED?
YES -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs were indicated in Step 15 , go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
NO -
If the screen indicates FAILED, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the MAF sensor/IAT sensor 1, the MAP sensor/IAT sensor 2, and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If the screen indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 14 .
