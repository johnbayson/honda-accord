VSA OFF Switch Test
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Push out the VSA OFF switch (A) from the back of the instrument panel.
  1. Disconnect the VSA OFF switch 5P connector (B) or VSA OFF switch 13P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between the VSA OFF switch 5P connector terminals No. 1 and No. 2 or VSA OFF switch 13P connector terminals No. 11 and No. 12. There should be continuity when the switch is pressed, and no continuity when the switch is released.
  1. Check for continuity between VSA OFF switch 5P connector terminals No. 4 and No. 5 or VSA OFF switch 13P connector terminals No. 1 and No. 5. There should be continuity at all times.
  1. Install the VSA OFF switch in the reverse order of removal.
