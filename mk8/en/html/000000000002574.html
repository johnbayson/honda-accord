DTC Troubleshooting: U0122 (107)
DTC U0122:
 F-CAN Malfunction (Transmission Control Module (TCM) - Vehicle Stability Assist (VSA) Modulator-Control Unit)
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and any on-board snapshot with the HDS, and review General Troubleshooting Information.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Are DTC U0029 and U0122 indicated at the same time?
YES -
Go to the DTC U0029 troubleshooting. ■
NO -
Go to Step 3 .
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Is DTC U0122 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals between the gauge control module, the VSA modulator-control unit, and the TCM.■
  1. Check for communication to the VSA system with the HDS.
Does the HDS communicate with the VSA system?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Go to the DLC circuit troubleshooting. ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and wait for at least 2 minutes.
  1. Disconnect the VSA modulator-control unit 47P connector.
  1. Disconnect the TCM connector.
  1. Check for continuity between TCM connector terminal No. 53 and VSA modulator-control unit 47P connector terminal No. 12.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between TCM connector terminal No. 53 and the VSA modulator-control unit, then go to Step 11 .
  1. Check for continuity between TCM connector terminal No. 54 and VSA modulator-control unit 47P connector terminal No. 13.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Update the VSA modulator-control unit if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good VSA modulator-control unit, then go to Step 11 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between TCM connector terminal No. 54 and the VSA modulator-control unit, then go to Step 11 .
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine, and wait for at least 2 minutes.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0122 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals between TCM connector terminals No. 53, No. 54, the gauge control module, and the VSA modulator-control unit, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Go to Step 16 .
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for U0122 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the HDS indicate PASSED?
YES -
Troubleshooting is complete. If the VSA modulator-control unit was substituted, replace the original VSA modulator-control unit. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs were indicated in Step 15 , go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
NO -
If the HDS indicates FAILED, check for poor connections or loose terminals between TCM connector terminals No. 53, No. 54, the gauge control module, and the VSA modulator-control unit, then go to Step 1 . If the HDS indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 14 .
