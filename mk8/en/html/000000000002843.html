NOTE:
  • The bearing and the bearing outer race should be replaced as a set.
  • Replace the bearing with a new one whenever the outer race is replaced.
  • Do not use the thrust shim from the torque converter housing.
  • Adjust bearing preload after replacing the bearing and outer race.
  • Cost all parts with ATF during installation.
  1. Remove the bearing outer race (A), the 76.2 mm thrust washer (B), and the 76 mm thrust shim (C) from the transmission housing (D) by heating the transmission housing to about 100 °C (212 °F) using a heat gun (E). Do not heat the transmission housing more than 100 °C (212 °F).
    NOTE: Let the transmission housing cool to room temperature before installing the bearing outer race.
  1. Remove the bearing outer race (A) and the 80 mm thrust washer (B) from the torque converter housing (C).
  1. Install the 80 mm thrust washer and a new bearing outer race in the torque converter housing.
  1. Install the bearing outer race to install securely in the torque converter housing using the 15 x 135L driver handle and the 78 x 80 mm bearing driver attachment.
  1. Install the 76 mm thrust shim (A), the 76.2 mm thrust washer (B), and a new bearing outer race (C) in the transmission housing (D).
    NOTE: Be sure to install the 76.2 mm thrust washer with the ‘‘41382 RKY'' mark facing downward.
  1. Install the bearing outer race in securely so there is no clearance between the bearing outer race, the 76.2 mm thrust washer, the 76 mm thrust shim, and the transmission housing using the 15 x 135L driver handle and the 72 x 75 mm bearing driver attachment.
