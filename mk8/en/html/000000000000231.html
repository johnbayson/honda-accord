Power Seat Motor Test - Driver's
  1. Remove the driver's seat.
  1. Disconnect the 5P connector from each power seat motor.
    NOTE: All connectors are terminal side of male terminals.
  1. Test each motor by connecting power and ground according to the table. When the motor stops running, disconnect battery power immediately.
  1. If the motor does not run or fails to run smoothly, replace it.
    • Slide motor
    • Recline motor
    • Front up-down motor
    • Rear up-down motor
    NOTE: The front and rear up-down motors are part of the seat frame.
