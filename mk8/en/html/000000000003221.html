Navigation System Forced Starting of Display
Special tools required

SCS service connector
 07PAZ-0010100
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Remove these items:
    • Tonneau cover and cargo net
    • Spare tyre floor lid
  1. Connect the SCS service connector (A) to the navigation service connector (B) located behind the navigation unit.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Check that the diagnosis menu for the picture diagnosis starts up, and then changes to the system link menu.
    NOTE: If the display fails to display the system link screen, refer to no picture is displayed.
