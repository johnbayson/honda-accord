A/T Gear Position Indicator Panel Light Harness Replacement
LHD Model
NOTE: The A/T gear position indicator panel light, the park pin switch, and the shift lock solenoid harness are not available separately. Replace the A/T gear position indicator panel light, the park pin switch, and the shift lock solenoid harness as a set.
  1. Remove the shift lever assembly.
  1. Remove the shift lever knob.
  1. Remove the A/T gear position indicator panel base.
  1. Release the lock (A) of the shift lock release (B), then remove the shift lock release and the release spring (C).
  1. Remove the shift lock solenoid.
  1. Remove the harness wire ties (A), and remove the screw (B) securing the park pin switch (C), and remove the light bulb (D) from the socket.
  1. Remove the A/T gear position indicator panel light harness (E), the park pin switch harness (F), and the shift lock solenoid harness (G) from the harness guide, replace it.
  1. Apply non-hardening thread lock sealant to the screw threads. Install a new park pin switch, and secure the park pin switch with the screw.
  1. Tie the harnesses of the A/T gear position indicator panel light harness, the park pin switch harness and the shift lock solenoid harness at the guide with new harness wire ties.
  1. Install the A/T gear position indicator panel light bulb in the socket.
  1. Install the shift lock release spring (A) in the shift lock release (B).
  1. Install the shift lock release and the release spring on the shift lock release shaft end.
  1. Make sure that the release spring end (A) is installed in the shift lock release (B), and other side end (C) of the release spring is on the catch (D).
  1. Install the A/T gear position indicator panel base (A) on the shift lever bracket.
    NOTE: Take care not to pinch the harnesses.
  1. Route the A/T gear position indicator panel light harness (B), the park pin switch harness (C), and the shift lock solenoid harness. Take a slack out of the harnesses, and secure the harnesses with at the harness guide.
  1. Install the A/T gear position indicator panel light socket (D) in the panel base.
  1. Install the shift lever knob.
  1. Install the shift lever assembly.
