A/T Countershaft Idler Gear and 4th Gear Axial Clearance Inspection
  1. Measure the clearance between the 50.8 mm collar (A) and the 50.8 mm washer (B) with a feeler gauge (C) in at least three places. Use the average as the actual clearance.
    Standard: 0.005−0.040 mm (0.0002−0.0016 in.)

  1. If the clearance is out of the standard, remove the 50.8 mm washer and measure its thickness.
  1. Select and install a new washer, then recheck the clearance.
