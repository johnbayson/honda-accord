DTC Troubleshooting: P0716 (15), P0717 (15)
DTC P0716:
 Problem in Input Shaft (Mainshaft) Speed Sensor Circuit
DTC P0717:
 Problem in Input Shaft (Mainshaft) Speed Sensor Circuit (No Signal Input)
NOTE:
  • Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and any on-board snapshot with the HDS, and review General Troubleshooting Information.
  • This code is caused by an electrical circuit problem and cannot be caused by a mechanical problem in the transmission.
  1. Check for proper input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor installation. If the sensor is installed improperly, correct the problem, then go to Step 25 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Block the rear wheels and raise the front of the vehicle, make sure it is securely supported, and allow the front wheels to rotate freely, or raise the vehicle on a lift.
  1. Start the engine, and turn the VSA off (the light on the VSA OFF switch comes on). Run the vehicle in either 2nd, 3rd, 4th, or 5th gear (not 1st) in D at speeds over 20 km/h (12 mph) for at least 10 seconds. Slow down and stop the wheels.
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for P0716 or P0717 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the HDS indicate FAILED?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
If the HDS indicates PASSED, intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for loose or poor connections between the TCM and the input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor. If the HDS indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 5 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect the TCM connector.
  1. Check for continuity between TCM connector terminals No. 52 and body ground, and between No. 59 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Repair open in the wires between TCM connector terminals No. 52, No. 59 and body ground (G151), or repair poor body ground (G151), then go to Step 24 .
  1. Connect the TCM connector.
  1. Disconnect the input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor connector terminal No. 1 and body ground.
Is there about 5 V?
YES -
Go to Step 17 .
NO -
Go to Step 14 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect the TCM connector.
  1. Check for continuity between TCM connector terminal No. 18 and input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor connector terminal No. 1.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 30 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between TCM connector terminal No. 18 and the input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor, then go to Step 24 .
  1. Measure the voltage between input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor connector terminals No. 2 and No. 3.
Is there about 5 V?
YES -
Replace the input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor, then go to Step 24 .
NO -
Go to Step 18 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect the TCM connector.
  1. Check for continuity between input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor connector terminal No. 2 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to body ground in the wire between TCM connector terminal No. 46 and the input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor, then go to Step 24 .
NO -
Go to Step 21 .
  1. Connect the TCM connectors.
  1. Check for continuity between TCM connector terminal No. 19 and input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor connector terminal No. 3.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 23 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor connector and TCM connector terminal No. 19, then go to Step 24 .
  1. Check for continuity between TCM connector terminal No. 46 and input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor connector terminal No. 2.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 30 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between TCM connector terminal No. 46 and the input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor, then go to Step 24 .
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine, and turn the VSA off (the light on the VSA OFF switch comes on). Run the vehicle in either 2nd, 3rd, 4th, or 5th gear (not 1st) in D at speeds over 20 km/h (12 mph) for at least 10 seconds. Slow down and stop the wheels.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0716 or P0717 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals between the input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor and the TCM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Go to Step 29 .
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for P0716 or P0717 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the HDS indicate PASSED?
YES -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other temporary DTCs or DTCs were indicated in Step 28 , go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
NO -
If the HDS indicates FAILED, check for poor connections or loose terminals between the input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor and the TCM, then go to Step 1 . If the HDS indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 27 .
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Update the TCM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good TCM.
  1. Start the engine, and turn the VSA off (the light on the VSA OFF switch comes on). Run the vehicle in either 2nd, 3rd, 4th, or 5th gear (not 1st) in D at speeds over 20 km/h (12 mph) for at least 10 seconds. Slow down and stop the wheels.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0716 or P0717 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals between the input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor and the TCM. If the TCM was updated, substitute a known-good TCM, then go to Step 32 . If the TCM was substituted, go to Step 1 .
NO -
Go to Step 34 .
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for P0716 or P0717 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the HDS indicate PASSED?
YES -
If the TCM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the TCM was substituted, replace the original TCM. If any other DTCs were indicated in Step 33 , go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
NO -
If the HDS indicates FAILED, check for poor connections or loose terminals between the input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor and the TCM. If the TCM was updated, substitute a known-good TCM, then go to Step 32 . If the TCM was substituted, go to Step 1 . If the HDS indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 32 .
