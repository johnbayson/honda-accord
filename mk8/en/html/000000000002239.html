Transmission Mount Replacement
  1. Remove the two bolts securing the expansion tank.
  1. Loosen the upper torque rod mounting bolt (A).
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing assembly.
  1. Raise the vehicle on the lift to full height.
  1. Remove the engine undercover.
  1. Loosen the lower torque rod mounting bolts (A).
  1. Lower the vehicle on the lift.
  1. Support the transmission with a jack and wood block under the transmission.
  1. Remove ground cable (A), then remove the transmission mount (B).
  1. Install the transmission mount with the new mounting bolts (C).
  1. Tighten the new transmission mount bracket mounting bolt and nuts (D).
  1. Install the ground cable.
  1. Raise the vehicle on the lift to full height.
  1. Tighten the lower torque rod mounting bolts.
  1. Install the engine undercover.
  1. Lower the vehicle on the lift.
  1. Tighten the upper torque rod mounting bolt.
  1. Install the expansion tank.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing assembly.
