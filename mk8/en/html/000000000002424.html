ATF Cooler Replacement
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. Remove the harness clamp (A) from the ATF cooler guide (B), then remove the ATF cooler guide.
  1. Disconnect the ATF cooler hoses (A) from the ATF cooler lines (B).
  1. Remove the harness clamps (C) from the ATF cooler lower bracket.
  1. Remove the ATF cooler assembly (D).
  1. If needed, remove the ATF cooler (A) from the brackets (B).
  1. Install the ATF cooler assembly in the reverse order of removal. If the ATF cooler hoses are disassembled from the ATF cooler, reassemble the ATF cooler hoses referring to the ATF cooler hose replacement.
  1. Connect the ATF cooler hoses.
  1. Install the front bumper.
  1. Install the splash shield.
