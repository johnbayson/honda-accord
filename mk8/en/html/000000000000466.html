Power Mirror Actuator Replacement
  1. Remove the mirror holder.
  1. Remove the mirror from the door.
  1. Disconnect the connector(s) from the power mirror.
  1. Record the power mirror 13P connector terminal locations and wire colors.
  1. Disassemble the power mirror 13P connector, and remove all terminals from it.
  1. Remove the gasket (A).
  1. Remove the screw and harness clip (B).
  1. Remove the cover (C) and screws, then separate the mirror housings (D) from the bracket.
  1. Remove the screws, and separate the brackets (E).
  1. Remove the side turn signal light (F) and disconnect the 2P connector from the light.
  1. Remove the screws and disassemble the mirror housing, then remove the mirror actuator (G).
  1. Install the mirror actuator in the reverse order of removal.
