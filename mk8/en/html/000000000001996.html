Engine Oil Pump Chain Replacement
Removal
NOTE: Keep the oil pump chain away from magnetic fields.
  1. Remove the cam chain.
  1. Install the engine hanger to the oil filter housing, then attach the chain hoist and support the engine.
  1. Remove the oil pan.
  1. Loosely install the crankshaft pulley.
  1. To hold the rear balancer shaft, insert a 8 mm long pin punch (A) (Snap-on PPC110LA or equivalent) into the maintenance hole in the balancer shaft holder and through the rear balancer shaft.
  1. Turn the crankshaft counterclockwise to compress the oil pump chain auto-tensioner.
  1. Compress the auto-tensioner fully, then insert the stopper (P/N 14511-RL0-003) or 2.5 mm (0.10 in.) diameter pin. Turn the crankshaft clockwise to secure the pin.
  1. Remove the oil pump chain auto-tensioner.
  1. Remove the oil pump chain guide (A) and tensioner arm (B).
  1. Remove the oil pump sprocket mounting bolt, then remove the oil pump sprocket, key, and oil pump chain.
Installation
  1. Set the crankshaft to top dead center (TDC). Align the punch mark (A) on the crankshaft sprocket with the pointer (B) on the engine block.
  1. Install the oil pump chain on the crankshaft sprocket with the colored link plate (A) aligned with the punch mark (B) on the crankshaft sprocket.
  1. To hold the rear balancer shaft, insert a 8 mm long pin punch (A) (Snap-on PPC110LA or equivalent) into the maintenance hole in the balancer shaft holder and through the rear balancer shaft.
  1. Apply new engine oil to the threads of the oil pump sprocket mounting bolts (B).
  1. Install the oil pump chain on the oil pump chain sprocket with the punch marks (C) aligned with the two colored link plates (D), then install the oil pump chain sprocket to the oil pump and tighten the oil pump sprocket mounting bolt.
  1. Remove the pin punch.
  1. Apply new engine oil to the threads of the oil pump chain guide mounting bolts (A) and the oil pump chain tensioner arm mounting bolt (B).
  1. Install the oil pump chain guide (C) and tensioner arm (D).
  1. Install the oil pump chain auto-tensioner.
  1. Remove the stopper from the oil pump chain auto-tensioner.
  1. Install the oil pan.
  1. Support the engine with a jack and wood block under the oil pan.
  1. Remove the engine hanger from the oil filter housing.
  1. Install the cam chain.
  1. Install the crankshaft pulley.
