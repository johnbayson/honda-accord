Power Seat Adjustment Switch Test/Replacement - Front Passenger's
Front Passenger's Seat
  1. Remove the front passenger's seat.
  1. Remove the power seat adjustment switch knobs (A) and the recline cover from the front passenger's seat , then remove the two screws and the power seat adjustment switch (B).
  1. Disconnect the 12P connector from the power seat adjustment switch.
  1. Reinstall the adjustment switch knobs to the switch.
