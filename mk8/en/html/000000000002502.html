A/T Intermediary Shaft Disassembly, Inspection, and Reassembly
  1. Remove the 26.5 mm washer, the snap ring, the cotter retainer, and the 19.8 mm cotters from the intermediary shaft.
  1. Remove the intermediary shaft 4th gear using a press, to disassemble the intermediary shaft and gears.
  1. Inspect the bearings for galling and rough movement.
  1. Check the shaft bearing surfaces for scoring and excessive wear, and check the shaft splines for excessive wear and damage.
  1. Check the sealing rings for excessive wear and damage.
  1. Check the intermediary shaft 3rd gear axial clearance, and the intermediary shaft installation height.
  1. Before installing the O-rings, wrap the shaft splines with tape to prevent damage to the O-rings.
  1. Lubricate all parts with ATF, and reassemble the shaft and gears.
  1. Install the press-fitted intermediary shaft 4th gear in the direction shown using the 40 mm driver handle and a press.
