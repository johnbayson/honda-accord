DTC Troubleshooting: P0973 (7)
DTC P0973:
 Short in Shift Solenoid Valve A Circuit
NOTE:
  • Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and any on-board snapshot with the HDS, and review General Troubleshooting Information.
  • This code is caused by an electrical circuit problem and cannot be caused by a mechanical problem in the transmission.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine. With the brake pedal pressed, shift to R, and wait for at least 1 second.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0973 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Go to Step 5 .
  1. Select Shift Solenoid Valve A in the Miscellaneous Test Menu, and test the shift solenoid valve A with the HDS.
Is a clicking sound heard?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Go to Step 8 .
  1. Start the engine. With the brake pedal pressed, shift to R, and wait for at least 1 second.
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for P0973 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the HDS indicate FAILED?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
If the HDS indicates PASSED, intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for intermittent short to body ground in the wire between shift solenoid valve A and the TCM. If the HDS indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 6 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect the TCM connector.
  1. Check for continuity between TCM connector terminals No. 52 and body ground, and between No. 59 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 11 .
NO -
Repair open in the wires between TCM connector terminals No. 52, No. 59 and body ground (G151), or repair poor body ground (G151), then go to Step 11 .
  1. Measure the resistance between TCM connector terminals No. 16 and No. 52 or No. 59.
Is there less than 12 Ω?
YES -
Go to Step 12 .
NO -
Go to Step 18 .
  1. Disconnect the shift solenoid wire harness connector.
  1. Check for continuity between TCM connector terminals No. 16 and No. 52 or No. 59.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to body ground in the wire between TCM connector terminal No. 16 and the shift solenoid wire harness connector, then go to Step 18 .
NO -
Go to Step 14 .
  1. Inspect shift solenoid valve A and the shift solenoid wire harness.
Is shift solenoid valve A OK?
YES -
Go to Step 15 .
NO -
Replace shift solenoid valve A or the shift solenoid wire harness, then go to Step 18 .
  1. Remove the TCM relay.
  1. Connect TCM relay 4P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between TCM connector terminal No. 55 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 24 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between TCM connector terminal No. 55 and the TCM relay 4P connector, then go to Step 18 .
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine in P, and wait for at least 1 second. With the brake pedal pressed, shift to the R, and wait for at least 1 second.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0973 indicated?
YES -
Check for intermittent short to body ground in the wire between shift solenoid valve A and the TCM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Go to Step 23 .
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for P0973 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the HDS indicate PASSED?
YES -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs were indicated in Step 22 , go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
NO -
If the HDS indicates FAILED, check for intermittent short to body ground in the wire between shift solenoid valve A and the TCM, then go to Step 1 . If the HDS indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 21 .
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Update the TCM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good TCM.
  1. Start the engine in P, and wait for at least 1 second. With the brake pedal pressed, shift to the R, and wait for at least 1 second.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0973 indicated?
YES -
Check for intermittent short to body ground in the wire between shift solenoid valve A and the TCM. If the TCM was updated, substitute a known-good TCM, then go to Step 26 . If the TCM was substituted, go to Step 1 .
NO -
Go to Step 28 .
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for P0973 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the HDS indicate PASSED?
YES -
If the TCM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the TCM was substituted, replace the original TCM. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs were indicated in Step 27 , go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
NO -
If the HDS indicates FAILED, check for intermittent short to body ground in the wire between shift solenoid valve A and the TCM. If the TCM was updated, substitute a known-good TCM, then go to Step 26 . If the TCM was substituted, go to Step 1 . If the HDS indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 26 .
