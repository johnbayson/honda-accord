Tailgate Adjustment
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • When removing components, use the trim tools or quivalent.
  1. With power tailgate: Do the battery terminal disconnection procedure, then wait at least 3 minutes before beginning work.
    NOTE: Open the tailgate by using the emergency lever while adjusting the tailgate:
    • Without power tailgate
    • With power tailgate
  1. Remove these items:
    • Tailgate weatherstrip
    • Tonneau cover and cargo net, for some models
    • Under floor cover:
      • -
      With temporary repair kit (TRK)
      -
      With compact spare tyre/with normal spare tyre
    • Rear seat cushion
    • Rear seat belt lower anchors, both sides
    • Rear seat side bolsters, both sides
    • Damper maintenance lids, both sides
    • Cargo net strikers, both sides, for some models
    • Rear seat belt anchor wires, both sides
    • Cargo area side trim panels, both sides
    • Rear door opening seals, both sides as needed
    • Roof end trim
    • Quarter pillar trim, both sides
    • Support struts, both sides
  1. Slightly loosen each nut and screw.
  1. Adjust the tailgate (A) alignment in the following sequence:
    • Adjust the tailgate hinges (B) right and left, as well as forward and rearward, by using the elongated holes in the hinges.
    • Turn the tailgate edge cushions (C), in or out as necessary, to make the tailgate fit flush with the body at the side edges.
    • Adjust the fit between the tailgate and tailgate opening by moving the striker (D), and pull the striker cap (E) up, then adjust the striker right or left until it is centered in the tailgate latch (F).
  1. Tighten each nut and screw securely.
  1. Check that the tailgate opens properly and locks securely.
  1. Reinstall the support struts securely.
  1. Apply touch-up paint to the hinge mounting bolts and around the hinges, and let the paint dry.
  1. Reinstall all of the removed parts.
  1. With power tailgate: Do the battery terminal reconnection procedure.
