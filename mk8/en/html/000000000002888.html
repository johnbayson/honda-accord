LKAS System Troubleshooting
NOTE
  • Retrieve DTCs, using the HDS.
  • The LKAS system consists of the LKAS control unit, a LKAS camera and the EPS control unit. Depending on the trouble that has been occurred, each unit or the camera individually writes in DTCs. Therefore, when a trouble occurs, determine which unit writes these DTCs at first, then starts diagnosing the trouble from the units or the LKAS camera the DTCs are coming from.
  1. Align the front wheels straight ahead.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Connect the HDS to the data link connector (DLC) (A).
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
    NOTE: See the HDS Help menu for specific instructions.
  1. Watch the LKAS indicator (orange).
Does the LKAS indicator (orange) come on?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Go to Step 7 .
  1. Make sure the HDS communicates with the LKAS control unit.
Is there communication?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Go to Step 15 .
  1. Check for DTCs in the LKAS control unit DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Is DTC 3, 4, 27, or 29 indicated?
YES -
Troubleshoot the indicated DTC(s).■
NO -
Go to Step 8 .
  1. Clear the LKAS control unit DTCs using the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), then turn it to ON (II).
  1. Watch the LKAS indicator (orange).
Does the LKAS indicator (orange) come on?
YES -
Go to Step 11 .
NO -

Troubleshoot the LKAS control unit DTCs.■
  1. Clear the DTC using the HDS, and make sure the DTCs is not left.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and disconnect the HDS from the data link connector (DLC).
  1. Check the fuse No. 1 (7.5 A) in the driver's under-dash fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 16 .
NO -
Repair short to body ground in the wire between fuse No. 1 (7.5 A) in the driver's under-dash fuse/relay box and LKAS control unit.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between body ground and LKAS control unit 26P connector No. 1 terminal individually.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 18 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the No. 1 (7.5 A) fuse in the driver's under-dash fuse/relay box and LKAS control unit.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and LKAS control unit 26P connector No. 13 terminal individually.
Are there continuity?
