Idle Speed Inspection
NOTE: Before checking the idle speed, check these items:
  • The malfunction indicator lamp (MIL) has not been reported on, and there are no DTCs.
  • Air cleaner
  1. Connect the HDS to the data link connector (DLC) (A) located under the driver's side of the dashboard, or a tachometer (B) to the test tachometer connector (C) located at the front left side of the engine compartment.
  1. Start the engine. Hold the engine speed at 3,000 rpm (min −1 ) without load (in neutral) until the radiator fan comes on, then let it idle.
  1. Check the idle speed without load conditions: headlights, blower fan, radiator fan, and air conditioner off.
    Idle speed should be:
    820±50 rpm (min −1 )

    NOTE: If the idle speed is not within specification, go to the symptom troubleshooting.
