Intercooler Replacement

Exploded View
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. Remove the front splash shield.
  1. Disconnect the intercooler intake hose (A) and the intercooler outlet hose (B), then remove the intercooler upper brackets (C).
  1. Remove the intercooler (D), then remove the intercooler outlet pipe (E) and the intercooler lower cushions (F).
  1. Install the intercooler outlet pipe with new O-ring (G) and the intercooler lower cushions, then install the intercooler.
  1. Install the intercooler upper brackets, then connect the intercooler intake hose and the intercooler outlet hose.
    NOTE: When tightening the screw of the hose band (H), align the edge of the hose band (I) with the mark (J) painted on the hose band. If you tighten the screw over the mark, replace the hose band.
  1. Install the front splash shield.
  1. Install the front bumper.
