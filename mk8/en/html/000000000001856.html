Heater Outlet Removal and Installation
Removal
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing assembly.
  1. Remove the intake air duct.
  1. Remove the intake shutter valve (ISV).
  1. Disconnect the engine coolant temperature (ECT) sensor 1 connector.
  1. Disconnect the heater hose (A) and water bypass hoses (B), then remove the heater outlet (C).
  1. Remove the bleed bolt (A) and ECT sensor 1 (B).
Installation
  1. Install the bleed bolt (A) and ECT sensor 1 (B).
  1. Install the heater outlet (A) with new O-ring (B), then install the heater hose (C) and water bypass hoses (D).
  1. Connect the ECT sensor 1 connector.
  1. Install the ISV.
  1. Install the intake air duct.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing assembly.
  1. Refill the expansion tank with engine coolant, and bleed air from the cooling system with the heater valve open.
