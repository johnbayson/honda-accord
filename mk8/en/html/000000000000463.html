Power Mirror Replacement
  1. Lower the door glass fully.
  1. With your hand, carefully pull the top edge of the tweeter cover (A) out to detach the hooks (B) and the clips, and remove the cover. If equipped, disconnect the door tweeter connector (C).
  1. Remove the front door panel.
  1. Disconnect the connector(s) (A).
  1. While holding the mirror (B), remove the nuts securing the mirror.
  1. Release the screw portion (A) from the hole in the door, then remove the mirror (B). Take care not to scratch the door.
  1. Install the mirror in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the connectors are plugged in properly.
    • Replace any damaged clips with new ones.
    • Push the clips on the cover into place securely.
