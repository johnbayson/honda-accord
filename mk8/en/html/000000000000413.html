Headlight Initial Position Learning Procedure
Do the headlight initial position learning when any of the related parts of the headlight leveling system shown is replaced:
  • Headlight
  • HID unit
  • Headlight leveling motor
  • Headlight leveling control unit
  • Suspension stroke sensor
Learning Procedure
Before learning the headlights:
  • Park the vehicle on a level surface, and remove all of the baggage from the vehicle.
  • Make sure the tire pressures are correct.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Slide the front seats backward fully.
  1. Have the driver (about 165 lb (75 kg)) or someone who weighs the same sit in the driver's seat.
  1. Connect the HDS to the data link connector
  1. Jump the SCS line with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Within 5 seconds, turn the headlights (low beam) or passing switch ON and OFF three times repeatedly, and leave them on.
    • If the headlights aiming move down then return to the original position, the initial position learning is complete.
    • If the headlights aiming don't move, go to Step 1 and repeat the procedure.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect the HDS from the data link connector.
  1. Adjust the headlight aiming to conform with the applicable requirements.
