Thermostat Housing Removal and Installation
Removal
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the diesel particulate filter (DPF).
  1. Remove the connecting pipe.
  1. Disconnect the upper radiator hose (A) and lower radiator hose (B).
  1. Remove the oil bypass pipe (A) and turbocharger bracket (B).
  1. Remove the intake shutter valve (ISV).
  1. Disconnect the water bypass hoses (A), then remove the heater pipe (B).
  1. Remove the thermostat housing.
  1. Remove the water outlet (A), thermostat cover (B), and thermostat (C).
Installation
  1. Install the new rubber seal (A) onto the thermostat, then install the thermostat (B) with pin (C) up, and install the thermostat cover (D).
  1. Install the water bypass pipe (E) with new O-ring (F).
  1. Install the thermostat housing (A) with new O-rings (B).
  1. Install the heater pipe (A) with new O-ring (B), then install the water bypass hoses (C).
  1. Install the ISV.
  1. Check the bolt (A) and oil bypass pipe (B) for clogs. If they are clogged clean or replace the bolt and/or pipe.
  1. Install the oil bypass pipe with new gaskets (C) and new washers (D).
  1. Install the turbocharger bracket (E).
  1. Install the upper radiator hose (A) and lower radiator hose (B).
  1. install the connecting pipe.
  1. Install the DPF.
  1. Refill the expansion tank with engine coolant, and bleed air from the cooling system with the heater valve open.
