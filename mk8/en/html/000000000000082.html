Steering Rack Guide Adjustment
Special Tools Required

Locknut eye wrench, 40 mm
 07MAA-SL00100
  1. Set the wheels in the straight ahead position.
  1. Loosen the rack guide screw locknut (A) with the locknut wrench, then remove the rack guide screw (B).
  1. Remove the old sealant from the rack guide screw (A), and apply new sealant (Three Bond 1215 or Loctite 5699) to the middle of the threads (B). Loosely install the rack guide screw on the steering gearbox.
    NOTE: If more than 5 minutes has passed after applying the sealant, remove the old sealant and residue, and reapply new sealant.
  1. Tighten the rack guide screw (A) to 25 N·m (2.5 kgf·m, 18 lbf·ft), then loosen it.
  1. Retighten the rack guide screw to 5.9 N·m (0.6 kgf·m, 4 lbf·ft), then back it off to the specified angle.
    Specified return angle:
    10±5 °

  1. Hold the rack guide screw stationary with a wrench, and tighten the locknut by hand until it's fully seated.
  1. Install the locknut wrench on the locknut (B), and hold the rack guide screw stationary with a wrench.
    Tighten the locknut to the specified torque.
  1. Check for unusual steering effort through the complete turning range.
  1. Check the steering wheel rotation play and the power assist.
