Multi-information Display Select/Reset/Information Switch Test/Replacement
  1. Remove the driver's airbag assembly.
  1. Disconnect the 7P connector (A) from the select/reset/information switch (B).
  1. Remove the screws and the switch.
  1. Measure resistance between the select/reset/information switch 7P connector No. 2 and No. 6 terminals in each switch position according to the table.
Position
Resistance
▲
About 33 Ω
SEL/RESET
About 1,043 Ω
▼
About 363 Ω

  1. If the resistance is not as specified, replace the switch.
  1. If the switch is OK, but there is a problem with the switch function, check the steering switches harness and cable reel.
