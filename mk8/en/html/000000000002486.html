A/T Clutch Disassembly
Special Tools Required

Clutch spring compressor set
 07LAE-PX40000

Clutch spring compressor attachment
 07LAE-PX40100

Clutch spring compressor attachment
 07HAE-PL50100

Clutch spring compressor bolt set 10
 07GAE-PG40200

Snap ring pliers
 07LGC-0010100
  1. Remove the snap ring (A) with a screwdriver (B).
  1. Remove the 1st clutch end-plate (A), the 1st clutch discs (B) (5), the 1st clutch wave-plates (C) (5), the disc spring (D), 1st-hold clutch plate B (E), the 1st-hold clutch discs (F) (3), the 1st-hold clutch wave-plates (G) (2), and the 1st-hold clutch flat-plate (H) (1) from the 1st/1st-hold clutch drum (I).
  1. Make reference marks on the clutch wave-plate.
  1. Remove the clutch end-plate (A), the clutch discs (B) (7), clutch wave-plates (C) (3), and the clutch flat-plates (D) (4) from the 2nd clutch drum (E).
  1. Make reference marks on the clutch wave-plates.
  1. Remove the clutch end-plate (A), the clutch discs (B) (6), the clutch wave-plates (C) (3), and the clutch flat-plates (D) (3) from the 3rd clutch drum (E).
  1. Make reference marks on the clutch wave-plates.
  1. Remove the clutch end-plate (A), the clutch discs (B) (5), the clutch wave-plates (C) (2), the clutch flat-plates (D) (3), and the disc spring (E) from the 4th clutch drum (F).
  1. Make reference marks on the clutch wave-plates.
  1. Remove the clutch end-plate (A), the clutch discs (B) (5), the clutch wave-plates (C) (2), the clutch flat-plates (D) (3), and the disc spring (E) from the 5th clutch drum (F).
  1. Make reference marks on the clutch wave-plates.
  1. Install the clutch spring compressor set.
  1. Be sure the clutch spring compressor set (A) is adjusted to have full contact with the spring retainer (B) on the 1st, 2nd, and 4th clutches.
  1. Set the clutch spring compressor set (A) on the spring retainer (B) of the 3rd and 5th clutches so that it pushes on the clutch return spring (C).
  1. Check the placement of the clutch spring compressor set. If either end of the clutch spring compressor set is set over an area of the spring retainer that is unsupported by the return spring, the spring retainer may be damaged.
  1. Check the placement of the clutch spring compressor set. If either end of the clutch spring compressor set is not set over the clutch return spring end (A), the spring retainer may be damaged.
  1. Compress the return spring until the snap ring can be removed.
  1. Remove the snap ring using snap ring pliers.
  1. Remove the clutch spring compressor set.
  1. Remove the snap ring (A), the spring retainer (B), and the return spring (C).
  1. Wrap a shop rag around the clutch drum (A), and apply air pressure to the fluid passage to remove the clutch piston (B). Place a finger tip on the other passage while applying air pressure.
  1. Remove the 1st/1st-hold clutch piston (A) from the clutch drum (B), and remove the O-rings from the 1st clutch piston.
  1. Remove the 1st-hold clutch piston (A) from the 1st clutch piston (B), and remove the O-rings from the 1st-hold clutch piston.
  1. Remove the clutch piston (A), and remove the O-rings from the 2nd, 3rd, 4th, and 5th clutch pistons.
