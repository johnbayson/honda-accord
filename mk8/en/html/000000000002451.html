A/T 4th Clutch Transmission Fluid Pressure Switch Replacement
  1. Do the battery removal procedure.
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing and the intake air duct.
  1. Place a floor jack under the transmission housing.
  1. Remove the ground cable (A) from the transmission mount bracket (B).
  1. Remove the transmission mount bracket mounting bolts and nuts from the transmission housing.
  1. Remove the transmission mount mounting bolts from the body frame, then remove the transmission mount (A).
    NOTE: Do not remove the TORX bolt (B) from the transmission mount. If the TORX bolt is removed, the transmission mount must be replaced as an assembly.
  1. Disconnect the 4th clutch transmission fluid pressure switch connector (A).
  1. Remove the bolt securing the harness holder (B).
  1. Remove the 4th clutch transmission fluid pressure switch (C).
  1. Install a new 4th clutch transmission fluid pressure switch and a new sealing washer (D), and tighten the switch.
  1. Make sure there is no water, oil, dust, or foreign particles inside the connector.
  1. Connect the connector securely.
  1. Position the transmission mount (A), and loosely install new transmission mount mounting bolts (B) to the body frame.
  1. Loosely install new transmission mount bracket mounting bolts (A) and nuts (B) to the transmission housing.
  1. Install the ground cable (C) to the transmission mount.
  1. Tighten the transmission mount bolts and nuts to the specified torque.
  1. Remove the floor jack.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing and the intake air duct.
  1. Do the battery installation procedure.
