Intake Air Resonator Removal/Installation
  1. Do the battery removal procedure.
  1. Remove the air cleaner.
  1. Remove the splash cover (A), the intake air duct (B), the bolt (C).
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. Remove the left front wheel.
  1. Remove the clips (A), then strip off the inner fender (B).
  1. Remove the intake air resonator (A).
  1. Remove the intake air duct (B).
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal with a new seal rings (C).
  1. Do the battery installation procedure.
