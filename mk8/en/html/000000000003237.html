Relay Circuit Board Test
Radiator Fan Relay Circuit and Fan Control Relay Circuit
  1. Do the battery terminal disconnection procedure.
  1. Disconnect the connectors B (14P) and C (10P) from under-hood fuse/relay box (the relay circuit board).
  1. Test each relay circuit as shown:
    • Radiator fan relay circuit
      • -
      When power is connected to the B2 or B8 terminal and ground is connected to the B1 terminal, there should be continuity between the C1 and C7 terminals.
      When power is disconnected, there should be no continuity between the C1 and C7 terminals.
    • Fan control relay circuit
      • -
      When power is connected to the B2 or B8 terminal and ground is connected to the B1 terminal, there should be continuity between the C10 and B7 terminals.
      When power is disconnected, there should be no continuity between the C10 and C7 terminals.
  1. If any of these relay circuit fails the test, replace the relay circuit board.
  1. Do the battery terminal reconnection procedure.
Wiper Relay Circuit, Wiper Motor HIGH/LOW Relay Circuit and Wiper Motor Intermittent Relay Circuit
  1. Do the battery terminal disconnection procedure.
  1. Disconnect the connectors B (14P) and C (10P) from under-hood fuse/relay box (the relay circuit board).
  1. Test each relay circuit as shown:
    • Wiper motor relay circuit
      • -
      When power is connected to the B3 and C9 terminal and ground is connected to the B11 and B14 terminal, there should be continuity between the B3 and B6 terminals.
      When power is disconnected, there should be no continuity between the B3 and B6 terminals.
    • Wiper motor HIGH/LOW relay circuit
      • -
      When power is connected to the B3 and C9 terminal and ground is connected to the B5 and B11 terminal, there should be voltage between the B3 and B4 terminals.
      When power is disconnected, there should be continuity between the B6 and C9 terminals.
    • Wiper motor intermittent relay circuit
      • -
      When power is connected to the B3 and C9 terminal and ground is connected to the B11 and B14 terminal, there should be voltage between the B3 and B6 terminals.
      When power is disconnected, there should be continuity between the B6 and C9 terminals.
  1. If any of these relay circuit fails the test, replace the relay circuit board.
  1. Do the battery terminal reconnection procedure.
