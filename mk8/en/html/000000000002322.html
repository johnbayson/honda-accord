Brake Booster Replacement
RHD model
  1. Recover the refrigerant with a recovery/recycling/charging station.
  1. Remove the master cylinder.
  1. Remove the clutch fluid reservoir (A) from the bracket. Do not disconnect the clutch reservoir hose.
  1. Disconnect the vacuum hose (B) from the brake booster (C).
  1. Disconnect the A/C line (A) from the evaporator core, then remove the A/C line assembly mount nut (B).
  1. Remove the receiver lines (A).
  1. Remove the expansion tank (A) without disconnecting hoses.
  1. Remove the suction line (A), then remove the expansion tank bracket mount bolts (B).
  1. Remove the driver's dashboard undercover.
  1. Remove the lock pin (A) and the joint pin (B), then loosen the nut (C) and disconnect the yoke (D) from the brake pedal.
  1. Remove the brake booster mounting nuts (E).
  1. Remove the yoke from the brake booster rod (F).
  1. Remove the lock pin (A) and the joint pin (B), then remove the clutch pedal mouting nut (C) and disconnect the yoke (D) from the clutch pedal.
  1. Remove the clutch master cylinder (A), then remove it aside. Do not disconnect the clutch line.
  1. Remove the brake booster (B) from the engine compartment.
    • Be careful not to damage the booster surfaces and threads of the booster stud bolts.
    • Be careful not to bend or damage the brake lines.
    NOTE: Use new brake booster gasket (C) reassembly.
  1. Install the brake booster in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Install the master cylinder after installing the brake booster.
    • Check the brake pedal height and free play after installing the master cylinder, and adjust it if necessary.
    • Bleed the brake system.
    • Do the charge the A/C system.
