DTC Troubleshooting: B1382
DTC B1382:
 Tailgate Lock Full Switch Input Circuit Malfunction
  1. Open the tailgate with the tailgate outer handle.
  1. Close the tailgate manually.
  1. Open the tailgate with the tailgate outer handle.
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC B1382 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for loose or poor connections.■
  1. Slowly close the tailgate manually.
  1. Operate the power tailgate outer handle and wait for 10 seconds. Check for the closer motor release operation.
Can you hear the closer motor operate?
YES -
Go to Step 16 .
NO -
Go to Step 8 .
  1. Check the No. 7 (PTG CLOSER) (20 A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Replace the fuse and recheck. If the fuse is blown again, repair a short to ground in the wire between the under-hood fuse/relay box and the power tailgate control unit. ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect power tailgate control unit connector B (20P).
  1. Measure the voltage between power tailgate control unit connector B (20P) No. 12 terminal and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 12 .
NO -
Repair an open in the wire.■
  1. Disconnect power tailgate closer unit connector A (2P).

Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 14 .
NO -
