Exhaust Camshaft Gear Replacement
Removal
NOTE: Keep the cam chain away from magnetic fields.
  1. Remove the cylinder head cover.
  1. Remove the cam chain sprocket.
  1. Remove the intake camshaft gear.
  1. Hold the exhaust camshaft with an open-end wrench, then loosen the exhaust camshaft gear mounting bolt.
  1. Remove the exhaust camshaft gear mounting bolt (A), then remove the exhaust camshaft gear (B).
Installation
NOTE: Keep the cam chain away from magnetic fields.
  1. Install the exhaust camshaft gear to the exhaust cam shaft.
  1. Apply new engine oil to the threads and flange of the exhaust camshaft gear mounting bolt, and install it. Hold the exhaust camshaft with an open-end wrench, then tighten the bolt.
    Specified Torque:
    12 x 1.25 mm
    118 N·m (12.0 kgf·m, 86.8 lbf·ft)

  1. Install the intake camshaft gear.
  1. Install the cam chain sprocket.
  1. Install the cylinder head cover.
