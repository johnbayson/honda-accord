Front Fender Fairing Replacement
For Some Models
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to scratch the body.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Side sill panel, for some models
    • Front inner fender
  1. Open the front door. Remove the upper clip, and from inside the door, remove the lower clip securing the front fender fairing (A) and the front fender (B).
  1. From the wheel arch, release the clips (A, B) securing the front fender fairing (C).
  1. Remove the front fender fairing (A).
  1. Install the fender fairing in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, replace them with new ones.
    • Before installing the clips in the door opening area, install the front fender fairing (A) to the front fender (B) properly as shown.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
