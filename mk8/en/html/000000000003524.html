DTC Troubleshooting: B1388
DTC B1388:
 Power Tailgate Drive Motor Circuit Malfunction
  1. Open the tailgate with the tailgate outer handle.
  1. Close the tailgate automatically with the inside tailgate switch.
    NOTE: If the tailgate does not close, wait for about 5 seconds, then try it again. If the tailgate does not close even if repeats five times, go to Step 3 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0) and then back to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC B1388 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for loose or poor connections.■
  1. Check the No. 3-2 (PTG MTR) (40 A) fuse, No. 7 (PTG CLOSER) (20 A) fuse, and No. 15 (10 A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box.
Are the fuses OK?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Replace the blown fuse and recheck. If the fuse is blown again, check for a short to ground in the wire.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect power tailgate control unit connectors A (5P) and B (20P).
  1. Measure the voltage between body ground and power tailgate control unit connector A (5P) No. 2 terminal, and between body ground and connector B (20P) No. 11 and No. 12 terminals respectively.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
An open in the wire.■
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and power tailgate control unit connector A (5P) No. 1 terminal.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 11 .
NO -
An open in the wire or poor ground (G901).■
  1. Disconnect power tailgate drive unit connector A (2P).
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and power tailgate control unit connector A (5P) No. 3 and No. 5 terminals respectively.
Is there continuity?
YES -
A short to ground in the wire.■
NO -
Go to Step 13 .
  1. Check for continuity between power tailgate control unit connector A (5P) No. 3 and No. 5 terminals and power tailgate drive unit connector A (2P) No. 1 and No. 2 terminals.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 14 .
NO -
An open in the wire.■
  1. Connect battery power to the power tailgate control unit connector A (5P) No. 3 (or No. 5) terminal and ground the No. 5 (or No. 3) terminal.
  1. Check the power tailgate motor operation.
Does the motor run normally?
YES -
Replace the power tailgate control unit. ■
NO -
Replace the power tailgate drive unit. ■
