Door Lock Actuator Test
Driver's Door Without Super Locking
  1. Remove the driver's door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 10P connector (A) from the actuator (B).
  1. Check actuator operation by connecting power and ground according to the table. To prevent damage to the actuator, apply battery voltage only momentarily.
  1. If the actuator does not operate as specified, replace it.
