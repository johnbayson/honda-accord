License Plate Light Replacement
  1. Open the trunk lid, and remove the rear license trim.
  1. Pull the license plate light assembly out, and disconnect the 2P connector (A) from the light.
    License Plate Light:
    5 W

  1. Separate the lens (B) and housing (C), then remove the bulb (D).
  1. Install the license plate light in the reverse order of removal.
