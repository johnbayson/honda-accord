Side Engine Mount Upper Bracket Replacement
  1. Support the engine with a jack and wood block under the oil pan.
  1. Remove the two bolts securing the expansion tank.
  1. Remove the torque rod stiffener (A) and upper torque rod (B).
  1. Remove the side engine mount upper bracket (A).
  1. Install the side engine mount upper bracket, then loosely tighten the new bolts and nut (B), and loosely tighten the bolt (C).
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing assembly.
  1. Loosen the transmission mounting bolt and nuts (A).
  1. Raise the vehicle on the lift to full height.
  1. Remove the engine undercover.
  1. Loosen the lower torque rod mounting bolts (A).
  1. Lower the vehicle on the lift.
  1. Tighten the side engine mount upper bracket mounting bolts and nut.
  1. Tighten the transmission mounting bolt and nuts.
  1. Raise the vehicle on the lift to full height.
  1. Tighten the lower torque rod mounting bolt.
  1. Install the engine undercover.
  1. Lower the vehicle on the lift.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing assembly.
  1. Install the upper torque rod (A) and torque rod stiffener (B), then tighten the new torque rod stiffener mounting bolts (C).
    NOTE: The up mark (D) that installs the torque rod is upturned.
  1. Tighten the new upper torque rod mounting bolts in the numbered sequence shown.
  1. Install the expansion tank.
