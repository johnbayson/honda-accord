Cylinder Head Cover Installation
  1. Thoroughly clean the head cover gasket and groove.
  1. Install the head cover gaskets (A) in the groove of the cylinder head cover (B).
  1. Remove all of the old liquid gasket from the chain case cover mating surfaces, bolts, and bolt holes.
  1. Clean and dry the cylinder head cover mating surfaces.
  1. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M or 08C70-K0334M) to the chain case mating areas and camshaft holder mating areas.
    NOTE:
    • Apply liquid gasket about 2.5 mm (0.098 in.) diameter bead along the broken line (A).
    • Do not install components if 5 minutes or more have passed after applying the liquid gasket. Instead, remove the old residue and reapply the liquid gasket.
  1. Place the cylinder head cover (A) on the cylinder head, then slide the cover slightly back and forth to seat the head cover gaskets.
  1. Inspect the cover washers (B). Replace any washer that is damaged or deteriorated.
  1. Tighten the bolts in three steps. In the final step, tighten all the bolts, in sequence, to 9.8 N·m (1.0 kgf·m, 7.2 lbf·ft).
    NOTE:
    • Wait at least 30 minutes before filling the engine with oil.
    • Do not run the engine for at least 3 hours after installing the head cover.
  1. Secure the A/C line with the clamp (A) and bolts (B).
  1. Install the expansion tank.
  1. Install the four injectors.
  1. Install the breather hose (A), turbocharger boost control solenoid valve mounting bolt and nut (B), and vacuum line mounting bolt (C).
  1. Install the harness clamp (A) and the dipstick (B).
  1. Install the harness clamp bracket mounting bolt (A).
  1. Connect the fuel rail pressure (FRP) sensor connector (B), camshaft position (CMP) sensor connector (C), oil pressure switch connector (D), crankshaft position (CKP) sensor connector (E), and oil level sensor connector (F).
  1. Install the engine cover adapters.
  1. Install the engine cover.
