Torque Converter Housing Secondary Shaft Bearing Replacement
Special Tools Required

Driver handle 15 x 135L
 07749-0010000

Bearing driver attachment 62 x 68 mm
 07746-0010500
  1. Remove the bolt, then remove the lock washer (A) and the bearing set plate (B).
  1. Remove the secondary shaft bearing (A) by heating the torque converter housing to about 100 °C (212 °F) using a heat gun (B). Do no heat the torque converter housing more than 100 °C (212 °F).
    NOTE: Let the torque converter housing cool to room temperature before installing the secondary shaft bearing.
  1. Install new O-rings (A) on the ATF guide collar (B), then install the ATF guide collar in the torque converter housing.
  1. Install a new secondary shaft bearing (C) in the direction shown.
  1. Install the secondary shaft bearing securely in the torque converter housing using the 15 x 135L driver handle and the 62 x 68 mm bearing driver attachment.
  1. Check that the bearing groove aligns with the torque converter housing surface, then install the bearing set plate with aligning the bearing groove.
  1. Install a new lock washer and bolt, then bend the lock tab of the lock washer against the bolt head.
