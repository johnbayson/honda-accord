Fuel Tank Replacement
  1. Drain the fuel tank.
  1. Reinstall the fuel tank unit without connecting the fuel tank unit 4P connector and the quick-connect fittings.
  1. Remove the fuel fill pipe cover.
  1. Disconnect the tubes (A) from the fuel fill pipes (B). Slide back the clamps, then twist the hose as you pull to avoid damaging them.
  1. Lift the vehicle, and support it with jackstands.
  1. Remove the hose (A) from the clamp (B).
  1. Remove the exhaust pipe.
  1. Remove the middle floor undercover.
  1. Disconnect the hose (A) from the EVAP two way valve (B).
  1. Remove the fuel tank protector (C).
  1. Place a jack or other support under the fuel tank (D).
  1. Remove the strap bolts (E) and straps (F).
  1. Remove the fuel tank.
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
    NOTE: When installing the fuel tank protector, make sure to insert it in the same direction as the clip (G).
  1. Disconnect the hose (A) from the EVAP two way valve (B).
  1. Remove the fuel tank protector (C).
  1. Place a jack or other support under the fuel tank (D).
  1. Remove the strap bolts (E) and straps (F).
  1. Remove the fuel tank.
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
    NOTE: When installing the fuel tank protector, make sure to insert it in the same direction as the clip (G).
