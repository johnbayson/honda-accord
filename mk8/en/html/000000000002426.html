ATF Warmer Replacement
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the bolts securing the ATF warmer (A).
  1. Remove the water by-pass hoses (A) and the ATF warmer hoses (B) from the ATF warmer (C), and turn the hose ends up to prevent fluid from flowing out.
  1. Install a new ATF warmer on the warmer bracket.
  1. Install the water by-pass hoses to the ATF warmer, and secure the hoses with the clips.
  1. Install the ATF warmer hoses.
  1. Install the splash shield.
  1. Check the ATF level, and add ATF to proper level if necessary.
  1. Fill the engine cooling system with recommended coolant.
  1. Start the engine in P or N, and warm it up to normal operating temperature (the radiator fan comes on). Turn off the engine, and recheck the ATF level.
