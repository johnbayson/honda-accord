Radiator Replacement
  1. Remove the front grille cover.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Disconnect the engine coolant temperature (ECT) sensor 2 connector (A), then remove the harness clamps (B).
  1. Remove the Intercooler intake pipe mounting bolts.
  1. Clean any dirt off the quick connector (A) and lower radiator hose.
  1. Pull out the lock (B) by hand, then wiggle the quick connector loose, and remove it from the radiator. Do not use any tools to remove the quick connector.
  1. Remove the water bypass hose (C) and radiator drain plug (D).
  1. Disconnect the diesel particulate filter (DPF) differential pressure sensor connector (A) and fan motor connectors (B), then remove the harness clamps (C).
  1. Remove the water bypass line (D) and remove the DPF differential pressure sensor (E).
  1. Disconnect the upper radiator hose (A) and water bypass hoses (B).
  1. Remove the A/C condenser fan shroud assembly (A) and radiator fan shroud assembly (B).
  1. Remove the Intercooler intake pipe mounting bolt.
  1. Remove the harness clamp (A) and the radiator upper brackets (B).
  1. Remove the A/C condenser mounting bolts (C).
  1. Pull up the radiator (A), then remove the ECT sensor 2 (B).
  1. Install the ECT sensor 2 with new O-ring (C).
  1. Install the radiator. Make sure the lower cushions (D) are set securely.
  1. Set the A/C condenser, then tighten the mounting bolts (A).
  1. Install the radiator upper brackets (B), then install the harness clamp (C).
  1. Install the Intercooler intake pipe mounting bolt.
  1. Install the A/C condenser fan shroud assembly (A) and radiator fan shroud assembly (B).
  1. Install the upper radiator hose (A) and water bypass hoses (B).
  1. Install the water bypass line (A) and install the DPF differential pressure sensor (B).
  1. Connect the DPF differential pressure sensor connector (C) and fan motor connectors (D), then remove the harness clamps (E).
  1. Check the quick connector (A) and set ring (B) for cracks or damage. If the connector and/or set ring are cracked or damaged, replace the connector.
  1. Make sure the set ring is in place inside the quick connector. If the set ring is displaced or not properly seated in the connector, replace the quick connector.
  1. Install the new O-ring (C) in the quick connector.
  1. Check the lock (D). If the lock is damaged or deformed, replace it. When installing the new lock on the connector, press it straight down along the groove.
  1. Clean the connecting surfaces of the radiator (E), then apply clean engine coolant around the connecting surfaces.
  1. Push down the lock (A), then push the quick connectors (B) onto the radiator until you hear it click.
  1. Install the washer bypass hoses (C) and radiator drain plug (D) with new O-ring (E).
  1. Install the Intercooler intake pipe mounting bolts.
  1. Connect the ECT sensor 2 connector (A), then install the harness clamps (B).
  1. Install the splash shield.
  1. Install the front grille cover.
  1. Refill the expansion tank with engine coolant, and bleed air from the cooling system with the heater valve open.
