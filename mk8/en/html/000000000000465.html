Power Mirror Actuator Test
  1. Remove the door panel.
  1. Disconnect the power mirror connector A (13P) and connector B (2P).
  1. Check actuator operation by connecting power and ground according to the table.
  1. If the mirror fails to work properly, check for an open in the wire between the connector and the mirror holder. If the wire is OK, replace the mirror actuator.

Defogger Test
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 5 and No. 10 terminals of the connector A (13P). There should be continuity.
  1. If there is no continuity, check for an open in the wire between the connector and the mirror holder. If the wire is OK, replace the mirror holder.
