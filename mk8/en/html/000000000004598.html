Service Reminder System General Information - for applicable EU countries
Service Reminder Information Display
The service reminder system is an important feature of the multi-information display. Based on engine operating conditions, the Accord's onboard computer (ECM/PCM) calculates the remaining engine oil life. The system also shows the service reminder information display along with the code for other scheduled maintenance items needing service.
Service Reminder Information Display
The service reminder system is an important feature of the multi-information display. Based on engine operating conditions, the Accord's onboard computer (ECM/PCM) calculates the remaining engine oil life. The system also shows the service reminder information display along with the code for other scheduled maintenance items needing service.
Applicable Countries
The service reminder system will not be applied for all the EU countries. Confirm the countries where the system is applied. The system is not active in default setting. The system needs to be activated by HDS at Honda dealer. If the vehicle is taken to outside of the applicable countries, apply the maintenance schedule for non-applicable countries.
NOTE: If you turn the Service Reminder System OFF after the first activation, it cannot be reactivated. When one month or more passes after disconnecting battery terminal, adjust the date and time on Service Reminder System using HDS.
How to turn ON/OFF Service Reminder System with HDS
  1. Connect the Honda Diagnostic System (HDS) to the data link connector (DLC).
  1. Turn the ignition swith to ON (II).
  1. Select GAUGES from the BODY ELECTRICAL system select menu with the HDS.
  1. Select ADJUSTMENT, then select SERVICE REMINDER.
  1. Select SETTING, then ON/OFF the service reminder system.
Service Information
  1. The service reminder information display is shown on the multi-information display. When the ignition switch is ON (II), and the remaining days are more than 30 days, the display shows a warning symbol (A), the maintenance item (B), and remaining distance or days (C). To see the service reminder information display, turn the ignition switch to ON (II), then push and release the SEL/RESET switch until the service reminder information display appears.
NOTE:
  • Depending on the customer's driving condition, the remaining distance may not be always in agreement with the mileage in the odometer.
  • The system switches the display between the remaining distance and the remaining days, depending on customer's driving conditions. If the customer does not drive frequently, generally the display shows the remaining days. The system monitors the driving condition, and records the elapsed time and distance from the last maintenance. Based on this data, the system assumes the future driving conditions and selects the appropriate service reminder information display.
  1. When the ignition switch is ON (II), and the remaining days are within 29 to 10 days, the warning symbol (A) or the maintenance service message ‘‘SERVICE DUE SOON'' (B) is displayed. The message indicator (C) remains lit until the warning symbol or the message is canceled. To cancel the message or warning symbol, press the INFO or SEL/RESET switch. The service reminder information display continues to show the warning symbol and the remaining distance or days (D) along with all the maintenance item(s) currently needing service.
    Complete list of maintenance items (E).
    NOTE: The language used in the warning message can be changed into several language. Refer to the Owner's Manual for details.
  1. When the ignition switch is ON (II), and the remaining days are within 9 to 0 day, the warning symbol or the maintenance service message ‘‘SERVICE DUE NOW'' (A) is displayed. The message indicator (B) remains lit until the warning symbol or the message is canceled. To cancel the message or warning symbol, press the INFO or SEL/RESET switch. The service reminder information display continues to show the warning symbol and the remaining distance or days along with all the maintenance item(s) currently needing service.
  1. If the indicated maintenance is not done, the ‘‘SERVICE OVER DUE'' message (A) or warning symbol appears along with total negative distance or days (B), for example ‘‘−10,'' on the display. This means the indicated maintenance item should have been done more than 10 days (or 10 km) ago. The message indicator (C) remains lit until the indicated maintenance is done.
Resetting the Service Reminder Information Display
NOTE:
  • The vehicle must be stopped to reset the display.
  • If a required service is done and the display is not reset, or if the service reminder information display is reset without doing the service the system will not show the proper maintenance timing. This can lead to serious mechanical problems because there will be no accurate record of when the required maintenance is needed.
  • Each maintenance item can be independently reset with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Press the SEL/RESET switch repeatedly until the service reminder information display appears.
  1. Press and hold the SEL/RESET button for about 10 seconds, the service reminder reset display appears.
    NOTE:
    • If you are resetting the display when the remaining days are more than 30 days, make sure any maintenance item(s) requiring service are done before resetting the display.
    • To cancel the reset mode, push the SEL/RESET switch.
  1. To reset the service reminder information display, select reset indicator (A) by pressing the INFO switch, then push the SEL/RESET switch. The maintenance item code(s) will disappear, and the remaining distance or days will also reset.
    The display will return to the service reminder information display, and you will see the next required maintenance item and remaining distance or days.
