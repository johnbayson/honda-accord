Booming sound while driving with audio unit on or off (with premium audio system)
NOTE:
  • Check the vehicle battery condition first.
  • Check the connectors for poor connections or loose terminals.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Operate the audio unit, and check the function of the speakers.

YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -

  • 5-door:

YES -
Go to Step 69 .
NO -
Go to Step 4 .
  1. Turn the audio power switch to off.
  1. Do the active noise cancellation (ANC) self-diagnostic function.
  1. Press the No. 1 button.
Is the low-frequency hum sound heard?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
