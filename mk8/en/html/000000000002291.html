M/T Countershaft Disassembly
NOTE: Refer to the Exploded View in the Countershaft Reassembly, as needed, removing components except pressed in the countershaft.
  1. Securely clamp the countershaft assembly in a bench vise with wood blocks.
  1. Remove the 20 mm special bolt (left-hand threads).
  1. Support 6th gear (A) on the steel blocks (B), then use a press (C) and an attachment (D) to press the countershaft (E) out of the ball bearing and the 6th gear.
  1. Remove the 35 mm shim.
  1. Support 4th gear (A) on the steel blocks (B), then use a press (C) and an attachment (D) to press the countershaft (E) out of 5th gear and 4th gear.
  1. Support 2nd gear (A) on the steel blocks (B), then use a press (C) and an attachment (D) to press the countershaft (E) out of 3rd gear and 2nd gear.
