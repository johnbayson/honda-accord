SRS DTC Troubleshooting: 11-1x
DTC 11-1x (‘‘x'' can be 0, 2 thru 9 or A thru F):
Open in the Driver's Airbag First Inflator (LHD model)
NOTE:
  • Before doing this troubleshooting procedure, review SRS Precautions and Procedures and General Troubleshooting Information.
  • Before replacing the SRS unit, check the SRS unit software version with the HDS. If the software version is not the latest, update the SRS unit software and retest.
  1. Clear the DTC memory.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II), then wait for 10 seconds.
  1. Read the DTC.
Is DTC 11-1x indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Go to Troubleshooting Intermittent Failures. If another DTC is indicated, troubleshoot the DTC.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Do the battery terminal disconnection procedure , then wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Disconnect the driver's airbag 4P connector from the cable reel.
  1. Install a jumper wire between the No. 2 and the No. 3 terminals of the cable reel 4P connector.
  1. Do the battery terminal reconnection procedure.
  1. Read the DTC.
Is DTC 11-1x indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Faulty driver's airbag; replace the driver's airbag. ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Do the battery terminal disconnection procedure , then wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Remove the column cover , then disconnect the dashboard wire harness 4P connector (A) from the cable reel.
  1. Install a jumper wire between the No. 2 and the No. 3 terminals of the dashboard wire harness 4P connector.
  1. Do the battery terminal reconnection procedure.
  1. Read the DTC.
Is DTC 11-1x indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 16 .
NO -
Open in the cable reel; replace the cable reel. ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Do the battery terminal disconnection procedure , then wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Disconnect SRS unit connector A (39P) from the SRS unit.
  1. Install a jumper wire between the No. 3 and the No. 4 terminals of SRS unit connector A (39P).
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 2 and the No. 3 terminals of the dashboard wire harness 4P connector.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Faulty SRS unit or poor connection at SRS unit connector A (39P) and the SRS unit. Check the connection between the connector and the SRS unit. If the connection is OK, replace the SRS unit. ■
NO -
Open in the dashboard wire harness; replace the dashboard wire harness.■
