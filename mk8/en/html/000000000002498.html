A/T Differential Carrier Bearing Replacement
Special Tools Required

Attachment, 45 x 55 mm
 07MAD-PR90100
NOTE:
  • The bearing and the outer race should be replaced as a set.
  • Inspect and adjust the carrier bearing preload whenever the bearing is replaced.
  • Check the bearing for wear and rough rotation. If the bearing is OK, removal is not necessary.
  1. Remove the carrier bearing (A) with a commercially available bearing puller (B), a bearing separator (C), and a spacer (D).
  1. Install new carrier bearings using the 45 x 55 mm attachment and a press.
    NOTE:
    • Press the bearing on until it bottoms.
    • Use the small end of the 45 x 55 mm attachment to install the bearings.
    • Press the bearing on securely so there is no clearance between the bearing and the differential carrier.
