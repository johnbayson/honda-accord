TCM Replacement
NOTE:
  • Make sure the HDS is loaded with the latest software version.
  • If you are replacing the TCM after substituting a known-good TCM, reinstall the original TCM, then do this procedure.
  1. Connect the HDS to the data link connector (DLC) (A) located under the driver's side of the dashboard.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Make sure the HDS communicates with the TCM and other vehicle systems. If it does not, go to the DLC circuit troubleshooting.
  1. Select the A/T system with the HDS.
  1. Select the REPLACE TCM MENU, then select READ DATA, and follow the screen prompts.
    NOTE:
    • Doing this step copies (READS) the ATF life data from the original TCM so you can later download (WRITES) it into the new TCM.
    • If READ DATA indicates FAILED, continue with this procedure.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Remove the strut brace.
  1. RHD model: Remove the nut securing the under-hood fuse/relay box, then reposition the under-hood fuse/relay box.
  1. Disconnect the TCM connector (A).
  1. LHD model: Remove the bolts securing the TCM (B).
  1. RHD model: Remove the harness clamp (C) from the harness bracket (D).
  1. RHD model: Loosen the bolts (E), then remove the TCM assembly (F).
LHD Model
RHD Model
  1. RHD model: Remove the TCM cover (A) and the TCM bracket (B) from the TCM (C).
  1. Install the TCM in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Manually input the VIN to the TCM with the HDS.
    NOTE: DTC P0630 ‘‘VIN Not Programmed or Mismatch'' may be stored because the VIN has not been programmed into the TCM; ignore it, and continue this procedure.
  1. If the READ DATA (ATF life) failed in Step 5 , go to Step 20 . Otherwise go to Step 18 .
  1. Select the A/T SYSTEM with the HDS.
  1. Select the REPLACE TCM MENU, then select WRITE DATA, and follow the screen prompts.
    NOTE: If the WRITE DATA indicates FAILED, continue with this procedure.
  1. If the READ DATA failed in Step 5 or the WRITE DATA failed in Step 19 , replace the ATF, then go to Step 21 .
  1. Select the A/T system, and reset the TCM with the HDS.
  1. Update the TCM if it does not have the latest software.
  1. Do the accelerator pedal kickdown click system learn procedure.
