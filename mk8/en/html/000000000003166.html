Interior Trim Removal/Installation - Rear Side Area
Cargo Area Side Trim Panel
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch trim and panels.
  • When removing components, use the trim tools or equivalent.
  1. For some models: Remove the tonneau cover (A), and if equipped, remove the cargo net (B).
  1. For some models: If necessary, remove the screws, then remove the cargo net holder (A) from the cargo net frame (B), then remove the knob (C) and the spring (D) from the holder by pushing them in.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Tailgate weatherstrip, as needed
    • Rear under floor cover
    • Rear seat cushion
    • Rear seat belt lower anchor
    • Rear seat side bolster
  1. Pull the damper maintenance lid (A) up by hand to release the hooks (B), then remove the lid.
  1. With cargo net: Remove the screws, then remove the cargo net striker (A).
  1. Remove the rear seat belt anchor wire.
  1. Remove the floor side lid (A).
  1. Left side on any model/right side on a model without premium sound system: Remove the cargo area side lid (A).
  1. Pry out the covers (A) and remove the bolts (B), then remove the tie down hooks (C) and the convenience hook (D).
  1. Remove the floor lid clip (A) by pinching its tabs (B).
  1. Remove the cargo area side trim panel (A).
    -1.
    Remove the clip (B) from the front portion of the trim panel.
    -2.
    Pull the upper edge of the trim panel back to release the upper hooks (C) and the tab (D) from the quarter pillar trim (E).
    -3.
    Pull out the trim panel back by hand to detach the clips (F, G, H).
    -4.
    Pull the front portion of the trim panel inward to release it from the sear seat-back striker (I).
    -5.
    Disconnect the cargo area light connector (J) on each side and the cargo area accessory power socket connector (K) on the left side.
    -6.
    Remove the front lower corner of the trim panel from under the rear seat-back.
    -7.
    Carefully move the trim panel inward, and remove it.
  1. Remove the bolts (A, B) and detach the connector clip (C), then remove the front lid support (D) and the rear lid support (E).
  1. Install the trim panel in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, replace them with new ones.
    • Make sure the cargo area light connector and the cargo area accessory power socket connector are plugged in properly.
    • Push the clips and the hooks into place securely.
Rear Wheelhouse Insulator - For Some Models
NOTE: Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the cargo area side trim panel.
  1. Detach the clips (A) and the harness holder (B) from the stud bolts, and detach the side wire harness clips (C), then remove the rear wheelhouse insulator (D).
  1. Install the insulator in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, replace them with new ones.
    • Push the clips and the harness holder into place securely.
