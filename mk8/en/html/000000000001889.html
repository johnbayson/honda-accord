Camshaft Replacement
Removal
NOTE: Keep the cam chain away from magnetic fields.
  1. Remove the cylinder head cover.
  1. Remove the cam chain sprocket.
  1. Remove the intake camshaft gear.
  1. Remove the fuel pump.
  1. Remove the camshaft holder bolts. To prevent damaging the camshafts, unscrew the bolts, in sequence, two turns at a time.
  1. Remove the upper camshaft holders and camshafts.
Installation
NOTE: Keep the cam chain away from magnetic fields.
  1. Make sure the keyways on the intake camshaft and the exhaust camshaft are facing up, then set the camshafts on the lower camshaft holder.
  1. Position the camshaft journal marks correctly, and position the arrows to face the cam chain side, then install the upper camshaft holders as shown illustration.
  1. Tighten the bolts in sequence to the specified torque.
    Specified Torque:
    6 x 1.0 mm
    12 N·m (1.2 kgf·m, 8.8 lbf·ft)

  1. Install the fuel pump.
  1. Install the intake camshaft gear.
  1. Install the cam chain sprocket.
  1. Install the cylinder head cover.
