Rear Seat-back Latch Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  1. Remove the rear seat-back.
  1. Remove the seat-back cover/pad from the seat-back frame.
  1. Remove the bolts (A, B). On the left rear seat-back only, remove the seat-back latch patch (C) from the seat-back frame (D).
Left rear seat-back
Right rear seat-back
  1. Release the pivot pins (A) of the latch knob (B) from the seat-back frame (C). The left rear seat-back is shown; the right rear seat-back is similar.
  1. Remove the seat-back latch (A) from the seat-back frame (B). The left rear seat-back is shown; the right rear seat-back is similar.
  1. Install the latch in the reverse order of removal. Make sure the seat-back locks securely and unlocks properly.
