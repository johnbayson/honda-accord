DTC Troubleshooting: U0107 (207)
DTC U0107:
 CAN Malfunction (ECM-ISV Actuator Control Module)
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and review the general troubleshooting information.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0107 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the intake shutter valve and the ECM.■
  1. Check the No. 18 ISV (15 A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Go to Step 6 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect the intake shutter valve 10P connector.
  1. Remove the No. 18 ISV (15 A) fuse from the under-hood fuse/relay box.
  1. Check for continuity between intake shutter valve 10P connector terminal No. 8 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the intake shutter valve and the under-hood fuse/relay box. Also replace the No. 18 ISV (ISA) fuse, then go to Step 34 .
NO -
Replace the No. 18 ISV (15 A) fuse, then go to Step 34 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect the intake shutter valve 10P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II), and measure the voltage between intake shutter valve 10P connector terminal No. 8 and body ground within 1 second.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 23 .
NO -
Go to Step 13 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Check the ISV control relay circuit.
Is the ISV control relay circuit OK?
YES -
Go to Step 15 .
NO -
Replace the under-hood fuse/relay box, then go to Step 34 .
  1. Disconnect under-hood fuse/relay box connector C (10P).
  1. Check for continuity between intake shutter valve 10P connector terminal No. 8 and under-hood fuse/relay box connector C (10P) terminal No. 4.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 17 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the intake shutter valve and the under-hood fuse/relay box, then go to Step 34 .
  1. Disconnect under-hood fuse/relay box connector B (14P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage under-hood fuse/relay box connector B (14P) terminal No. 8 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 20 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the ISV control relay circuit (IGP2 line) and PGM-FI main relay 2 (SOL), then go to Step 33 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and wait 2 minutes.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 2 (58P).
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 29 and under-hood fuse/relay box connector B (14P) terminal No. 13.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 39 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the under-hood fuse/relay box and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 29, then go to Step 34 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and intake shutter valve 10P connector terminals No. 4 and No. 9 individually.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 25 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the intake shutter valve and G101, then go to Step 34 .
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 1 (96P).
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 15 and intake shutter valve 10P connector No. 6.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 27 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the intake shutter valve and ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 15, then go to Step 34 .
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 16 and intake shutter valve 10P connector No. 1.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 28 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the intake shutter valve and ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 16, then go to Step 34 .
  1. Substitute a known-good intake shutter valve.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0107 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 40 .
NO -
Replace the original intake shutter valve, then go to Step 33 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0107 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the intake shutter valve and the ECM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0107 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the intake shutter valve and the ECM. If the ECM was updated, substitute a known-good ECM, then recheck. If the ECM was substituted, go to Step 1 .
NO -
If the ECM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the ECM was substituted, replace the original ECM. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
