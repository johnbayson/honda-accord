  1. Install a new O-ring (A) the torque converter (B), then install the torque converter on the mainshaft (C).
  1. Install the dowel pins (D) in the torque converter housing.
  1. Place the transmission on a jack, and raise the transmission to the engine level, then fit the transmission to the engine.
  1. Install the lower transmission mounting bolts (A) (B) and the harness bracket (C).
Front side
Rear side
  1. Install the upper transmission housing mounting bolts.
  1. Install new set ring (A) on the intermediate shaft (B).
  1. Clean the areas where the intermediate shaft contacts the transmission (differential) with solvent, and dry with compressed air. Apply ATF to the intermediate shaft splines (C), then install the intermediate shaft. Be sure not to allow dust or other foreign particles to enter the transmission.
  1. Install new set ring (A) on the left driveshaft (B).
  1. Clean the areas where the left driveshaft contacts the transmission (differential) with solvent, and dry with compressed air. Then install the left driveshaft. Be sure not to allow dust or other foreign particles to enter the transmission. Turn the steering knuckle fully outward, and slide the driveshaft into the differential until you feel its set ring fully engage the side gear.
  1. Apply the recommended grease to the right driveshaft inboard-joint splines.
  1. Slide the right driveshaft over the intermediate shaft splines until you feel the driveshaft fully engage the intermediate shaft set ring.
  1. Support the front subframe with a wood piece and a jack, and lift it up to the body.
  1. Position the steering gearbox washers (A) on the front subframe (B), and lift up the front subframe.
  1. Loosely install new four front subframe mounting bolts (C), the front stiffener mounting bolts (D) and new rear stiffener mounting bolts (E).
  1. Loosely tighten the subframe mounting bolt (A) in the right rear stiffener; insert the subframe alignment pin through the positioning slot (B) on the right rear stiffener, through the positioning hole (C) on the front subframe, and into the positioning hole on the body, then tighten the front subframe mounting bolt until the front subframe insulator contacts the body.
  1. Loosely tighten the front subframe mounting bolt in the left rear stiffener in the same manner in Step 15 .
  1. Reinsert the subframe alignment pin through the positioning slot on the right rear stiffener, through the positioning hole on the front subframe, and into the positioning hole on the body, then tighten the front subframe mounting bolt to the specified torque.
  1. Tighten the subframe mounting bolt in the left rear stiffener in the same manner in Step 17 .
  1. Tighten the right and left front subframe mounting bolts (A) to the specified torque.
  1. Check that the positioning holes and slots are aligned using the subframe alignment pin.
  1. Tighten the rear and front stiffener mounting bolts to the specified torque.
  1. Remove the jack.
  1. Remove both front subframe mid-mount mounting bolts (A), and replace it.
  1. Tighten both front subframe mid-mount mounting bolts.
  1. Install the new lower torque rod connecting bolts (A) to the bracket on the engine block.
  1. Install the catalytic converter mounting bracket (A).
  1. Attach the torque converter to the drive plate with the eight bolts (A). Rotate the crankshaft pulley as necessary to tighten the bolts to 1/2 of the specified torque, then to the final torque, in a crisscross pattern. After tightening the last bolt, check that the crankshaft rotates freely.
  1. Install the torque converter cover (B).
  1. Connect the ground cable connector at the front of the front subframe.
  1. Connect the damper forks (A) and the lower arms (B) with new damper fork mounting bolts (C), then loosely tighten new damper fork mounting nuts (D).
  1. Connect the knuckle ball joints (E) on the lower arms. Loosely tighten a new castle nuts (F).
    NOTE:
    • For headlight leveling system model: Be careful not to damage the suspension stroke sensor arm when connecting the knuckle ball joint from the left side lower arm.
    • Do not install the cotter pin (G) onto the castle nut until the castle nut tightened to the specified torque.
  1. Install the exhaust pipe bracket (B) on the exhaust pipe A using new self-locking nuts (C) (2).
  1. Install the exhaust pipe A, the pipe joint (D) using the bolts, new self-locking nuts (E) (9), and new gasket (F).
  1. Install the harness clamps (A) on the harness brackets (B).
  1. Install new ATF warmer (A) on the warmer bracket (B).
  1. Install the suspension stroke sensor if equipped.
  1. Install the harness holder (A) with the bolt.
  1. Connect the 4th clutch transmission fluid pressure switch connector (B).
  1. Position the transmission mount (A), and loosely install new transmission mount mounting bolts (B) to the body frame.
  1. Loosely install new transmission mount bracket mounting bolts (A) and nuts (B) to the transmission housing.
  1. Install the ground cable (C) to the transmission mount bracket (D).
  1. Remove the engine support hanger.
  1. Remove the subhanger stay (A) from the lower camshaft holder.
  1. Set the steering gearbox into position, then loosely install the front side steering gearbox mounting bolts (A) with the washers (B).
