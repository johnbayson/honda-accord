EPS DTC Troubleshooting: 21-01, 21-02
DTC 21-01:
 Excessive Change of the Vehicle Speed Signal (Regular Diagnosis)
DTC 21-02:
 Comparison between the Vehicle Speed and the Engine Speed Signal (Regular Diagnosis)
NOTE:
  • If the D indicator to brink, troubleshoot the A/T SYSTEM.
  • Even though the system is operating normally, the EPS indicator will come on caused by the detecting condition of DTC 21-01 or DTC 21-02.
  • Clear the DTC related to the VSA.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Raise the vehicle, and support it with safety stands in the proper locations, and allow all wheels to rotate freely.
  1. Connect the HDS to the data link connector (DLC).
  1. Turn OFF the VSA function by using the VSA OFF switch.
  1. Start the engine, then run the vehicle in D position.
  1. Check the VEHICLE SPEED in the EPS DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is the vehicle speed indicated?
YES -
The system is OK at this time.■
NO -
Go to Step 7 .
  1. Check for DTCs in the A/T SYSTEM with the HDS.
Is DTC P0721, P0722 or P0723 indicated?
YES -
Troubleshoot the indicated DTC.■
NO -
Go to Step 8 .
  1. Start the engine and check the tachometer.

Go to Step 10 .

  1. Check for DTCs in the gauge control module with the HDS.
