Intake Air Resonator Removal/Installation
  1. Do the battery removal procedure.
  1. Remove the air cleaner.
  1. Remove the air intake cover (A), the intake air ducts (B).
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. Remove the left front wheel.
  1. Remove the clips (A), then strip off the inner fender (B).
  1. Remove the intake air resonator (A).
    NOTE: When installing the resonator, use the new the new seal ring (B).
  1. Disconnect ECM/PCM connectors A, B, and C, then remove the ECM/PCM assembly (D).
    NOTE: ECM/PCM connectors A, B, and C have symbols (A=□, B=△, C=○) embossed on them for identification.
  1. Remove the battery base (A).
  1. Remove the intake air duct (A).
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Do the battery installation procedure.
