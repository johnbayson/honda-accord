M/T Clutch Housing Countershaft Bearing Replacement
Special Tools Required

Oil seal driver, 65 mm
 07JAD-PL90100

Adjustable bearing puller, 20−40 mm
 07JAC-PH80100

Bearing remover shaft
 07JAC-PH80200

Slide hammer weight
 07741-0010201
  1. Remove the bearing set plate (A) from the clutch housing (B).
  1. Remove the needle bearing (A) using the 20−40 mm adjustable bearing puller (B), slide hammer weight (D) and bearing remover shaft (E), then remove oil guide plate C.
  1. Position oil guide plate C and the new needle bearing (A) in the bore of the clutch housing (B).
  1. Install the needle bearing using the 65 mm oil seal driver (D).
  1. Install the bearing set plate (A) with bolts (B).
