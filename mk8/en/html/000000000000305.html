Front Door Sash Inner Trim Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the door.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Tweeter cover
    • Door panel
  1. Remove the front door sash inner trim (A).
    -1.
    Pull back along the outside edge of the trim to release the hooks (B) from the door glass opening flange at the center pillar portion of the door sash.
    -2.
    Release the hook (C) from the glass run channel (D) at the rear corner portion of the door sash.
    -3.
    Pull back along the outside edge of the trim to release the hooks (E) from the door glass opening flange at the roof portion and the front pillar portion of the door sash.
  1. Hold the trim up, and align the corner portion of the trim with the rear corner portion of the door sash, then set the hook of the trim to the glass run channel. Then set the hooks of the trim along the trim at the center pillar portion, the roof portion, and the front pillar portion of the door sash.
