A/T Assembly Removal
Special Tools Required

Engine support hanger
 AAR-T1256-J00
*

Subhanger stay
 07MAK-PY30100
*: This special tool is available from Snap-on Tools    International, LLC.
NOTE:
  • Use fender covers to avoid damaging painted surfaces.
  • To avoid damaging the wires and the terminals, unplug the wiring connectors carefully while holding the connector portion.
  • Mark all wiring and hoses to avoid misconnection. Also, be sure that they do not contact other wiring or hoses, or interfere with other parts.
  • Engine support hanger (AAR-T1256-J00) must be used with the side engine mount installed.
  1. Secure the hood in the wide open position with the support strut.
  1. Do the battery removal procedure.
  1. Remove the connector cover, harness clamps, and disconnect the engine control module (ECM) connectors, then remove the ECM/bracket assembly.
  1. Remove the front grille cover.
  1. Remove the brake booster vacuum hose clamp (A), the auxiliary under-hood fuse/relay box (LHD model) (B), and the strut brace mounting nuts (C), then remove the strut brace (D).
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing and the intake air duct.
  1. Remove the two bolts (A) and loosen the two bolts (B), then remove the battery base (C).
  1. Raise the vehicle on a lift, and make sure it is securely supported.
  1. Remove the front wheels.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Remove the drain plug (A), and drain the automatic transmission fluid (ATF).
  1. Reinstall the drain plug with a new sealing washer (B).
  1. Remove the nuts securing the shift cable bracket (A).
  1. Remove the spring clip (B) and the control pin (C), then separate the shift cable end (D) from the selector control lever (E).
  1. Check the bushing (F) in the shift cable end for proper fit and wear. If the bushing is loose or worn, replace the shift cable.
  1. Remove the harness clamp (A) from the clamp bracket (B).
  1. Disconnect the A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve C connector (A), then remove the harness clamp (B) from the clamp bracket (C).
  1. Disconnect these connectors:
    • A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve A connector (A)
    • A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve B connector (B)
    • Shift solenoid wire harness connector (C)
  1. Remove the transmission ground cable (D).
  1. Remove the bolt securing the harness holder (E).
  1. Disconnect these connectors:
    • Input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor connector (A)
    • Transmission range switch connector (B)
  1. Disconnect these connectors:
    • 3rd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch connector (A)
    • Output shaft (countershaft) speed sensor connector (B)
    • ATF temperature sensor connector (C)
  1. Remove the harness clamp (D) from the clamp bracket (E).
  1. Remove the heater hose (A) from the hose clamp (B), then remove the bolt securing the clamp bracket.
  1. Remove the air intake passenge.
  1. Disconnect the 2nd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch connector (A), then remove the harness clamp (B) from the harness bracket (C).
  1. Remove the bolt securing the ATF cooler hose clamp bracket.
  1. Remove the bolts securing the ATF cooler pipe brackets.
  1. Remove the ATF cooler hoses (A) from the ATF cooler lines (B). Turn the ends of the cooler hoses up to prevent ATF from flowing out, then plug the cooler hoses and lines.
  1. Check for any signs of leakage at the hose joints.
  1. Remove the harness clamp (A) from the harness bracket (B), and remove the air cleaner bracket (C).
  1. Remove the front side steering gearbox mounting bolts (A) and the washers (B).
Driver's side
Passenger's side
  1. Remove the rear side steering gearbox mounting bolts (A), the stiffener plate mounting bolt (B), and the stiffener plate (C).
    NOTE: The washers (D) are on the bottom of the steering gearbox mounts.
  1. Hang up both sides of the steering gearbox end securely to prevent damage the EPS subharness.
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Attach the subhanger stay (A) to the threaded hole in the lower camshaft holder using a 10 x 1.25 mm bolt (B).
  1. Install the engine support hanger (AAR-T1256-J00) to the vehicle, and attach the hook (A) to the subhanger stay (B). Tighten the wing nut (C) by hand, and lift and support the engine. Take care not to damage the hood opener cable (D).
  1. Remove the ground cable (A) from the transmission mount bracket (B).
  1. Remove the transmission mount bracket mounting bolts and nuts from the transmission housing.
  1. Remove the transmission mount mounting bolts from the body frame, then remove the transmission mount (A).
    NOTE: Do not remove the TORX bolt (B) from the transmission mount. If the TORX bolt is removed, the transmission mount must be replaced as an assembly.
  1. Disconnect the 4th clutch transmission fluid pressure switch connector (A).
  1. Remove the bolt securing the harness holder (B).
  1. Remove the bolts securing the ATF warmer (A) from the warmer bracket (B).
  1. Remove the harness clamps (A) from the harness brackets (B).
  1. Remove the self-locking nuts (B) (11)
  1. Remove the exhaust pipe A, the pipe joint (C), and the gaskets (D).
  1. Remove the exhaust pipe bracket (E).
  1. Remove the damper fork mounting nuts (A) while holding the damper fork mounting bolts (B), then remove the damper fork mounting bolts.
    NOTE: Be careful not to damage the suspension stroke sensor arm.
  1. Remove the cotter pins (C), then remove the castle nuts (D).
  1. Separate the knuckle ball joints from the lower arms (E).
  1. Disconnect the ground cable connector at the front of the front subframe.
  1. Remove the torque converter cover (A), and remove the drive plate bolts (B) (8) while rotating the crankshaft pulley.
  1. Remove the catalytic converter mounting bracket (A).
  1. Remove the suspension stroke sensor if equipped.
  1. Loosen both front subframe mid-mount bolts (A).
  1. Remove the lower torque rod connecting bolts (A).
  1. Support the front subframe with a wood piece and a jack.
  1. Remove the four front subframe mounting bolts securing the stiffeners (A), and remove the six stiffener mounting bolts securing the front subframe (B), and lower the front subframe.
    NOTE:
    • Check that the front subframe is free of the vacuum hose, the ATF cooler hoses, ATF warmer, ATF warmer hoses, ATF cooler pipe and electrical wiring.
    • Pick up the steering gearbox washers (C).
  1. Remove the left side driveshaft from the differential and the right side driveshaft from the intermediate shaft. Coat all precision machined surfaces with clean engine oil, then put plastic bags over the driveshaft ends. Hang the driveshaft to the body with a strap.
  1. Remove the intermediate shaft. Coat all precision machined surfaces with clean engine oil, then put plastic bags over the intermediate shaft ends.
  1. Remove the upper transmission housing mounting bolt.
  1. Securely support the transmission with a jack.
  1. Remove the lower transmission mounting bolts and the harness bracket (A).
  1. Slide the transmission away from the engine to remove it from the vehicle.

Rear side
  1. Remove the torque converter, the O-ring, and the dowel pins.
  1. Inspetion the drive plate, and replace it if it is damaged.
