DTC Troubleshooting: B1380
DTC B1380:
 Power Release Latch Gear Position Switch Open/Neutral Input Circuit Malfunction
  1. Open the tailgate with the tailgate outer handle.
  1. Close the tailgate manually to the half latch position.
  1. Open the tailgate with the tailgate outer handle.
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC B1380 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for loose or poor connections.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect power tailgate control unit connectors B (20P) and C (14P).
  1. Connect the battery power to power tailgate control unit connector B (20P) No. 9 terminal and ground the No. 20 terminal momentarily. (The motor should run in the release direction.)
  1. Check for continuity between power tailgate control unit connector C (14P) No. 3 and No. 11 terminals.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Go to Step 14 .
  1. Connect the battery power to power tailgate control unit connector B (20P) No. 20 terminal and ground the No. 9 terminal momentarily. Do the next step while the motor is running. (The motor should run in the close direction.)
  1. While the closer motor running, check for continuity between power tailgate control unit connector C (14P) No. 3 and No. 11 terminals.
Is there continuity varied shown? Continuity → No continuity → Continuity
YES -
Replace the power tailgate control unit. ■
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Disconnect power tailgate closer unit connector B (6P).
  1. Check for continuity between power tailgate control unit connector C (14P) No. 3 terminal and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
A short to ground in the wire.■
NO -
Replace the power tailgate closer unit.■
  1. Disconnect power tailgate closer unit connector B (6P).
  1. Check for continuity between power tailgate control unit connector C (14P) No. 3 terminal and power tailgate closer unit connector B (6P) No. 5 terminal.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Replace the power tailgate closer unit.■
NO -
An open in the wire.■
