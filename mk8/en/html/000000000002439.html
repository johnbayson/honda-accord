A/T Housing ATF Filter Removal/Inspection/Installation
  1. Remove the three 6.0 mm bolts securing the ATF filter cover (A) and the ATF pipe (B).
  1. Remove the ATF pipe from the ATF filter cover, and remove the ATF filter (C) from the cover.
  1. Clean the ATF filter, then check that it is in good condition, and is not clogged. Replace the ATF filter if it is clogged or damaged.
  1. Install the ATF filter with a new O-ring (D) in the filter cover, and install the ATF pipe in the cover, then install them in the transmission housing.
  1. Secure the ATF filter cover with the two bolts (E), then secure the ATF pipe with the bolt (F).
