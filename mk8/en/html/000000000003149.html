Roof Molding Replacement
For Some Models
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the body.
  • When removing components, use the trim tools or equivalent.
  1. Remove the roof rail and the center bracket.
  1. Starting at the rear edge of each roof molding, pull up the front roof molding (A), the center roof molding (B), and the quarter roof molding (C). Slide the moldings rearward, and pull up the moldings to release the brackets (D) from the pins (E), then remove the moldings from body. Front roof molding: Remove the molding from under the windshield molding (F).
  1. Install the molding in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Front roof molding: Take care not to damage the windshield molding.
    • Make sure the roof molding is installed securely.
