A/T Clutch Pressure Control Solenoid Valve A and B Replacement
  1. Do the battery removal procedure.
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing and the intake air duct.
  1. Remove the battery base.
  1. Remove the nuts securing the shift cable bracket (A).
  1. Remove the spring clip (B) and the control pin (C), then separate the shift cable end (D) from the selector control lever (E).
  1. Check the bushing (F) in the shift cable end for proper fit and wear. If the bushing is loose or worn, replace the shift cable.
  1. Disconnect the A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve C connector (A), then remove the harness clamp (B) from the clamp bracket (C).
  1. Disconnect these connectors:
    • A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve A connector (A)
    • A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve B connector (B)
    • Shift solenoid wire harness connector (C)
  1. Remove the transmission ground cable (D).
  1. Remove the bolt securing the harness cover (E).
  1. Disconnect these connectors:
    • Input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor connector (A)
    • Transmission range switch connector (B)
  1. Disconnect these connectors:
    • 3rd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch connector (A)
    • Output shaft (countershaft) speed sensor connector (B)
    • ATF temperature sensor connector (C)
  1. Remove the harness clamp (D) from the clamp bracket (E).
  1. Remove A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valves A and B, the ATF pipes (C), the gasket (D), and the O-rings (E).
  1. Check the fluid passage of the A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve for dust or dirt, and clean the passage.
  1. Install a new solenoid valve body gasket on the solenoid valve cover, and install the ATF pipes with the filter end in the transmission housing. Install new O-rings over the ATF pipes.
  1. Install A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valves A and B.
  1. Install the harness cover on its bracket, and secure it with the bolt.
  1. Check the connectors for rust, dirt, or oil, clean or repair if necessary, then connect the connectors securely.
  1. Apply molybdenum grease to the hole in the bushing in the shift cable end. Attach the shift cable end to the selector control lever, then insert the control pin into the selector control lever hole through the shift cable end, and secure the control pin with the spring clip.
  1. Secure the shift cable bracket with the nuts.
  1. Install the battery base.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing and the intake air duct.
  1. Do the battery installation procedure.
