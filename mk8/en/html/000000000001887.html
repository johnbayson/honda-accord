Cylinder Head Cover Removal
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Remove the engine cover adapters.
  1. Disconnect the fuel rail pressure (FRP) sensor connector (A), camshaft position (CMP) sensor connector (B), oil pressure switch connector (C), crankshaft position (CKP) sensor connector (D), and oil level sensor connector (E).
  1. Remove the harness clamp bracket mounting bolt (F).
  1. Remove the harness clamp (A) and the dipstick (B).
  1. Remove the breather hose (A), turbocharger boost control solenoid valve mounting bolt and nut (B), and vacuum line mounting bolt (C).
  1. Remove the four injectors.
  1. Remove the two bolts securing the expansion tank.
  1. Remove the A/C line mounting bolts (A) and unclamp the A/C line from the clamp (B).
  1. Remove the cylinder head cover.
