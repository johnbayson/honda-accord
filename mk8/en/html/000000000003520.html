DTC Troubleshooting: B1383
DTC B1383:
 Tailgate Lock Ratchet Switch Input Circuit Malfunction
  1. Open the tailgate with the tailgate outer handle.
  1. Close the tailgate manually to the half latch position.
  1. Open the tailgate with the tailgate outer handle.
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC B1383 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for loose or poor connections.■
  1. Select POWER TAILGATE from the BODY ELECTRICAL system select menu, and enter the DATA LIST.
  1. Check the ON/OFF information of the TAILGATE LOCK SWITCH RATCHET with the DATA LIST.
Is the information indicator OFF?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Go to Step 9 .
  1. Turn the tailgate latch to half latch position using a screwdriver and check the ON/OFF information of the TAILGATE LOCK SWITCH RATCHET with the DATA LIST, then turn the tailgate latch to full latch position using a screwdriver and check the ON/OFF information of the TAILGATE LOCK SWITCH RATCHET with the DATA LIST.
Are both information indicators ON?
YES -
Do the steps Step 9 to 15, and Step 16 to 22.
NO -
Go to Step 16 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Close the tailgate.
  1. Open the tailgate with the tailgate outer handle.
  1. Disconnect power tailgate control unit connector C (14P).
  1. Check for continuity between power tailgate control unit connector C (14P) No. 10 and No. 11 terminals.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 14 .
NO -
Replace the power tailgate control unit. ■
  1. Disconnect power tailgate closer unit connector B (6P).
  1. Check for continuity between power tailgate closer unit connector B (6P) No. 4 terminal and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
A short to ground in the wire.■
NO -

  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Close the tailgate.
  1. Open the tailgate with the tailgate outer handle.
  1. Disconnect power tailgate control unit connector C (14P).
  1. Turn the tailgate latch to the half latch position using a screwdriver and check for continuity between power tailgate control unit connector C (14P) No. 1 and No. 11 terminals, then turn the tailgate latch to the full latch position using a screwdriver and check for continuity between power tailgate control unit connector C (14P) No. 1 and No. 11 terminals.
Is there continuity in each condition?
YES -
Replace the power tailgate control unit. ■
