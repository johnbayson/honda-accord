DTC Troubleshooting: B1387
DTC B1387:
 Power Tailgate Drive Motor B-phase Pulse Signal Input Error
  1. Open the tailgate with the tailgate outer handle.
  1. Close the tailgate slowly to the half latch position.
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC B1387 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for loose or poor connections.■
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Measure the voltage between power tailgate control unit connector B (20P) No. 15 and No. 18 terminals while the power tailgate is opening and closing manually.
Does it alternate between about 0 V and 5 V? NOTE: Digital voltmeter will read about 2.5 V.
YES -
Replace the power tailgate control unit. ■
NO -
Go to Step 7 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect power tailgate control unit connectors B (20P) and C (14P).
  1. Disconnect power tailgate drive unit connector B (8P).
  1. Check for continuity between power tailgate control unit connector B (20P) No. 15 and No. 18 terminals and power tailgate drive unit connector B (8P) No. 4 and No. 6 terminals.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 11 .
NO -
An open in the wire.■
  1. Check for continuity between power tailgate control unit connector C (14P) No. 13 terminal and power tailgate drive unit connector B (8P) No. 8 terminal.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 12 .
NO -
An open in the wire.■
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and power tailgate drive unit connector B (8P) No. 6 and No. 8 terminals respectively.
Is there continuity?
YES -
A short to ground in the wire.■
NO -
Go to Step 13 .
  1. Reconnect power tailgate control unit connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between power tailgate control unit connector C (14P) No. 13 terminal and connector B (20P) No. 18 terminal.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Replace the power tailgate control unit. ■
NO -
Go to Step 16 .
  1. Measure the voltage between the power tailgate control unit connector B (20P) No. 15 and No. 18 terminals.
Is there about 5 V?
YES -
Replace the power tailgate drive unit. ■
NO -
Replace the power tailgate control unit. ■
