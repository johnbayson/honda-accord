Alternator Removal and Installation
Removal
  1. Do the battery terminal disconnection procedure.
  1. Remove the drive belt.
  1. Disconnect the diesel particulate filter (DPF) differential pressure sensor connector (A), and remove the DPF differential pressure sensor (B) from the bracket.
  1. Remove the bolts (A) securing the water bypass line.
  1. Disconnect the A/C condenser fan motor connector (B), then remove the A/C condenser fan/shroud assembly (C).
  1. Remove the alternator cover.
  1. Disconnect the alternator connector (A), BLK wire (B), and A/C compressor clutch connector (C).
  1. Remove the 6 mm bolt (A) and 8 mm bolts (B), then remove the alternator.
Installation
  1. Install the alternator.
  1. Connect the alternator connector (A), BLK wire (B), and A/C compressor clutch connector (C).
  1. Install the alternator cover.
  1. Install the A/C condenser fan/shroud assembly (A), then connect the A/C condenser fan motor connector (B).
  1. Install the bolts (C) securing the water bypass line.
  1. Install the DPF differential pressure sensor (A), then connect the DPF differential pressure sensor connector (B).
  1. Install the drive belt.
  1. Do the battery terminal reconnection procedure.
