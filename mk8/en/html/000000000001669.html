Starter Removal and Installation
Removal
  1. Do the battery terminal disconnection procedure.
  1. Remove the intake manifold.
  1. Disconnect the starter cable (A) from the B terminal, and disconnect the BLK/WHT wire (B) from the solenoid S terminal.
  1. Remove the harness clamp (C).
  1. Remove the two bolts (A) securing the starter, then remove the starter from the engine.
Installation
  1. Install the starter, then tighten the two bolts.
  1. Install the harness clamp (A).
  1. Install the starter cable (B) to the B terminal and connect the BLK/WHT wire (C) to the solenoid S terminal. Make sure the crimped side of the ring terminal faces away from the starter when you connect it.
  1. Install the intake manifold.
  1. Do the battery terminal reconnection procedure.
  1. Start the engine to make sure the starter works properly.
