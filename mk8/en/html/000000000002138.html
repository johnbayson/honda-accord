DTC Troubleshooting: P104A (71), P104B (72), P104C (73), P104D (74)
DTC P104A:
 No. 1 Injector System Malfunction
DTC P104B:
 No. 2 Injector System Malfunction
DTC P104C:
 No. 3 Injector System Malfunction
DTC P104D:
 No. 4 Injector System Malfunction
NOTE:
  • Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data, and review the general troubleshooting information.
  • If any of DTCs listed below are indicated at the same time as DTC P104A, P104B, P104C, and/or P104D, troubleshoot those DTCs first, then recheck for DTC P104A, P104B, P104C, and/or P104D.
    DTC P0201, P0202, P0203, P0204: No. 1−No. 4 cylinder injector circuit open
    DTC P1041, DTC P1042, DTC P1043, DTC P1044: No. 1−No. 4 cylinder injector circuit shorted
    DTC P2146: No. 1, No. 2, No. 3, or No. 4 cylinder injector circuit malfunction
