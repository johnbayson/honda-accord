SRS DTC Troubleshooting: 45-2x, 45-8x, 45-Bx, 46-2x, 46-8x, 46-Bx
DTC 45-2x, 45-8x, 45-Bx (‘‘x'' can be 0 thru 9 or A thru F):
 Internal Failure of the Left Side Impact Sensor (second)
DTC 46-2x, 46-8x, 46-Bx (‘‘x'' can be 0 thru 9 or A thru F):
 Internal Failure of the Right Side Impact Sensor (second)
NOTE: Before doing this troubleshooting procedure, review SRS Precautions and Procedures and General Troubleshooting Information.
  1. Clear the DTC memory.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II), then wait for 10 seconds.
  1. Read the DTC.
Is DTC 45-2x, 45-8x, 45-Bx, 46-2x, 46-8x, or 46-Bx indicated?
YES -
Faulty left or right side impact sensor (second); replace the left or right side impact sensor (second). ■
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Go to Troubleshooting Intermittent Failures. If another DTC is indicated, troubleshoot the DTC.
