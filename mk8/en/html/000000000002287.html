M/T Assembly Disassembly

Exploded View - Clutch Housing

Exploded View - Transmission Housing
NOTE: Place the clutch housing on two pieces of wood thick enough to keep the mainshaft from hitting the workbench.
  1. Remove the release bearing and the release fork.
  1. Remove the strainer cover (A), the O-ring (B), the strainer set spring (C), and the oil pump strainer (D).
  1. Remove the reverse lockout solenoid (A) and the back-up light switch (B).
  1. Remove the detent bolts (A), the 22.0 mm spring (B), the 26.1 mm spring (C), the 27.4 mm springs (D), the steel balls (E).
  1. Remove the oil filler plug (F) and the 20 mm sealing washer (G).
  1. Remove the detent bolts (A), the 22.0 mm spring (B), the 27.4 mm spring (C), the steel balls (D).
  1. Remove the output shaft (countershaft) speed sensor (E), the plain washer (F), and the O-ring (G).
  1. Remove the 6 mm fluid check bolt (A), the 6 mm sealing washer (B), the drain plug (C), and the 14 mm sealing washer (D).
  1. Loosen the 10 mm flange bolts in a crisscross pattern in several steps, then remove them with transmission hanger A and transmission hanger B.
  1. Remove the transmission case cap (A) and the O-ring (B).
  1. While expanding the 75 mm snap ring (C) of the countershaft ball bearing with snap ring pliers, lift the transmission housing (D). Release the snap ring pliers, and remove the transmission housing and the three 14 x 20 mm dowel pins (E).
  1. Remove oil guide pipe A, the oil guide pipe (B), and the magnet (C).
  1. Remove the 8 x 50 mm pin (A) and the reverse shift lever (B).
  1. Slide up the reverse idler gear shaft (A) slightly, and remove the 20 mm thrust washers (B), the 20 mm thrust needle bearings (C), the reverse idler gear shaft assembly (D), the 33 mm spring washer (E), and the reverse shift fork (F) as an assembly.
  1. Remove the 8 x 50 mm pin (A), the select return spring (B), the shift piece shaft (C), the shift piece (D) and the interlock (E).
  1. Remove the 5 x 22 mm spring pin (A) with the 3 x 22 mm spring pin, the select arm (B), the select lever (C), and the 14 x 20 x 4 mm dust seal (D).
  1. Apply tape to the mainshaft splines to protect the seal, then remove the mainshaft assembly (A) and countershaft assembly (B) with the shift fork assembly (C) from the clutch housing (D).
  1. Remove the 64 mm spring washer (E).
  1. Remove the oil pump shaft (A), and the differential assembly (B).
  1. Remove the oil pump plate (A), the oil pump inner rotor (B), and the oil pump outer rotor (C).
  1. Remove the oil gutter plate (A).
  1. Remove the 87 mm shim (A) from the transmission housing (B).
  1. Remove the 8 mm special bolt (A), the 8 mm spring washer (B), the shift arm (C), the change lever (D), and the 16 x 23 x 5 mm dust seal (E).
