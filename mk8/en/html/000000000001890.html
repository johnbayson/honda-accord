Camshaft Inspection
NOTE: Do not rotate the camshaft during inspection.
  1. Remove the camshafts.
  1. Remove the lower camshaft holder and rocker arm/hydraulic tappet assemblies.
  1. Put the lower camshaft holder, camshafts, and upper camshaft holders on the cylinder head, then tighten the bolts in sequence to the specified torque.
    Specified Torque:
    6 x 1.0 mm
    12 N·m (1.2 kgf·m, 8.8 lbf·ft)

  1. Seat the camshaft by pushing it away from the camshaft gear end of the cylinder head.
  1. Zero the dial indicator against the end of the camshaft, then push the camshaft back and forth, and read the end play. If the end play is beyond the service limit, replace the cylinder head and recheck. If it is still beyond the service limit, replace the camshaft.
    Camshaft End Play
    Standard (New):
    0.05−0.15 mm (0.002−0.006 in.)
    Service Limit:
    0.16 mm (0.0063 in.)

  1. Loosen the camshaft holder bolts two turns at a time, in a crisscross pattern. Then remove the upper camshaft holders from the lower camshaft holder.
  1. Lift the camshafts out of the cylinder head, wipe them clean, then inspect the lift ramps. Replace the camshaft if any lobes are pitted, scored, or excessively worn.
  1. Clean the camshaft journal surfaces in the lower camshaft holder, then set the camshafts back in place. Place a plastigage strip across each journal.
  1. Install the upper camshaft holders, then tighten the bolts to the specified torque as shown in Step 3 .
  1. Remove the upper camshaft holders. Measure the widest part of plastigage on each journal.
    • If the camshaft-to-holder clearance is within limits, go to Step 12 .
    • If the camshaft-to-holder clearance is beyond the service limit, and the camshaft has been replaced, replace the cylinder head.
    • If the camshaft-to-holder clearance is beyond the service limit, and the camshaft has not been replaced, go to Step 11 .
    Camshaft-to-Holder Oil Clearance
    No. 1 Journal:
    Standard (New):
    0.030−0.069 mm(0.0012−0.0027 in.)
    Service Limit:
    0.08 (0.003 in.)
    No. 2, 3, 4, 5 Journals:
    Standard (New):
    0.050−0.089 mm(0.0020−0.0035 in.)
    Service Limit:
    0.10 mm (0.004 in.)

  1. Check the total runout with the camshaft supported on V-blocks.
    • If the total runout of the camshaft is within the service limit, replace the cylinder head.
    • If the total runout is beyond the service limit, replace the camshaft and recheck the camshaft-to-holder oil clearance. If the oil clearance is still beyond the service limit, replace the cylinder head.
    Camshaft Total Runout
    Standard (New):
    0.03 mm (0.001 in.) max.
    Service Limit:
    0.04 mm (0.002 in.)

  1. Measure cam lobe height.
    Cam Lobe Height Standard (New)
    Intake A:
    38.003 mm (1.4962 in.)
    Intake B:
    36.154 mm (1.4230 in.)
    Exhaust A:
    35.814 mm (1.4100 in.)
    Exhaust B:
    37.286 mm (1.4679 in.)
