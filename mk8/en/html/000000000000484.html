Navigation System Symptom Troubleshooting - Rearview camera does not come on or work properly
Rearview camera image does not come on or work properly
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II), and start the engine.
Does the Navigation screen come on and show an image when the vehicle is started?
YES -
Go to Step 2 .
NO -
Go to troubleshooting for No picture is displayed. ■
  1. Go into the Self-Diagnosis Mode, and use the System Links diagnostic to check the connection between the navigation unit and the rearview camera.
Is the ‘‘R-camera'' link on the screen?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Go to Step 11 .
  1. Confirm the system connection.
Is the ‘‘R-camera'' link red on the screen?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Go to Car Status and check the BACK-Reverse indication status. ■
  1. Check the connections between navigation display unit 28P connector and the rearview camera 6P connector.
Are the connections OK?

NO -
The wrong navigation unit is installed. Replace the navigation unit with the correct part. ■
  1. Remove the DVD disc from the navigation unit and check the label color of the DVD disc.
Is the DVD disc label white (or possibly gray for Canada)?
YES -
Reinsert the DVD disc, and go to Step 4 .
NO -
Replace the DVD disc with the correct color. See the Obtaining a Navigation DVD in the General Troubleshooting Information.
