M/T Assembly Reassembly
NOTE: Prior to reassembling, clean all parts in solvent, dry them, and apply MTF to all contact surfaces.
  1. If any of the items listed were replaced, do the carrier bearing preload adjustment before reassembling the transmission.
    • Transmission housing
    • Clutch housing
    • Differential carrier
    • Spacer
    • Carrier bearing and outer race
  1. Press in the new 16 x 23 x 5 mm dust seal (A) into the transmission housing, then install the change lever (B), the shift arm (C), the 8 mm spring washer (D), and the 8 mm special bolt (E).
    NOTE: Press the dust seal end until it is 0±0.2 mm (0±0.008 in.) below the surface of the transmission housing edge (F).
  1. Select the proper size 87 mm shim (A) according to the measurements made during the Mainshaft Thrust Clearance Adjustment. Install the 81 mm shim into the transmission housing (B).
  1. Install the oil gutter plate (A).
    NOTE: Press the gutter plate top until it is 0−1.7 mm (0−0.07 in.) below the surface of the clutch housing edge (B).
  1. Install the oil pump outer rotor (A), the oil pump inner rotor (B), and the oil pump plate (C).
    NOTE: Make sure the punch marks (D) on the oil pump outer and inner rotor facing up.
  1. Install the differential assembly (A) and the oil pump shaft (B).
  1. Install the 64 mm spring washer (A). Note the installation direction of the 64 mm spring washer.
  1. Apply tape to the mainshaft splines (B) to protect the seal. Install the mainshaft and countershaft (C) with the shift fork assembly (D) as an assembly.
  1. Press in the new 14 x 20 x 4 mm dust seal (A) into the clutch housing, then install the select lever (B), the select arm (C), the new 5 x 22 mm spring pin (D), and the new 3 x 22 mm spring pin (E).
    NOTE:
    • Press the dust seal end until it is 0±0.2 mm (0±0.008 in.) below the surface of the clutch housing edge (F).
    • Make the seams (G) of the 5 x 22 mm and 3 x 22 mm spring pin are opposite side.
  1. Set the select return spring (A), the 8 x 50 mm pin (B), and the interlock (C) as shown.
  1. Install the shift piece (A) and shift piece shaft (B).
  1. Assemble the 20 mm thrust needle bearings (A), the 20 mm thrust washers (B), the reverse idler gear shaft assembly (C), and the reverse shift fork (D).
  1. Install the 33 mm spring washer (E) to the reverse idler gear assembly. Note the installation direction of the spring washer.
  1. Slide the reverse idler gear shaft assembly (A) on the rib (B) of the clutch housing, then install it into the hole (C) of the clutch housing.
  1. Install the reverse shift lever (A), and 8 x 50 mm pin (B).
  1. Install oil guide pipe A, oil guide pipe (B), and magnet (C).
  1. Clean any dirt or oil from the transmission housing sealing surface.
  1. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M, 08C70-K0334M, or 08C70-X0331S) evenly the clutch housing mating surface of the transmission housing.
    NOTE: Do not install components if too much time has passed after applying the liquid gasket. Instead, remove the old residue and reapply the liquid gasket.
  1. Install the three 14 x 20 mm dowel pins (A).
  1. Place the transmission housing on the clutch housing, making sure to line up the shafts, and join the shift arm (B) in the groove of the shift piece (C) by turning the change lever (D).
  1. While expanding the 75 mm snap ring (E) on the countershaft ball bearing using the snap ring pliers, push the transmission housing down to start the countershaft ball bearing through the snap ring. Release the pliers, and push down the housing until it bottoms and the snap ring snaps in place in the countershaft ball bearing snap ring groove.
    NOTE: Install the transmission case cap (F) with the new O-ring (G) after setting in the 75 mm snap ring.
  1. Check the change lever operation.
  1. Make sure the 75 mm snap ring (A) is securely seated in the groove of the countershaft bearing.
    Dimension (1) as installed:
    1.67−9.42 mm (0.066−0.371 in.)

  1. Install the 10 mm flange bolts finger-tight with transmission hanger A and transmission hanger B.
  1. Torque the 10 mm flange bolts in a crisscross pattern in several steps.
    10 x 1.25 mm:
    44 N·m (4.5 kgf·m, 33 lbf·ft)

  1. Install the 6 mm fluid check bolt (A), the new 6 mm sealing washer (B), the drain plug (C), and the new 14 mm sealing washer (D).
  1. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M, 08C70-K0334M, or 08C70-X0331S) to the threads of the detent bolts (A), then install the steel balls (B), the 22.0 mm spring (C), the 27.4 mm spring (D), and the detent bolts.
  1. Install the new O-ring (E), plain washer (F), and output shaft (countershaft) speed sensor (G).
  1. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M, 08C70-K0334M, or 08C70-X0331S) to the threads of the detent bolts (A), then install the steel balls (B), the 22.0 mm spring (C), the 26.1 mm spring (D), the 27.4 mm spring (E), and the detent bolts.
  1. Install the oil filler plug (F) with the new 20 mm sealing washer (G).
  1. Clean any dirt or oil from the reverse lockout solenoid sealing surface.
  1. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K023M, 08C70-K0334M, or 08C70-X0331S) evenly to the transmission housing mating surface of the reverse lockout solenoid.
    NOTE: Do not install components if too much time has passed after applying the liquid gasket. Instead, remove the old residue and reapply the liquid gasket.
  1. Install the reverse lockout solenoid (A).
  1. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M, 08C70-K0334M, or 08C70-X0331S) to the threads of the back-up light switch (B), then install the switch in the transmission housing.
  1. Install the oil pump strainer (A), the strainer set spring (B), and the strainer cover (C) with the new O-ring (D).
    NOTE: Clean the strainer anytime the transmission is disassembled.
