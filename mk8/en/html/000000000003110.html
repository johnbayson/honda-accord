Rear Seat Belt Replacement
NOTE: Check the rear seat belts for damage, and replace them if necessary.
Rear Seat Belt
  1. Remove the rear seat cushion.
  1. Remove the lower anchor bolt (A).
  1. Remove the rear seat side bolster.
  1. Pull the rear seat belt (A), the tongue plate (B), and the lower anchor (C) down through the anchor wire (D). Remove the retractor mounting bolt (E) and retractor bolt (F), then remove the rear seat belt and the retractor (G).
  1. Remove these items:
    • Tonneau cover and cargo net, for some models
    • Damper maintenance lid
    • Cargo net striker, for some models
  1. If the vehicle is equipped with no cargo net, remove the anchor wire mounting bolts (A). Remove the anchor wire bolt (B), then remove the rear seat belt anchor wire (C).
  1. Install the rear seat belt and retractor in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Apply medium strength liquid thread lock to the anchor bolts before reinstallation.
    • Check that the retractor locking mechanism functions.
    • Before installing the anchor bolt, make sure there are no twists or kinks in the rear seat belt.
Rear Center Seat Belt
  1. Detach the clips, and release the hook (A), then remove the rear center seat belt cover (B).
  1. Remove these items:
    • Rear seat cushion
    • Rear seat belt lower anchors, both sides
    • Rear seat side bolsters, both sides
    • Tailgate weatherstrip
    • Tonneau cover and cargo net, for some models
    • Rear under floor cover
    • Damper maintenance lids, both sides
    • Cargo net strikers, both sides
    • Rear seat belt anchor wires, both sides
    • Cargo area side trim panels, both sides
    • Rear door opening seals, both sides as needed
    • Roof end trim
    • Quarter pillar trim, both sides
    • Rear grab handles, both sides
    • Front cargo net hook covers, both sides, with cargo net
    • Rear cargo hook covers, both sides, for some models
    • Ceiling light
  1. Lower the rear portion of the headliner (A) to gain access to remove the rear seat belt and the retractor (B).
    -1.
    Detach the antenna lead connectors on both sides (C).
    -2.
    With the help of an assistant for supporting a headliner, carefully detach the rear clips.
    NOTE: Do not bend the headliner. Bending the headliner will crease and damage it.
  1. While an assistant holding the headliner, remove the upper anchor bolts (A), then remove the upper anchor (B).
  1. While an assistant holding the headliner, remove the retractor mounting bolt (A) and retractor bolt (B), then remove the retractor (C).
  1. Install the rear center seat belt and retractor in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, replace them with new ones.
    • Apply medium strength liquid thread lock to the anchor bolts before reinstallation.
    • Check that the retractor locking mechanism functions
    • Before installing the upper anchor bolt, make sure there are no twists or kinks in the seat belt.
Seat Belt Buckles and Center Seat Belt Detachable Anchor
  1. Remove the rear seat cushion.
  1. Disconnect the seat belt switch connectors (A), detach the harness clip (B), and remove the harness cushion (C).
LHD model
RHD model
  1. Remove the center anchor bolts (A), then remove the right seat belt buckle (B), the center seat belt buckle detachable anchor (C), the center seat belt buckle (D), and the left seat belt buckle (E).
  1. Install the seat belt and buckles in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace the removed cushion tape with a new one.
    • Make sure the seat belt switch connectors are plugged in properly.
    • Apply medium strength liquid thread lock to the center anchor bolts before reinstallation.
    • Tighten the bolts by hand first, then tighten to the specified torque.
    • Assemble the washer on the center anchor bolt as shown.
