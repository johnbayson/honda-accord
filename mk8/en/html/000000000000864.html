SRS DTC Troubleshooting: Fx-11
DTC Fx-11 (''x'' can be 0 thru 9 or A thru F):
Airbags and/or Tensioners Deployment Recorded
NOTE: Before doing this troubleshooting procedure, review SRS Precautions and Procedures and General Troubleshooting Information.
The SRS unit must be replaced after any airbags and/or tensioners have deployed. ■
