DTC Troubleshooting: P2010 (178)
DTC P2010:
 IMRC Valve Circuit Shorted to Power
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and review the general troubleshooting information.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine, and blip the engine several times.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P2010 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the IMRC valve and at the ECM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and wait 2 minutes.
  1. Disconnect the IMRC valve 5P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between IMRC valve 5P connector terminals No. 4 and No. 5.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Go to Step 10 .
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 1 (96P).
  1. Check for continuity between IMRC valve 5P connector terminals No. 4 and No. 5.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wires between the IMRC valve (IMRCM+ line) and the IMRC valve (IMRCM− line), then go to Step 20 .
NO -
Go to Step 25 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between body ground and IMRC valve 5P connector terminals No. 4 and No. 5 individually.
Is there about 1 V or less?
YES -
Go to Step 12 .
NO -
Go to Step 13 .
  1. At the IMRC valve side, measure the resistance between IMRC valve 5P connector terminals No. 4 and No. 5.
Is there about 1 Ω or less?
YES -
Go to Step 18 .
NO -
Go to Step 25 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and wait 2 minutes.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 1 (96P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between IMRC valve 5P connector terminal No. 4 and body ground.
Is there about 1 V or less?
YES -
Go to Step 17 .
NO -
Repair short to power in the wire between the IMRC valve and ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 72, then go to Step 20 .
  1. Measure the voltage between IMRC valve 5P connector terminal No. 5 and body ground.
Is there about 1 V or less?
YES -
Go to Step 25 .
NO -
Repair short to power in the wire between the IMRC valve and ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 96, then go to Step 20 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Replace the IMRC valve.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine, and blip the engine several times.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P2010 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the IMRC valve and the ECM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Start the engine, and blip the engine several times.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P2010 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the IMRC valve and the ECM. If the ECM was updated, substitute a known-good ECM, then recheck. If the ECM was substituted, go to Step 1 .
NO -
If the ECM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the ECM was substituted, replace the original ECM. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
