High Mount Brake Light Replacement
  1. Open the trunk lid.
  1. Disconnect the 2P connector (A) from the high mount brake light.
    High Mount Brake Light:
    21 W

  1. Turn the bulb socket (B) 45 ° counterclockwise to remove the bulb (C).
  1. To replace the high mount brake light housing, remove the rear shelf.
  1. Remove the high mount brake light (A).
  1. Install the high mount brake light in the reverse order of removal.
