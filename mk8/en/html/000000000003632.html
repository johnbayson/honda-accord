DTC Troubleshooting: P2080 (55)
DTC P2080:
 EGT Sensor 1 Circuit Range/Performance Problem
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and review the general troubleshooting information.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine. Hold the engine speed at 2,000 rpm (min −1 ) without load (in neutral) until the radiator fan comes on, then let it idle.
  1. Test-drive under these conditions:
    • Engine coolant temperature (ECT SENSOR 1) above 70 °C (158 °F)
    • Transmission in 3rd or 4th gear
    • Vehicle speed 50 km/h (31 mph) or more
    • Drive at a steady engine speed between 1,500−3,000 rpm (min −1 )
    • Drive about 2 minutes or more
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for DTC P2080 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the screen indicate FAILED?
YES -
Replace EGT sensor 1, then go to Step 6 .
NO -
If the screen indicates PASSED, intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at EGT sensor 1 and the ECM. If the screen indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 3 and recheck.
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine. Hold the engine speed at 2,000 rpm (min −1 ) without load (in neutral) until the radiator fan comes on, then let it idle.
  1. Test-drive under these conditions:
    • Engine coolant temperature (ECT SENSOR 1) above 70 °C (158 °F)
    • Transmission in 3rd or 4th gear
    • Vehicle speed 50 km/h (31 mph) or more
    • Drive at a steady engine speed between 1,500−3,000 rpm (min −1 )
    • Drive about 2 minutes or more
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P2080 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at EGT sensor 1 and the ECM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Go to Step 10 .
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for DTC P2080 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the screen indicate PASSED?
YES -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs were indicated in Step 9 , go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
NO -
If the screen indicates FAILED, check for poor connections or loose terminals at EGT sensor 1 and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If the screen indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 7 .
