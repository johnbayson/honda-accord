DTC Troubleshooting: U0104 (107)
DTC U0104:
 F-CAN Malfunction (Transmission Control Module (TCM) - Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) Unit)
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and any on-board snapshot with the HDS, and review General Troubleshooting Information.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Are DTC U0029 and U0104 indicated at the same time?
YES -
Go to the DTC U0029 troubleshooting. ■
NO -
Go to Step 3 .
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Is DTC U0104 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals between the gauge control module, the ACC unit, and the TCM.■
  1. Check for communication the ACC system with the HDS.
Does the HDS communicate with the ACC system?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Go to the DLC circuit troubleshooting. ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and wait for at least 2 minutes.
  1. Disconnect ACC unit 20P connector.
  1. Disconnect the TCM connector.
  1. Connect TCM connector terminal No. 53 to body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between ACC unit 20P connector terminal No. 10 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 11 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between TCM connector terminal No. 53 and the ACC unit 20P connector, then go to Step 13 .
  1. Connect TCM connector terminal No. 54 to body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between ACC unit 20P connector terminal No. 20 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Update the ACC unit if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ACC unit, then go to Step 13 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between TCM connector terminal No. 54 and the ACC unit 20P connector, then go to Step 13 .
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine, and wait for at least 2 minutes.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0104 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals between TCM connector terminals No. 53, No. 54, the gauge control module, the ACC unit, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Go to Step 18 .
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for U0104 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the HDS indicate PASSED?
YES -
Troubleshooting is complete. If the ACC unit was substituted, replace the original ACC unit. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs were indicated in Step 17 , go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
NO -
If the HDS indicates FAILED, check for poor connections or loose terminals between TCM connector terminals No. 53, No. 54, the gauge control module, and the ACC unit, then go to Step 1 . If the HDS indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 16 .
