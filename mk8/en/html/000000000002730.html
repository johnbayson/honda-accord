M/T Differential Carrier Bearings Replacement
Special Tools Required

Inner driver handle, 40 mm
 07746-0030100
NOTE: The illustrations show K24Z3 engine model.
  1. Check the carrier bearings for wear and rough rotation. If they rotate smoothly and their rollers show no signs of wear, the bearings are OK.
  1. Remove the carrier bearings (A) with a commercially available bearing puller (B).
  1. Install the new bearings (A) with the 40 mm inner driver handle (B). Press on each bearing until it bottoms. There should be no clearance between the bearings and the differential carrier.
    NOTE: Place the seal (C) part of the bearing towards the outside of the differential, then install it.
