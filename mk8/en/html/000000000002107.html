DTC Troubleshooting: P0405 (12)
DTC P0405:
 EGR Valve Position Sensor Circuit Low Voltage
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and review the general troubleshooting information.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Check the EGR VALVE POSITION in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is about 0.3 V or less indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the EGR valve and the ECM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect the EGR valve 5P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Check the EGR VALVE POSITION in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is about 0.3 V or less indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Go to Step 10 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and wait 2 minutes.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 1 (96P).
  1. Check for continuity between EGR valve 5P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the EGR valve and ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 31, then go to Step 16 .
NO -
Go to Step 20 .
  1. Measure the voltage between EGR valve 5P connector terminals No. 2 and No. 3.
Is there about 5 V?
YES -
Go to Step 14 .
NO -
Go to Step 11 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and wait 2 minutes.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 1 (96P).
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 17 and EGR valve 5P terminal No. 3.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 20 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the EGR valve and ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 17, then go to Step 16 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Replace the EGR valve.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0405 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the EGR valve and the ECM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0405 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the EGR valve and the ECM. If the ECM was updated, substitute a known-good ECM, then recheck. If the ECM was substituted, go to Step 1 .
NO -
If the ECM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the ECM was substituted, replace the original ECM. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
