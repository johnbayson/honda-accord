A/T Secondary Shaft 1st Clutch Hub Replacement
Special Tools Required

Driver handle, 15 x 135L
 07749-0010000

Driver attachment, 43.5 mm
 07947-6340500

Attachment, 78 x 90 mm
 07GAD-SD40101
  1. Remove the 1st-hold clutch hub (A) from 1st gear (B) using the driver handle, the 43.5 mm driver attachment, and a press.
  1. Remove the 1st clutch hub (C) from 1st gear.
  1. Install a new 1st clutch hub (A) over 1st gear (B), and install the 1st-hold clutch hub (C) in 1st gear.
  1. Install the 1st-hold clutch hub (A) in 1st gear (B) using the driver handle, the 78 x 90 mm attachment, and a press.
