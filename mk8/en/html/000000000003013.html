Wiper Arm/Nozzle Adjustment
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II), then turn the wiper switch ON, and then back OFF.
  1. When the wiper arms stop at the park position, confirm that they are at the standard position.
    a:
    Position at about 48 mm (1.9 in.) from the top of cowl cover (A).
    b:
    Position at about 33.2 mm (1.3 in.) from the top of cowl cover (A).

    NOTE: LHD model is shown, RHD model is symmetrical.
  1. When you turn on the washer(s), confirm 50 % or more of the washer fluid lands within the spray area. If the spray area is not within the standard positions, adjust the nozzle(s).
    d:
    Position at about 166 mm (6.5 in.) from the top of the black ceramic area (B) at the lower windshield.
    e:
    Position at about 380 mm (15 in.) from the top of the black ceramic area (B) at the lower windshield.
    f:
    Position at about 250 mm (9.8 in.) from the windshield center line (C).
