DTC Troubleshooting: P2002 (187)
DTC P2002:
 DPF Efficiency Below Threshold
Special Tools Required

Gauge 600 kPa
 07YAJ-0010100
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and review the general troubleshooting information.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine, and hold the engine speed at 2,000 rpm (min −1 ) without load (in neutral) until the radiator fan comes on, then let it idle.
  1. Hold the engine speed above 3,200 rpm (min −1 ) without load (in neutral) for 10 seconds.
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for DTC P2002 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the screen indicate FAILED?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
If the screen indicates PASSED, intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the DPF differential pressure sensor and the ECM. If the screen indicates EXECUTING, keep driving until a result comes on. If the screen indicates OUT OF CONDITION, go to Step 1 and recheck.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Check for a looseness exhaust pressure lines between the DPF differential pressure sensor and the DPF converter.
Are the lines OK?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Reconnect the exhaust pressure line(s), then go to Step 25 .
  1. Visually check the DPF converter body condition.
Is the condition OK?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Replace the DPF converter, then go to Step 25 .
  1. Disconnect the exhaust pressure lines between the DPF differential pressure sensor and the DPF converter.
  1. Apply vacuum to front exhaust pressure line (A).
Does it hold vacuum?
YES -
Check for a restricted between the DPF differential pressure sensor and the DPF converter, then go to Step 24 .
NO -
Go to Step 11 .
  1. Apply vacuum to rear exhaust pressure line (A).
Does it hold vacuum?
YES -
Check for a restricted between the DPF differential pressure sensor and the DPF converter, then go to Step 24 .
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Close the front exhaust pressure line of the DPF converter side.
  1. Apply vacuum to front exhaust pressure line.
Does it hold vacuum?
YES -
Go to Step 14 .
NO -
Repair the front exhaust pressure line, then go to Step 24 .
  1. Close the rear exhaust pressure line of the DPF converter side.
  1. Apply vacuum to rear exhaust pressure line.
Does it hold vacuum?
YES -
Go to Step 16 .
NO -
Repair the rear exhaust pressure line, then go to Step 24 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Check the DIESEL PARTICULATE FILTER (DPF) DIFFERENTIAL PRESSURE in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is there about 0−2 kPa (0−0.02 kgf/cm 2 , 0−0.29 psi)?
YES -
Go to Step 18 .
NO -
Replace the DPF differential pressure sensor, then go to Step 23 .
  1. Connect the pressure side of vacuum pump (A) and the gauge 600 kPa to the DPF differential pressure sensor port (B) as shown.
    NOTE:
    • Do not connect the vacuum side of vacuum pump to the DPF differential pressure sensor port (B).
    • Do not connect the vacuum pump to the rear exhaust pressure port of the DPF differential pressure sensor.
  1. Slowly apply 40 kPa (0.41 kgf/cm 2 , 5.8 psi) of pressure to the sensor.
  1. Check the DIESEL PARTICULATE FILTER (DPF) DIFFERENTIAL PRESSURE in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is there about 30−50 kPa (0.30−0.51 kgf/cm 2 , 4.35−7.25 psi)?
YES -
Go to Step 21 .
NO -
Replace the DPF differential pressure sensor, then go to Step 23 .
  1. Slowly apply 80 kPa (0.81 kgf/cm 2 , 11.6 psi) of pressure to the sensor.
  1. Check the DIESEL PARTICULATE FILTER (DPF) DIFFERENTIAL PRESSURE in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is there about 70−90 kPa (0.71−0.92 kgf/cm 2 , 10.15−13.05 psi)?
YES -
Replace the DPF converter, then go to Step 23 .
NO -
Replace the DPF differential pressure sensor, then go to Step 23 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Reconnect all exhaust pressure lines.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine, and hold the engine speed at 2,000 rpm (min −1 ) without load (in neutral) until the radiator fan comes on, then let it idle.
  1. Hold the engine speed above 3,200 rpm (min −1 ) without load (in neutral) for 10 seconds.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P2002 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the DPF differential pressure sensor and the ECM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Go to Step 30 .
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for DTC P2002 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the screen indicate PASSED?
YES -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs were indicated in Step 29 , go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
NO -
If the screen indicates FAILED, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the DPF differential pressure sensor and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If the screen indicates EXECUTING, keep driving until a result comes on. If the screen indicates OUT OF CONDITION, go to Step 27 .
