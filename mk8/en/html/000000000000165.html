ACC Unit Removal/Installation
  1. Remove the driver's dashboard lower cover.
  1. Disconnect the 20P connector (A) from the ACC unit (B).
  1. Remove the bolts (C) and the ACC unit.
  1. Install the ACC unit in the reverse order of removal.
  1. If you replace a new ACC unit, follow these items:
    • Do the ACC unit update procedure.
    • Do the CMBS initial check.
