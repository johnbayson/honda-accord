Keyless/Power Door Locks/Security System Symptom Troubleshooting - Doors automatically relock 30 seconds after being unlocked with the transmitter even though a door has been opened
Doors automatically relock 30 seconds after being unlocked with the transmitter even though a door has been opened
NOTE:
  • Before troubleshooting, check the B-CAN DTCs. If any DTC is indicated, troubleshoot the indicated DTC first.
  • The driver's side door switches are connected to the driver's MICU, and the passenger's side door switches are connected to the passenger's MICU.
  1. Place the ceiling light switch in the DOOR position.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Watch the ceiling light and the door indicators on the gauge control module.
Does the ceiling light and door indicators come on when the door is open, and go off when the door is closed?
YES -
Substitute a known-good driver's (or passenger's) under-dash fuse/relay box and recheck. If the symptom goes away, replace the original under-dash fuse/relay box.■
NO -
Repair an open in the wire between the driver's (or passenger's) MICU and each door switch. If the wire is OK, replace the door switch.■
