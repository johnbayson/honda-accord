Engine Oil Pan Installation
Special Tools Required

Subframe alignment pin
 070AG-SJAA10S
  1. Remove any old liquid gasket from the oil pan mating surfaces, bolts, and bolt holes.
  1. Clean and dry the oil pan mating surfaces.
  1. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M or 08C70-K0334M) to the engine block mating surface of the oil pan and to the inside edge of the bolt holes.
    NOTE:
    • Apply liquid gasket about 2.5 mm (0.098 in.) diameter bead along the broken line (A).
    • Do not install components if 5 minutes or more have passed after applying the liquid gasket. Instead, remove the old residue and reapply the liquid gasket.
  1. Install the oil pan.
  1. Tighten the bolts in three steps. In the final step tighten all bolts, in sequence, to 12 N·m (1.2 kgf·m, 8.8 lbf·ft). Wipe off the excess liquid gasket on the each side of crankshaft pulley and flywheel.
    NOTE:
    • Wait at least 30 minutes before filling the engine with oil.
    • Do not run the engine for at least 3 hours after installing the oil pan.
  1. Install the clutch cover (A), and install the transmission mounting bolts (B).
  1. Connect the oil level sensor connector (A), then install the oil level sensor cover (B).
  1. Install the harness holder bracket.
  1. Install the lower torque rod bracket.
  1. Install the A/C compressor bracket.
  1. Install the diesel particulate filter (DPF) brackets.
  1. If the engine is still in the vehicle, do Step 13 through 30.
  1. Lift up the front side of subframe until it is contact with body. Loosely install the new subframe mounting bolts (A), the stiffener mounting bolts (B), and the stiffeners (C).
  1. Lift up the rear side of subframe until it is contact with body. Loosely install the new stiffener mounting bolts (A) and loosely tighten the subframe mounting bolts (B).
  1. Loosely install the subframe middle mount.
  1. Remove the jack.
  1. Insert the subframe alignment pin through the positioning slot (A) on the right rear stiffener, through the positioning hole (B) on the subframe, and into the positioning hole (C) on the body, then loosely tighten the subframe right rear mounting bolt.
  1. Insert the frame positioning guide pin through the positioning slot on the left rear stiffener, through the positioning hole on the subframe, and into the positioning hole on the body, then loosely tighten the subframe left rear mounting bolt.
  1. With the frame positioning guide pin in place, tighten the subframe right rear mounting bolt.
  1. With the frame positioning guide pin in place, tighten the subframe left rear mounting bolt.
  1. Tighten the stiffener mounting bolts to the specified torque.
  1. Tighten the subframe mounting bolts to the specified torque.
  1. Check that the positioning slots on the right/left rear stiffener, the positioning holes on the subframe, and the positioning holes on the body are aligned using the frame positioning guide pin.
  1. Install the damper fork new mounting bolts.
  1. Install exhaust pipe A.
  1. With HID model: Connect the headlight front leveling sensor connector.
  1. Install the splash shield.
  1. Remove the steering gearbox mounting bolts.
  1. Install the A/C compressor, then connect the A/C compressor clutch connector.
  1. Install the A/C condenser fan/shroud assembly, then connect the A/C condenser fan motor connector.
  1. Install the bolts securing the water bypass line.
  1. Install the DPF differential pressure sensor, then connect the DPF differential pressure sensor connector.
  1. Install the drive belt.
  1. Install the strut brace.
  1. Check the wheel alignment.
