Previous Destinations button is dim and not selectable in the Enter destination by screen
The vehicle may be new, or the customer deleted the destination. Enter a destination, and allow the system to route to it. After the trip, the Previous Destinations button will be selectable.
Owner's manual for details
Today's Destinations button is dim and not selectable in the Enter destination by screen
The customer has not entered a group of locations for Today's Destinations. This is normal. The button is only selectable if the customer is using this function.
Owner's manual for details
