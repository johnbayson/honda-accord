M/T Reverse Lockout Solenoid Test
  1. Remove the reverse lockout solenoid.
  1. Connect the battery positive terminal to the No. 2 terminal of the reverse lockout solenoid 2P connector, and connect the battery negative terminal to the No. 1 terminal.
  1. Check that the reverse lockout solenoid operates.
  1. If the reverse lockout solenoid does not work, replace it.
