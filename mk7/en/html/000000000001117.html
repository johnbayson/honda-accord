Rear Panel Installation
  1. Set the new rear panel, and check the body dimentions, passenger's compartment, middle and rear floor under view, top view, side view, opening repair chart.
  1. Tack weld the new parts.
  1. Temporarily install the trunk lid, taillight, and rear bumper, then check for differences in level and clearance. Check the operation of the trunk lid.
  1. Do the main welding.
    • Weld the rear panel (A) and rear panel gusset (B).
    • Weld the rear panel and rear floor (C).
