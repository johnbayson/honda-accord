Engine Mount Replacement - Lower Torque Rod
  1. Raise the vehicle on the hoist to full height.
  1. Support the engine with a jack and wood block under the oil pan.
  1. Remove the lower torque rod (A).
  1. Install the lower torque rod, then tighten the new bolts (B) and loosely tighten the bolt (C).
  1. Remove a jack.
  1. Tighten the lower torque rod mounting bolt.
  1. Lower the vehicle on the hoist.
  1. Loosen the upper torque rod bolts, then retighten the bolts in the numbered sequence shown.
