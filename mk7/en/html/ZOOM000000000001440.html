1. Coat the new O-rings (A) with clean engine oil, and insert the injectors (B) into the fuel rail (C).
  1. Install the injector clip (D).
  1. Coat the injector O-rings (E) with clean engine oil.
  1. Then install the injectors in the injector base (F).
  1. Install the fuel rail mounting nuts (G) and the ground cable bolt (G101).
  1. Connect the connectors on the injectors and the engine mount control solenoid valve.
  1. Connect the quick-connect fittings.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), but do not operate the starter. After the fuel pump runs for about 2 seconds, the fuel rail will be pressurized. Repeat this two or three times, then check for fuel leakage.
  1. Install the intake manifold cover.
