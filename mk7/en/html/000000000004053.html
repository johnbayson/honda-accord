Navigation System Symptom Troubleshooting - System locks up constantly
System locks up constantly
  1. Start the engine and turn the ignition switch OFF, then turn the ignition switch ON (II).
Does the system reboot?
YES -
The system is OK at this time.■
NO -
Check the DVD for scratches or damage and the navigation unit for water damage. If OK, go into the Diagnostic Menu and do all of the ‘‘Unit Check'' tests. ■
