DTC Troubleshooting: P0603 (131)
DTC P0603:
 ECM/PCM Internal Control Module Keep Alive Memory (KAM) Error
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Wait 1 second.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Is DTC P0603 indicated?
YES -
Update the ECM/PCM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM/PCM, then recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away with a known-good ECM/PCM, replace the original ECM /PCM.■
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time.■
