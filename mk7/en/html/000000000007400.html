Maintenance Schedule for Normal Conditions
60,000km(3year)
Follow the Normal Conditions Maintenance Schedule if the severe driving conditions specified in the Severe Conditions Maintenance Schedule do not apply.



Service at the indicated distance or time whichever comes first
Replace engine oil
Replace engine oil filter
Inspect front and rear brakes

Check the brake pad and disc thickness. Check for damage or cracks front pad , front disc , rear pad , rear disc

Check the brake linings for cracking, grazing, wear, and contamination

Check the calipers for damage, leaks, and tightness
Check lights alignment
   Check the position of the headlights
Test drive
  Check for road stability, noise, vibration, and dashboard   operation
Check expiry date for temporary repair kit bottle (if equipped)
Visually inspect the following items:

Check for correct installation and position, check for cracks, deterioration, rust, and leaks

Check tightness of screws, nuts, and joints. If necessary, retighten
Tie-rod ends, steering gearbox, and boots
  Check rack grease and steering linkage. Check the boot for   damage and leaking grease
Suspension components Front susupension , Rear suspension

Check the bolts for tightness

Check all dust cover for deterioration and damage
Driveshaft boots

Check the boot and boot band for cracks

Check rack grease
Brake hoses and lines (including ABS/VSA)
   Check the master cylinder and ABS/VSA modulator   control-unit and leakage
All fluid levels and condition of fluids
   Check levels and check for leaks. If necessary, add engine   coolant, MTF,    ATF, brake fluid,    power steering fluid, and windshield   fluid
Exhaust system
  Check the exhaust pipe and muffler for damage, leaks, and   tightness K20Z2 engine ,    K24A3 engine
Fuel lines and connections
   Check fuel lines for loose connections, cracks, and   deterioration. Retighten loose connections   and replace any damaged parts
Tyre condition
  Check for pressure, puncture, cuts, and irregular thread   wear

Every 40,000 km (25,000 miles)
Replace air cleaner element
Replace spark plugs K20Z2 engine , K24A3 engine
Inspect the valve clearance
Every 120,000 km (75,000 miles)
Replace spark plugs K20Z2 engine , K24A3 engine
Every 120,000 km (75,000 miles) or 8 years
Replace manual transmission fluid (MTF)
   Use genuine Honda MTF
At 120,000 km (75,000 miles) or 8 years, then every 80,000 km (50,000 miles) or 4 years
Replace automatic transmission fluid (ATF)
  Use genuine Honda ATF-Z1    K20Z2 engine ,    K24A3 engine
At 200,000 km (120,000 miles) or 10 years, then every 100,000 km (60,000 miles) or 5 years
Replace engine coolant
   Use genuine Honda All Season Antifreeze/Coolant Type 2  
Every 3 years
Replace brake fluid
  Use only DOT 3 or DOT 4 brake fluid. We recommend   genuine Honda Brake Fluid. Check that brake fluid level   between the upper and lower marks on the reservoir  
Every year
Inspect vehicle corrosion:
  refer to the Body Repair Manual

Under body Corrosion Check
