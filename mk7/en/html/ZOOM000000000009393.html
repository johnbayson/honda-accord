Removal
  1. Make sure you have the anti-theft codes for the radio and the navigation system, then write down the customer's radio station presets.
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery.
  1. Remove the driver's airbag assembly and the steering wheel.
  1. Remove the driver's dashboard under cover.
  1. Remove the driver's dashboard lower cover.
  1. Remove the column covers.
  1. Remove the steering joint cover A.
  1. Release the tilt/telescopic lever, and adjust the steering column to full tilt up position, and to the full telescopic in position.
  1. Tighten the tilt/telescopic lever.
