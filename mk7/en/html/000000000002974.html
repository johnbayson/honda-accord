Fuel Filter and Hand Primer Removal/Installation
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Remove the air cleaner.
  1. Remove the hand primer cover (A).
  1. Remove the hand primer braket (B).
  1. Remove the fuel heater bolts (A).
  1. Remove the bolt (B), hoses (C) and fuel filter (D).
  1. To prevent deform, connect the fuel pipes to the fuel filter temporary.
  1. Install the fuel filter. Be careful not to deform any of the fuel pipes.
  1. Install the part in the reveres order of removal.
  1. Bleed the fuel system.
