SRS Indicator Troubleshooting
Side airbag cutoff indicator stays on after bulb check (If the indicator stays on, and SRS indicator is off, it does not set a DTC). Side airbag cutoff indicator is flashing
  • Make sure nothing is on the front passenger's seat.
  • If the side airbag cutoff indicator stays on after the ignition switch is turned ON (II), initialize the OPDS unit.
    • -
    If the side airbag cutoff indicator operates normally, the system is OK.
    -
    If the side airbag cutoff indicator stays on, replace the OPDS sensor/seat-back. The sensor is part of the seat-back pad.
