Intercooler Duct Cover Replacement
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the front bumper.
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Pull the inter-cooler duct cover (A) out to release the hooks (B), and release the outside hook (C), then remove the cover from the front bumper (D).
  1. Install the outside hook into the hole in the front bumper, then install the cover by pushing on the hook portions until the hooks snap into place.
