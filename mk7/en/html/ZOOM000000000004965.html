1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the two bolts securing the expansion tank, then move the expansion tank.
  1. Remove the harness clamp, then disconnect the radiator fan switch (High) connector.
  1. Remove the lower radiator hose and water bypass hose, then remove the thermostat cover and thermostat.
  1. Install the thermostat with a new rubber seal, then install the thermostat cover.
  1. Install the lower radiator hose and water bypass hose.
  1. Install the expansion tank.
  1. Refill the expansion tank with engine coolant, and bleed air from the cooling system with the heater valve open.
