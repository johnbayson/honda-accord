1. If the transmission fluid is dirty, remove the drain plug (D) and drain the fluid.
  1. Reinstall the drain plug with a new washer, and refill the transmission fluid to the proper level.

    Oil Capacity

    1.9 L (2.0 US qt, 1.7 Imp qt) at Fluid Change
    2.1 L (2.2 US qt, 1.8 Imp qt) at Overhaul

    Always use Honda Manual Transmission Fluid (MTF). Using motor oil can cause stiffer shifting because it does not contain the proper additives.
  1. Reinstall the oil filler plug (A) with a new washer.
