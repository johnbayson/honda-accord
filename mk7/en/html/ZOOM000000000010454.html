Precaution for the Dynamic Camera Aiming
Perform the dynamic camera aiming on a road that runs parallel and horizontal to adjacent lanes and that allows the vehicle to drive between 72−112 km/h (45−70 mph) for 10 minutes or more. The aiming fails to unless it is completed, within 30 minutes.
The dynamic camera aiming may not be completed in bad weather such as rain, fog, or snow. The white (yellow) lines on the road are not clearly visible because of the reflecting wet road surface.
When you perform the dynamic camera aiming in the dark, turn the light on. In this case, adjust the vehicle's optical axis properly and check that the vehicle's headlights and the windshield have not adhesion of dirt.
When the dynamic camera aiming has failed for some reason, an aiming failure code is displayed on the HDS. In this case, troubleshoot the indicated failure code with the HDS.
