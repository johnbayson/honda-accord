Low Fuel Indicator Test
  1. Do the gauge drive circuit check.
    • If the low fuel indicator flashes, go to Step 2 .
    • If the low fuel indicator does not flush, replace the gauge control module assembly.
  1. Perform the multiplex integrated control system troubleshooting test mode A.
    • If any DTC's are indicated, do the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
    • If no DTC's are indicated, go to Step 3 .
