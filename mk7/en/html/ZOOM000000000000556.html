Front Door Sill Area
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  1. Left side: Remove the footrest.
  1. Left side: Remove the front side cap from the front door sill trim, and remove the opener lock cylinder and bolt.
  1. Detach the hooks (A) and tabs (B) from the kick panel (C) and center pillar lower trim (D), and pull the front door sill trim (E) up by hand to detach the clips (F, G), then remove it.
  1. Disconnect the trunk lid opener/fuel fill door opener cable (H) from the opener (I).
  1. If necessary, pull the front side outer trim (J) up to detach the clips (K), then remove the trim.
