DTC Troubleshooting: 4
DTC 4:
 Upper FET Stuck ON
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Turn the steering wheel from lock-to-lock several times, and wait 10 seconds or more.
Does the EPS indicatior come on?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
  1. Stop the engine, and verify the DTC.
Is DTC 4 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Perform the appropriate troubleshooting for the code indicated.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, then disconnect EPS control unit connector B (2P).
  1. Check for continuity between EPS control unit connector B (2P) terminal No. 2 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Check for loose terminals and poor connections at the EPS control unit. If necessary, substitute a known-good EPS control unit and recheck.■
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the EPS control unit and the body ground (G506).■
