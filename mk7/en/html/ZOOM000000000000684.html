For some models: SRS components are located in this area. Review the SRS component locations LHD, SRS component locations RHD and the precautions and procedures before performing repairs or service.
NOTE:
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
Seat-back Cover
  1. Remove the front seat.
  1. Remove the headrest.
  1. For some models: From under the seat cushion, disconnect the recline motor connector (A), and if equipped, the seat-back heater connector (B).
    Detach the side airbag connector clip (C), OPDS unit connector clip (D) (for some RHD models), and harness clips (E). Release the clips (F) (except power seat) and seat cushion cover (G) from the seat cushion frame spring (H), then pull the cover back, and remove the clamps (I).
    NOTE: 4-way power seat on LHD model is symmetrical to RHD model, but it has no OPDS unit.
  1. Except power seat: Remove these items from the front seat, then remove the seat cushion, manual seat, manual height adjustable seat :
    • Recline cover
    • Center cover
  1. Power seat: Remove these items from the front seat, then remove the seat-back, 4-way power seat, 8-way power seat.
