Intercooler Replacement

Exploded View
  1. Remove the intercooler inlet hose.
  1. Raise the vehicle on the hoist to full height.
  1. Remove the left front tyre/wheel.
  1. Pull the left front inner fender to expose the intercooler.
  1. Remove the intercooler outlet hose (A), then remove two nuts (B) securing the intercooler bracket (C).
  1. Remove the intercooler (D) from the bracket.
  1. Install the intercooler in the reverse order of removal.
