Cautions for the Vehicle
  • All wheels of the vehicle should touch the ground.
  • Make sure that the suspensions are not modified.
  • Check the air pressure of all the tyres. If the pressures are uneven, inflate the tyres to the specified value.
  • Do not place the wheels of the vehicle on a steel plate or a roller when performing the aiming.
  • The vehicle should be empty and not be loaded when performing the aiming.
NOTE: Empty vehicle: A full fuel tank, tools and a spare tyre are present. Unload any baggage other than and the spare tyre.
  • Make sure that all doors are closed when performing the aiming.
  • Do not put any objects on the instrument panel.
  • Make sure that there are no stains on the lens of the LKAS camera, and the interior and exterior surface of the front glass of the lens parts.
  • Set the steering wheel in the straight ahead position.
  • Do not operate the steering wheel after the vehicle is set in the aiming position.
  • Shift the lever to the P position and apply the parking brake.
  • If you are towing a trailer, unhitch the trailer.
