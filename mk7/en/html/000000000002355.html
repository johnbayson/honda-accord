DTC Troubleshooting: P0563 (34-6)
DTC P0563:
 ECM Power Source Circuit High Voltage
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check the BATTERY in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is about 18.2 V or higher indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Go to Step 4 .
  1. Check the battery voltage with a circuit tester.
Is about 18.2 V or higher indicated?
YES -
Replace the battery, then go to Step 6 .
NO -
Go to Step 8 .
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Check the BATTERY in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is about 18.2 V or higher indicated?
YES -
Check the alternator, then go to Step 6 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time.■
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0563 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
  1. Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0563 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
If the ECM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the ECM was substituted, replace the original ECM. ■
