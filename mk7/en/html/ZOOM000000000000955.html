Troubleshooting Intermittent Failures
If there was a malfunction, but it does not recur, it will be stored in the memory as an intermittent failure, and the SRS indicator will come on.
After checking the DTC, troubleshoot as follows:
  1. Read the DTC (see ‘‘Reading the DTC'').
  1. Erase the DTC memory (see ‘‘Erasing the DTC Memory'').
  1. Set the parking brake, and start the engine, and let it idle.
  1. The SRS indicator comes on for about 6 seconds and then goes off.
  1. Shake the wire harness and the connectors, take a test drive (quick acceleration, quick braking, cornering), turn the steering wheel fully left and right, and hold it there for 5 to 10 seconds. If the problem recurs, the SRS indicator will come on.
  1. If you can't duplicate the intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.
