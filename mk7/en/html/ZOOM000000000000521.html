From under the dash, disconnect the interior wire harness connector (A) (with sunroof), clutch switch connector (B) (on LHD M/T model) or clutch subwire harness connector (C) (on RHD M/T model), brake switch connector (D), floor wire harness connectors (E), engine compartment wire harness connectors (F), inertia switch connector (G), and disconnect the dashboard wire harness connectors (H) from the under-dash fuse/relay box (I), then release the wire harness clips. On auto A/C model, disconnect the air hose (J).
    NOTE: Lift the white wire harness connector locks (K) before trying to remove the connectors from the fuse box.
