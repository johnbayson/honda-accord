DTC P0113:
 IAT Sensor 1 Circuit High Voltage
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check IAT SENSOR 1 in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is about 4.90 V or higher indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the MAP/IAT sensor and the ECM.■
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are DTC P0103 and P0113 indicated at same time?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Go to Step 14 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the MAF/IAT sensor 5P connector.
  1. Connect MAF/IAT sensor 5P connector terminals No. 3 and No. 5 with a jumper wire.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check IAT SENSOR 1 in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is about 4.90 V or higher indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Go to Step 24 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and wait 30 seconds.
  1. Remove the jumper wire from the MAF/IAT sensor 5P connector.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 1 (96P).
