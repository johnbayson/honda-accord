NOTE: When the DVD-ROM is re-inserted or replaced, a map match must be done.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Press the EJECT button (A) of the navigation unit (B) located center console.
  1. Remove the DVD-ROM.
  1. Insert the new DVD-ROM.
    NOTE: Do not turn the ignition switch OFF, until data is down loaded to navigation unit.
