1. Remove the steering column covers.
  1. Disconnect the cable reel 5P (13P) connector (A) from the dashboard wire harness.
    ( ): With cruise control
  1. Using a jumper wire, connect the No. 1 terminal of the dashboard wire harness 5P (or 13P) connector to body ground.
    • If the horns sound, go to Step 4 .
    • If the horns don't sound, check these items:
      • -
      No. 13 (20 A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box.
      -
      Horn relay (With security alarm system).
      -
      Relay module in the under-hood fuse/relay box (Without security alarm system).
      -
      Horns.
      -
      An open in the wire.
