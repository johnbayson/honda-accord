  1. Clean both balancer shaft No. 2 journals and bearing halves with a clean shop towel.
  1. Place one strip of plastigage across each No. 2 journal.
  1. Reinstall the bearings and upper balancer shaft holder, then torque the bolts.
    NOTE:
    • Do not rotate the balancer shafts during inspection.
    • K20Z2 engine: Do not reuse the 8 mm bolts.

    Specified Torque

    6mm Bolts:
    12 N·m (1.2 kgf·m, 8.7 lbf·ft)
    8 mm Bolts:
    K20Z2 Engine:
    32 N·m (3.3 kgf·m, 24 lbf·ft) +30 °
    K24A3 Engine:
    29 N·m (3.0 kgf·m, 22 lbf·ft)
