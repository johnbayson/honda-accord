DTC Troubleshooting: 7
LKAS Camera DTC 7:
 High Temperature Detection with LKAS Camera
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Set the temperature control dial to Max Cool, the mode control switch to Vent and the recirculation control switch to Recirculate.
  1. Turn the A/C switch on and fun switch to Max, and running the air conditioning for 15 minutes or more.
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Stop the engine.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC 7 indicated?
YES -
Check for loose terminals in the LKAS camera 10P connector. If there is not a malfunction, replace the LKAS camera. ■
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
