Zinc-plated Steel Plate Repair
The zinc-plated steel plate used in some panels of the Accord require different repair techniques than ordinary steel plate. Refer to ‘‘Body Construction'' for the location of the zinc-plated panels.
  1. Before spot welding the zinc-plated steel plate, remove the paint from both sides of the flange to be welded. Apply sealer to the flange after welding.
    NOTE: Seal the sanded surfaces thoroughly to prevent rust.
  1. The electric continuity properties for zinc-plated steel plate are different from ordinary steel plate. When spot welding, increase the current by 10−20%, or increase the resistance welding time. Also increase the number of weld spots by 10−20%.
    NOTE: The MIG welding procedures for zinc-plated steel plate are the same as for ordinary steel plate.
  1. Before applying putty or body filler to the zinc-plated steel plate, sand the zinc plating thoroughly to promote adhesion and to prevent blistering.
    NOTE: Use only epoxy-based putties and fillers on zinc-plated steel plate, following the manufacturer's specifications.
  1. When performing paint work, protect the ground wire and ground wire mounting hole threads with a bolt or a plug.
