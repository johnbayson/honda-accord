DTC Troubleshooting: 34, 37, 50
EPS (LKAS) Control Unit DTC 34:
 EPS (LKAS) Control Unit (Problem with the CPU communication line)
EPS (LKAS) Control Unit DTC 37:
 EPS (LKAS) Control Unit (Problem with the motor voltage)
EPS (LKAS) Control Unit DTC 50:
 EPS (LKAS) Control Unit (CPU)
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Start the engine and test-drive the vehicle at 72 km/h (45 mph) or more, and turn the main switch ON.
  1. Watch the adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS indicator.
Does the adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS indicator come on?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Check for DTCs with HDS.
Is DTC 34, 37 or 50 indicated?
YES -
Check for loose terminals in the EPS (LKAS) control unit connectors, and repair if necessary. If no poor connections are found, replace the EPS (LKAS) control unit. ■
NO -
Perform the appropriate troubleshooting for the code indicated.■
