Check for continuity between engine control module (ECM)/powertrain control module (PCM) connector terminal E26 and cruise control unit 14P connector terminal No. 12.
  • If there is continuity, replace the ECM/PCM.
  • If there is no continuity, repair open or short in the wire.
  • Blown No. 13 (20 A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box
  • Blown No. 18 (15 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box

  • Faulty ECM/PCM
