M/T Reverse Lockout Solenoid Assembly Disassembly/Reassembly
  1. Make sure you have the anti-theft codes for the audio and the navigation system, then write down the customer's audio presets.
  1. Disconnect the negative (−) cable first, then the positive (+) cable from the battery. Remove the battery.
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing.
  1. Remove the intake air cover.
  1. Remove the resonator chamber.
  1. Transmission is supported from the bottom with a jack.
  1. Remove the transmission mount bolt (A), nuts (B), bolt (C), and the ground cable bolt (D).
  1. Remove the stud bolts (A).
  1. Remove the transmission mount (B).
  1. Disconnect the reverse lockout solenoid connector.
  1. Remove the bolts (A), reverse lockout solenoid assembly (B).
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
