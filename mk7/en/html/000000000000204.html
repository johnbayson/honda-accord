Headlight Adjuster Leveling Sensor Replacement
With HID Lamp
  1. Raise the vehicle and support it with safety stands in proper location.
  1. Disconnect the 3P connector (A) from the front or rear headlight adjuster leveling sensor (B).
  1. Remove the mounting bolt(s) and nut from the headlight adjuster leveling sensor.
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
