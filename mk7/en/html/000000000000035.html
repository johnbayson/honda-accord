Yaw Rate/Lateral Acceleration Sensor Replacement
NOTE: Do not damage or drop the sensor as it is sensitive.
  1. LHD type: Remove the driver's seat.
    RHD type: Remove the passenger's seat.
  1. Turn up the floor carpet (A).
  1. Remove the bracket mounting bolts (B).
  1. Remove the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor (C) with the bracket (D).
  1. Remove the bracket (E).
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Do the VSA sensor neutral position memorization.
