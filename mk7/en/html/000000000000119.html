SRS Symptom Troubleshooting - SRS indicator stays on
The SRS Indicator Stays On
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Disconnect SRS unit connector A (28P) from the SRS unit.
  1. Remove the gauge control module. Disconnect the gauge control module connector (30P) (A) from the gauge control module.
  1. Check resistance between the No. 14 terminal of gauge control module connector (30P) and the No. 19 terminal of SRS unit connector A (28P). There should be 1 Ω or less.
Is the resistance as specified?
YES -
Faulty SRS indicator circuit in the gauge control module or poor contact at gauge control module connector (30P); check the connection. If the connection is OK, replace the gauge control module .■
NO -
Open in the dashboard wire harness; replace the dashboard wire harness.■
