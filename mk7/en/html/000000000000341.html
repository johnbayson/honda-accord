DTC Troubleshooting: 3
DTC 3:
 Lower FET Stuck ON
  1. Disconnect EPS control unit connector A (2P) and the motor 2P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between EPS control unit connector A (2P) terminal No. 2 and motor 2P connector terminal No. 2.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between EPS control unit and motor.■
  1. Check for continuity between EPS control unit connector A (2P) terminal No. 1 and motor 2P connector terminal No. 1.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between EPS control unit and motor.■
  1. Check for continuity between EPS control unit connector A (2P) terminals No. 2 and No. 1.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the EPS control unit terminals A1 and A2.■
NO -
Check for loose terminals and poor connections at the EPS control unit and motor connections. If necessary, substitute a known-good EPS control unit and recheck.■
