  1. Check the DTCs by selecting the DTCs MENU (DTCs) from the HDS.
Is DTC B1008, B1060 and/or B1061 indicated?
YES -
Perform the gauge control module self-diagnostic procedure. ■
NO -
Go to Step 8 .
  1. Disconnect climate control unit connector B (16P).
  1. Disconnect under-dash fuse/relay box connector N (45P) from the fuse/relay box.
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 22 terminal of under-dash fuse/relay box connector N (45P) and the No. 14 terminal of climate control unit connector B (16P).
Is there continuity?
YES -
The climate control unit is faulty, replace the climate control unit .■
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the gauge control module and the climate control unit.■
