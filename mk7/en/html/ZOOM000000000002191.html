YES -
Substitute a known-good throttle actuator control module, then go to Step 18 and recheck. If DTC P0123 is not indicated, replace the original throttle actuator control module, then go to Step 18 . If DTC P0123 is indicated, go to Step 16 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the throttle body and the throttle actuator control module (TPSA line), then go to Step 18 .
