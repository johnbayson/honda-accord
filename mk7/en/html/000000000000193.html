Front Fog Light Replacement
  1. Remove the mounting screw from the front fog light.
  1. Remove the front fog light (A) from the front bumper.
  1. Disconnect the 2P connector (B) from the front fog light.
  1. Turn the bulb socket (A) 45 ° counterclockwise to remove it from the housing.
