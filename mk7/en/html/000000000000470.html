Engine Oil Filter Feed Pipe Replacement
  1. Remove the oil filter.
  1. Remove the oil filter feed pipe.
  1. Install the two 20 x 1.5 mm nuts (A) onto the new oil filter feed pipe. Hold the nut with a wrench, then tighten the other nut.
  1. Tighten the oil filter feed pipe to the block to 49 N·m (5.0 kgf·m, 36 lbf·ft), then remove the nuts from the oil filter feed pipe.
