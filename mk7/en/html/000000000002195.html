Window Antenna Repair
NOTE: To make an effective repair, the broken section must be no longer than 25.4 mm (1 in.).
  1. Lightly rub the area around the broken section (A) with fine steel wool, then clean it with alcohol.
  1. Carefully mask above and below the broken portion of the window antenna wire (B) with cellophane tape (C).
  1. Mix the silver conductive paint thoroughly. Using a small brush, apply a heavy coat of paint (A) extending about 3.18 mm (0.125 in.).'' on both sides of the break. Allow 30 minutes to dry.
  1. Check for continuity in the repaired wire.
  1. Apply a second coat of paint in the same way. Let it dry three hours before removing the tape.
