DTC Troubleshooting: 112, 113, 114
EPS (LKAS) Control Unit DTC 112:
 LKAS Control Unit (Problem with direction of the current)
EPS (LKAS) Control Unit DTC 113:
 LKAS Control Unit (Problem with output of the current)
EPS (LKAS) Control Unit DTC 114:
 LKAS Control Unit (CPU)
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Start the engine and test-drive the vehicle at 72 km/h (45 mph) or more, and turn the main switch ON.
  1. Watch the adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS indicator.
Does the adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS indicator come on?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Check for DTC with HDS.
Is DTC 112, 113 or 114 indicated?
YES -
Check for loose terminals in the LKAS control unit connectors, and repair if necessary. If no poor connections are found, replace the LKAS control unit. ■
NO -
Do the appropriate troubleshooting for the code indicated.■
