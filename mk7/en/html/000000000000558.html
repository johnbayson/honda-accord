VTEC Solenoid Valve Removal/Installation
  1. Disconnect the VTEC solenoid valve connector (A) and the VTEC oil pressure switch connector (B) (except KQ model).
  1. Remove the bolts (C).
  1. Remove the VTEC solenoid valve (D).
  1. Install the valve in the reverse order of removal with a new solenoid valve filter (E).
