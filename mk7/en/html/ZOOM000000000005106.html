1. Install the crankshaft pulley, and hold the pulley with handle (A) and holder attachment (B).
  1. Tighten the bolt to 29 N·m (3.0 kgf·m, 22 lbf·ft) with a torque wrench and 19 mm socket (C). Do not use an impact wrench. If you are using a new pulley bolt or new crankshaft, tighten the bolt to 34 N·m (3.5 kgf·m, 25 lbf·ft).
  1. Tighten the pulley bolt an additional 90 °.
  1. Install the splash shield.
  1. Install the right front tyre/wheel.
