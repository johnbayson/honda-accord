Trunk Lid Opener Switch Replacement
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the trunk lid.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Trunk lid trim
    • Rear License Trim
  1. Remove the bolts and disconnect the connector (A), then remove the trunk lid switch (B).
  1. Install the switch in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the connector is plugged in properly.
    • Make sure the trunk lid opens properly.
