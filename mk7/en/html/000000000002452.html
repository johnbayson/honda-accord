DTC Troubleshooting: 64
DTC 64:
 Sensor Power Voltage
  1. Clear the DTC using the HDS.
  1. Test-drive the vehicle.
Does the ABS indicator come on, and is DTC 64 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the VSA control unit 47P connector and steering angle sensor 5P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between the VSA control unit 47P connector terminal No. 7 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to body ground between the VSA modulator-control unit and steering angle sensor.■
NO -
Replace the VSA modulator-control unit. ■
