1. Inspect the oil passage, gear and bearing surfaces for wear and damage, then measure the countershaft at points A, B, C, and D. If any part of the countershaft is less than the service limit, replace it.

    Standard:

    A Ball Bearing Surface (Transmission Housing   Side):
    30.002−30.015 mm (1.1812−1.1817 in.)
    B Distance Collar Surface:
    42.975−42.991 mm (1.6919−1.6926 in.)
    C Countershaft Reverse Gear Surface:
    45.934−45.950 mm (1.8084−1.8091 in.)
    D Needle Bearing Surface (Clutch Housing Side):
    43.974−43.990 mm (1.7313−1.7319 in.)


    Service Limit:

    A:
    29.95 mm (1.179 in.)
    B:
    42.92 mm (1.690 in.)
    C:
    45.88 mm (1.806 in.)
    D:
    43.92 mm (1.729 in.)
