1. Remove the ATF feed pipes (A) and ATF joint pipes (B).
  1. Remove the ATF strainer (C).
  1. Remove the regulator valve body (D) (seven bolts).
  1. Remove the stator shaft (E) and stator shaft stop (F), then remove the regulator separator plate (G) and two dowel pins (H).
  1. Remove the servo body (I) (11 bolts), then remove the separator plate (J) and two dowel pins (K).
  1. Remove the cooler check valve spring (L) and valve (M), then remove the main valve body (N) (three bolts). Do not let the check balls (O) fall out.
  1. Remove the ATF pump driven gear shaft (P), then remove the ATF pump gears (Q).
  1. Remove the main separator plate (R) and two dowel pins (S).
  1. Remove the ATF magnet (T), clean and reinstall it in the torque converter housing (U).
