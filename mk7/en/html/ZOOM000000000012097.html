DTC P1032:
 Fuel Heater Relay Circuit Open
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Do the FUEL HEATER ON/OFF in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P1032 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the fuel heater relay and the ECM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Check the No. 8 FI ECU (ECM) (20 A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Repair short in the wire between the No. 8 FI ECU (ECM) (20 A) fuse and the fuel heater relay. Also replace the No. 8 FI ECU (ECM) (20 A) fuse, then go to Step 13 .
  1. Remove the left front inner fender. Open the relay holder (A), and disconnect the fuel heater relay.
  1. Check the fuel heater relay.
Is the fuel heater relay OK?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Replace the fuel heater relay, then go to Step 13 .
