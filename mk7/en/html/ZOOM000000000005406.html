1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Remove the drive belt.
  1. Remove the power steering (P/S) pump, and the P/S hose clamp. Do not disconnect the P/S hoses from the pump.
  1. Remove the alternator.
  1. Remove the A/C compressor. Do not disconnect the A/C line from the compressor.
  1. Remove the thermostat housing.
  1. Remove the oil filter housing assembly (A).
  1. Remove the oil filter assembly, engine oil cooler, drive belt auto-tensioner, and water pump, then replace the oil filter housing.
  1. Reassemble the oil filter housing reverse order of removal, then install the oil filter housing using the new O-rings (B).
  1. Install the thermostat housing.
  1. Install the A/C compressor.
  1. Install the alternator.
  1. Install the P/S pump and the P/S hose clamp.
  1. Install the drive belt.
  1. Install the engine cover.
