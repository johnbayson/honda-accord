DTC Troubleshooting: 86
DTC 86:
 F-CAN Communication
  1. Clear the DTC using the HDS.
  1. Start and run the engine for at least 5 seconds then turn the engine off.
  1. Check for DTCs using the HDS.
Is DTC 86 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the F-CAN communication line is OK at this time.■
  1. Check for DTCs in the ECM.
Are any DTCs indicated?
YES -
Troubleshoot the ECM DTCs.■
NO -
Replace the VSA modulator-control unit. ■
