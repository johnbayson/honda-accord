Intermediate Shaft Removal
  1. Drain the transmission fluid. Reinstall the drain plug using a new washer.
  1. Remove the right driveshaft.
  1. Remove the exhaust pipe bracket.
  1. Remove the lower torque rod.
  1. Remove the flange bolt (A) and two dowel bolts (B).
  1. Remove the intermediate shaft (A) from the differential. Hold the intermediate shaft horizontal until it is clear of the differential to prevent damage to the differential oil seal (B).
