Front Bumper Lower Grille Replacement
Special Tools Required

KTC trim tool set
 SOJATP2014
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Use the appropriate tool from the KTC trim tool set to avoid damage when prying components.
  • Take care not to scratch the front bumper.
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. Remove the license plate.
  1. Remove the screw (A), and bolts (B), then remove the license plate base (C) from the front bumper (D) and front bumper lower grille (E).
  1. From the back of the front bumper (A), remove the screws (B), and clip (C), then remove the air intake (D), from the front bumper lower grille (E).
  1. Detach the hooks (A) on the bottom of the front bumper lower grille (B), then remove the grille from the front bumper (C).
  1. Install the grille in the reverse order of removal, and note these items: Push the hooks into place securely.
