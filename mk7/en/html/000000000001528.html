DTC Troubleshooting: P0192 (95-1)
DTC P0192:
 FRP Sensor Circuit Low Voltage
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check the FRP SENSOR in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is about 0.25 V or less indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the FRP sensor and the ECM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and wait 30 seconds.
  1. Disconnect the FRP sensor 3P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check the FRP SENSOR in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is about 0.25 V or less indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Go to Step 10 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and wait 30 seconds.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 1 (96P).
  1. Check for continuity between FRP sensor 3P connector terminal No. 2 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the FRP sensor and ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 40, then go to Step 17 .
NO -
Go to Step 20 .
  1. Measure voltage between FRP sensor 3P connector terminals No. 1 and No. 3.
Is there about 5 V?
YES -
Go to Step 15 .
NO -
Go to Step 11 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and wait 30 seconds.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 1 (96P).
  1. Connect FRP sensor 3P connector terminal No. 1 to body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 9 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 21 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the FRP sensor and ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 9, then go to Step 17 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Replace the fuel rail.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0192 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the FRP sensor and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
  1. Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0192 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the FRP sensor and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
If the ECM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the ECM was substituted, replace the original ECM. ■
