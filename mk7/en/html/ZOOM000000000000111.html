NOTE:
  • Before reassembling, check that all parts are free of dirt and other foreign particles.
  • Replace parts with new ones whenever specified to do so.
  • Do not spill brake fluid on the vehicle; it may damage the paint; if brake fluid gets on the paint, wash it off immediately with water.
  • To prevent dripping, cover the disconnected line joints with rags or shop towels.
  1. Replace the brake hose (A) if the hose is twisted, cracked, or if it leaks.
  1. Disconnect the brake hose from the brake line (B) using a 10 mm flare-nut wrench (C).
