Hydraulic Power Steering Gearbox Mount Cushion Replacement
  1. Remove the steering gearbox.
  1. Position the 34 mm socket wrench (A) on the flange part of the gearbox housing with a washer (B), 10 x 105 mm flange bolt (C) and the 10 mm nut (D) as shown.
  1. Hold the nut with a wrench, and tighten the flange bolt with a wrench. Remove the gearbox mount cushion (E).
  1. Apply a mild soap and water solution to the new gearbox mount cushion surface (A), then place it on the gearbox mounting cushion hole.
  1. Position the 34 mm socket wrench on the flange part of the gearbox housing with a washer, flange bolt, and the nut as shown.
  1. Install the gearbox mount cushion by tightening the nut until the mount cushion edges (B) properly fit on the gearbox flange surface.
