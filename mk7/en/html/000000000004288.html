A/T Shift Lever Removal
NOTE: The illustration shows LHD model; RHD model is similar.
  1. Remove the center console.
  1. Shift the transmission into the R position.
  1. Remove the nut securing the shift cable end.
  1. Press the holder lock release (A), and pull out the socket holder (B) on the shift cable (C) of the shift lever bracket base (D). Do not remove the shift cable by pulling the shift cable guide (E).
  1. Disconnect shift lock solenoid connector (A) and park pin switch connector (B), and remove harness clamps (C) on the shift lever bracket base.
  1. Remove the shift lever assembly.
