Fuel Tank Replacement
  1. Drain the fuel tank.
  1. Jack up the vehicle, and support the with jackstands.
  1. Remove the muffler.
  1. Remove the heat shield (A).
  1. Disconnect the fuel fill hose (B).
  1. Disconnect the fuel vapor hose (C), hose (D), and parking brake cable holder bolts (E). Slide back the clamps, then twist the hoses as you pull to avoid damaging them.
  1. Place a jack, or other support, under the tank (F).
  1. Remove the strap bolts and the straps (G).
  1. Remove the fuel tank.
  1. Install the remaining parts in the reverse order of removal.
