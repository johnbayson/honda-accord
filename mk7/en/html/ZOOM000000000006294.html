DTC P0103:
 MAF Sensor Circuit High Input
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check the MAF SENSOR in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is about 199 gm/s or 4.90 V or higher indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the MAF/IAT sensor and the ECM.■
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are DTC P0103 and P0113 indicated at same time?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Go to Step 11 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and wait 30 seconds.
  1. Disconnect the MAF/IAT sensor 5P connector.
  1. At sensor side, measure resistance between MAF/IAT sensor 5P connector terminals No. 3 and No. 5.
Is there about 0 Ω?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Go to Step 7 .
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 1 (96P).
