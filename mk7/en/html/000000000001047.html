Trunk Lid Adjustment
  1. Remove the rear shelf.
  1. Adjust the alignment of the trunk lid (A) in the following sequence.
    • Adjust the trunk lid hinges (B) right and left, as well as forward and rearward, by using the elongated holes. Take care not to hit the rear window when loosening the bolts (C).
    • Turn the trunk lid edge cushions (D), in or out as necessary, to make the trunk lid fit flush with the body at the rear and side edges.
    • Adjust the fit between the trunk lid and the trunk lid opening by moving the striker (E).
  1. Tighten the bolts to the specified torque.
  1. Make sure the trunk lid opens properly and locks securely.
  1. Reinstall all remaining removed parts.
