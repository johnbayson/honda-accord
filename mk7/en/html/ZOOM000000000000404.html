Removal
  1. Make sure the front wheels are aligned straight ahead.
  1. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait at least 3 minutes.
  1. Remove the driver's airbag.
  1. Disconnect the connector (A) from the cable reel, then remove the steering wheel bolt (B).
  1. Confirm that the front wheels point straight ahead, then remove the steering wheel with a steering wheel puller . Do not tap on the steering wheel or steering column shaft when removing the steering wheel.
