1. With sunroof: Remove the headliner.
    -1.
    Remove the socket plug (A).
    -2.
    Remove the front door opening trim (B) and rear door opening trim (C) from each roof portion.
    -3.
    Release the fasteners (D) by pulling the front portion of the headliner (E) down.
    -4.
    With the help of an assistant, release the clip, detach the fasteners (F) by pulling the rear portion of the headliner down, then release the headliner from the sunroof frame (G) by sliding the headliner rearward, and lowering the headliner.
    -5.
    Remove the headliner through the front passenger's door opening.
