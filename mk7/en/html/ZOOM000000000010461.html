Horizontal Aiming
NOTE: Confirm that is there enough working space to perform the millimeter wave radar unit aiming through connecting HDS.
  1. Move the vehicle to pit or a flat space which is possible to work.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Connect the HDS to the data link connector (A).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
    NOTE: If DTC 61 or DTC 62 is indicated, troubleshoot the indicated DTC first.
  1. Start the engine.
