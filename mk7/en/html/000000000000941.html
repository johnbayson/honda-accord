A/T Interlock System - Park Pin Switch Test
NOTE: The illustration shows LHD model; RHD model is similar.
  1. Remove the center console.
  1. Disconnect the transmission gear selection switch/park pin switch connector.
  1. Shift the shift lever into the P position and release the shift lever button, then check for continuity between connector terminals No. 1 and No. 4. There should be no continuity.
  1. Shift out of the P position or press the shift lever button, and check for continuity between terminals No. 1 and No. 4. There should be continuity.
  1. If the park pin switch is faulty, replace the shift lever bracket base. The park pin switch is not available separately from the shift lever bracket base.
