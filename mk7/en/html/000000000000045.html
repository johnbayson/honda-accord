Power steering rack rattle or chattering
Procedure(s)
  1. Check for loose steering components (tie-rod and ball joints). Tighten or replace as necessary.
  2. Check the steering column shaft for wobbling. If the steering column wobbles, replace the steering column assembly.
  3. Check the rack guide adjustment.
  4. Check the power steering pump pulley:
  5. If the pulley is loose, tighten it.
  6. If the pump shaft is loose, replace the pump.
