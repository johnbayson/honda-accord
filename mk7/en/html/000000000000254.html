Power Seat Switch Test/Replacement - 4-way Adjustable
Front Passenger's power Seat (4-way Adjustable)
  1. Remove the power seat adjustment switch knobs and recline cover from the front passenger's power seat, then remove the two screws and the power seat switch.
  1. Disconnect the 12P connector from the power seat adjustment switch (A).
  1. Reinstall the adjustment switch knobs to the switch.
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals in each switch position according to the table.
  1. If the continuity is not as specified, replace the switch.
