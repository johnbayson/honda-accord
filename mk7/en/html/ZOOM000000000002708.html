Removal
NOTE:
  • Review the interior trim replacement procedure before performing repair or service
  • Removal of the side curtain airbag must be performed according to the precautions/procedures described at the beginning of the SRS.
  • The side curtain airbag system consists of the side curtain airbag module, including the roof trim, front grab handle. When the side curtain airbag has been deployed, these parts should be replaced.
  1. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait at least 3 minutes before beginning work.
  1. Remove the headliner.
  1. Remove the window antenna coil.
  1. Disconnect the side curtain airbag subharness from the side curtain airbag 2P connector (A).
