Trunk Latch Switch Test
  1. Open the trunk lid.
  1. Disconnect the 3P connector (A) from the trunk lid latch (B).
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 2 and No. 3 terminals.
    • There should be continuity with the trunk lid latch unlatched (trunk open).
    • There should be no continuity with the trunk lid latch latched (trunk closed).
  1. If the continuity is not as specified, replace the trunk lid latch switch.
