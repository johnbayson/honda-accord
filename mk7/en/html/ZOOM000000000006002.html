1. Remove the windshield portion of the roof molding (A).
    -1.
    Carefully insert a plastic spatula (B) in under the molding next to the lower clip (C).
    -2.
    While pulling the clip portion of the molding up by hand, push each of the eight small hooks (D) in the numbered sequence shown to release the clip from the retainer (E). Do not try to pry up the clip if it is hard to release from the retainer.
    -3.
    Gradually work your way up to release each of the upper clips (F, G, H).
