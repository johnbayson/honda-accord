Precaution for the Static Camera Aiming
To complete the aiming, it is necessary to succeed all of three targets' (center, passenger's side, driver's side) detections. If the detection for any one of targets failed or aborted on the way, the condition will be the aiming uncompleted.
When the static camera aiming was failure for some reason, aiming failure code is displayed on the HDS. In this case, troubleshoot the indicated failure code with the HDS.
Vehicle preparation
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Make sure that the adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS indicator goes out.
    NOTE: If the indicator remains on, check the DTC with HDS. When the LKAS camera is replaced, removed and attached or failing the aiming process,the DTC 10 of LKAS camera failure diagnosis (unfinished aiming of the LKAS) is stored and the adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS indicator, but the LKAS camera aiming can be performed. If the other DTC is displayed, perform the DTC failure diagnosis.
    If the source of failure in not found, the LKAS camera aiming can not be performed.
