Special Tools Required

Engine sub hanger plate
 07MAK-PY30100
  1. If the engine is out of the vehicle, go to Step 16 .
  1. Raise the hoist to full height.
  1. Drain the engine oil.
  1. Remove the front tyres/wheels.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Disconnect the stabilizer links.
  1. Remove the right side damper fork.
  1. Disconnect the right side suspension lower arm ball joint.
  1. Remove the right side driveshaft. Coat all precision-finished surfaces with clean engine oil. Tie plastic bag over the driveshaft end.
  1. Lower the hoist.
  1. Remove the front mount stop, then remove the front mount bolt.
  1. Remove the rear mount stop, then remove the rear mount bolt.
  1. Remove the ground cable, then remove the upper bracket.
  1. Remove a bolt securing the side engine mount bracket, then attach the special tool as shown.
  1. Lift the engine to 30−60 mm (1.2−2.4 in.).
