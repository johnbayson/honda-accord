Interior Trim Removal/Installation - Trunk Area
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
  1. Remove the trim as shown. Unlock the trunk floor lid to remove it.
  1. Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and replace any damaged clips.
