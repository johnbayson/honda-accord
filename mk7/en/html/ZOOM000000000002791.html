Inner Taillight
  1. Open the trunk lid and disconnect the connectors (A) from the trunk lid.
    Taillight:
    5 W
    Back-up Light:
    21 W
    Rear Fog Light:
    21 W

  1. Remove the three nuts and the inner taillight (B).
  1. Turn the bulb socket 45 ° counterclockwise to remove the bulb.
  1. Inspect the gasket; replace it if it is distorted or stays compressed.
  1. Install the taillight and tighten the nuts to 5 N·m (0.5 kgf·m, 4 lbf·ft).
  1. After installing the taillight, run water over it to make sure it does not leak.
