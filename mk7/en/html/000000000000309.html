Power Window Master Switch (Door Multiplex Control Unit) Replacement
  1. Carefully pry off the driver's door switch trim.
  1. Disconnect the power mirror and power window switch connectors (A).
    NOTE: LHD type is shown, RHD type is similar.
  1. Remove the four mounting screws, then remove the master switch (A) from the panel (B).
    NOTE: LHD type is shown, RHD type is similar.
