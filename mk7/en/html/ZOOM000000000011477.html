SRS components are located in this area. Review the SRS component locations and the precautions and procedures before performing repairs or service.
NOTE:
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  • When using a flat-tip screwdriver, wrap it with protective tape to prevent damage.
  • Take care not to bend and scratch the headliner.
  • Be careful not to damage the dashboard and other interior trim.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Front pillar trim, both sides
    • Ceiling light
    • Front seat belt upper anchor, both sides
    • Center pillar lower trim
    • Center pillar upper trim, both sides
    • Roof end trim
    • Quarter pillar trim, both sides
    • Camera cover, with adaptive cruise control
  1. From both sides, using a handy remover, release the tabs (A), then remove the sunvisor cap (B) from the bracket (C). Turn the cap, and remove it.
