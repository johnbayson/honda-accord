Exhaust Manifold Removal and Installation
K20A3 engine
  1. Remove the rocker arm oil control solenoid (VTEC solenoid valve).
  1. Remove the cover and exhaust manifold bracket, then remove the exhaust manifold.
  1. Install the exhaust manifold and tighten the bolts/nuts in a crisscross pattern in two or three steps, beginning with the inner nut.
  1. Install the other parts in the reverse order of removal.
