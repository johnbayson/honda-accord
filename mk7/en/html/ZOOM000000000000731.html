Front Passenger's Door: With Super Locking
  1. Remove the passenger's door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 6P connector from the actuator.
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals.
    • There should be continuity between the No. 2 and No. 3 terminals when the door lock knob switch is in the UNLOCK position and no continuity when the switch is in the LOCK position.
  1. If the continuity is not as specified, replace the door lock actuator.
