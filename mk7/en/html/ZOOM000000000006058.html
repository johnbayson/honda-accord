Rear Door Sill Area
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  1. Detach the hooks (A) and tab (B) from the center pillar lower trim (C) and the rear side trim panel (D), and pull the rear door sill trim (E) up by hand to detach the clips (F, G), then remove it.
  1. If necessary, pull the rear side outer trim (H) up to detach the clips (I), then remove the trim.
