Clutch Pedal Replacement
  1. Disconnect the clutch pedal position switch connector (A).
  1. Pry out the lock pin (B), and pull the pedal pin (C) out of the yoke.
  1. Remove the master cylinder mounting nuts (D) and clutch pedal mounting bolt (E).
  1. Remove the clutch pedal (F).
  1. Install the clutch pedal (A).
  1. Install the clutch pedal mounting bolt (B) and master cylinder mounting nuts (C).
  1. Apply grease to the pedal pin (D), then install it and new lock pin (E) to the yoke.
  1. Connect the clutch pedal position switch connector (F).
  1. Adjust the clutch pedal and clutch pedal position switch.
