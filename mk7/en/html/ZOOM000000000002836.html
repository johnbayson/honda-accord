1. Attach the rubber dam (A), clips (B) with adhesive tape, and fasteners (C) with adhesive tape to the inside face of the rear window (D) as shown:
    • First attach the rubber dam, then attach the molding (E) around the edge of the rear window. Be sure both end of the molding contact with bottom of the rubber dam. If necessary, cut the rubber dam.
    • Be sure the clips, and fasteners line up with the alignment marks (F).
    • Be careful not to touch the windshield where adhesive will be applied.
    Rubber dam adhesive tape:
    Thickness 0.16 mm (0.0063 in.)
    Width 4 mm (0.16 in.)
    Clips adhesive tape:
    Thickness 0.4 mm (0.016 in.)
    Width 10 mm (0.39 in.)
    Fasteners adhesive tape:
    Thickness 0.8 mm (0.03 in.)
    Width 7 mm (0.28 in.)
