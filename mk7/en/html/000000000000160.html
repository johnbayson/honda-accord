Cowl Cover Replacement
NOTE: LHD is shown; RHD is symmetrical.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Windshield wiper arms
    • Front fender trim, both sides
  1. Remove the hood rear seal (A) by pulling it out. Using a clip remover, detach the clip (B) from the cowl covers. Take care not to scratch the cowl covers.
  1. Detach the clips (C, D) on the passenger's cowl cover (E) by carefully pulling the cover upward. Release the hook (F) on the passenger's cowl cover from the driver's, then remove the cover by releasing the hook (G). Take care not to scratch the body.
  1. Disconnect the windshield washer tubes (H). LHD is shown; RHD is disconnecting the washer tube at the center.
  1. Detach the clips (C, D) on the driver's cowl cover (I) by carefully pulling the cover upward, then remove the cover by releasing the hooks (G). Take care not to scratch the body.
  1. RHD: Disconnect the windshield washer tube.
  1. Install the covers in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Make sure the washer tubes are connected securely.
    • Push the clip portions into place securely.
