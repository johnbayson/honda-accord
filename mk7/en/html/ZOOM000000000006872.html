1. Align the bolt hole (A) on the steering joint with the groove (B) around the pinion shaft, and loosely install the joint bolt (C). Be sure that the joint bolt is securely in the groove in the pinion shaft. Pull on the steering joint to make sure that the steering joint is fully seated. Tighten the steering joint bolt to the specified torque.
  1. Finish the installation, and note these items:
    • Make sure the connectors are properly connected.
    • Reinstall the steering wheel.
    • Reinstall the driver's dashboard lower cover.
    • Reinstall the driver's dashboard under cover.
    • Reinstall the steering joint cover A and column covers.
    • Reconnect the battery.
    • Reset the power window control unit.
    • Enter the anti-theft codes for the radio and the navigation system, then the customer's radio station presets.
    • Set the clock.
    • Verify cruise control, audio remote, navigation guide and turn signal switch operation.
    • Make sure the steering wheel is centered.
