160,000km(8year)
This Maintenance Schedule outlines the minimum required maintenance that you should perform to ensure the trouble-free operation of vehicle. Due to regional and climatic difference, some additional service may be required. Please consult the warranty booklet for a more detailed description.
About replacing engine oil, engine oil filter, manual transmission fluid (MTF) and automatic transmission (ATF)
Use the Normal Conditions schedule for replacing engine oil, engine oil filter, MTF and ATF, if the severe driving conditions specified in the severe driving conditions below do not apply, and if only OCCASIONALLY driving in severe driving conditions. If the vehicle is driven MAINLY in any of the following severe driving conditions, use the Severe Conditions schedule.
Severe Driving Conditions on engine oil and engine oil filter replacement:
  • Driving less than 8 km (5 miles) per trip or, in freezing temperature, driving less than 16 km (10 miles) per trip.
  • Driving in extremely hot over 35 °C (95 °F) conditions.
  • Extensive idling, or long periods of stop-and-go driving
  • Trailer towing, driving with a car-top carrier, or driving in mountains conditions.
  • Driving on muddy, or de-iced roads.
Severe Driving Conditions on MTF and ATF replacement:
  • Driving in extremely hot over 35 °C (95 °F) conditions.
  • Trailer towing, driving with a car-top carrier, or driving in mountainous conditions.
