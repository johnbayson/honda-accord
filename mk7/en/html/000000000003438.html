Emblem Replacement - Supplement
NOTE: When removing the emblems, take care not to scratch the body.
  1. Use a piece of string to cut through the adhesive tape on the emblem.
  1. Clean the body surface with a sponge dampened in alcohol. After cleaning, keep oil, grease, and water from getting on the surface.
  1. Apply the emblems where shown. Align the application tape with the edge of the tailgate, then press the emblem into place, and remove the application tape.
