1. Place a suitable container under the fuel filter to catch up the water.
  1. Connect the hose (A) to water screw (B).
  1. Loosen the water screw at the underside of the fuel filter until water drops.
  1. Drain about 85 m (2.9 fl·oz, 3.0 lmp·oz) to 150 m (5.1 fl·oz, 5.3 lmp·oz) of water.
  1. Tighten the water screw securely.
  1. Bleed the fuel system.
