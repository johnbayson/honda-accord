DTC Troubleshooting: 62
Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) Unit DTC 62:
Millimeter Wave Radar Sensor Error
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II) and the main switch ON.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC 62 indicated?
YES -
Check for loose or poor connections. If OK, replace the millimeter wave radar unit. After replacement, do the millimeter wave radar unit aiming procedure. ■
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
