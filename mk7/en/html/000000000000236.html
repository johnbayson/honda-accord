Power Mirror Actuator Test
  1. Remove the door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 13P connector (A) from the power mirror actuator (B).
  1. Check actuator operation by connecting power and ground according to the table.
  1. If the mirror fails to work properly, replace the mirror actuator.
Defogger Test
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 2 and No. 7 terminals of the 13P connector. There should be continuity with the defogger switch ON. If there is no continuity, check for an open circuit.
