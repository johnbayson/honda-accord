DTC Troubleshooting: 3
DTC 3:
 A Problem in the Air Mix Control Linkage, Door, or Motor
  1. Test the air mix control motor.
Is the air mix control motor OK?
YES -
Substitute a known-good audio-HVAC module, and recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away, replace the original printed circuit board.■
NO -
Replace the air mix control motor, or repair the linkage and doors.■
