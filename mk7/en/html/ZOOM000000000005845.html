Recommended PAG oil: DENSO ND-OIL 8
  • P/N 38897-PR7-003: 120 mL (4 fl·oz, 4.2 lmp·oz)
  • P/N 38898-PR7-003: 250 mL (8 1/3 fl·oz, 8.8 lmp·oz)
  • P/N 38899-PR7-A01: 40 mL (1 1/3 fl·oz, 1.4 lmp·oz)
Add the recommended refrigerant oil in the amount listed if you replace any of the following parts.
  • To avoid contamination, do not return the oil to the container once dispensed, and never mix it with other refrigerant oils.
  • Immediately after using the oil, reinstall the cap on the container, and seal it to avoid moisture absorption.
  • Do not spill the refrigerant oil on the vehicle; it may damage the paint; if it gets on the paint, wash it off immediately.

A/C compressor .
For A/C compressor replacement, subtract the volume of oil drained from the removed A/C compressor from 160 mL (5 1/3 fl·oz, 5.6 lmp·oz), and drain the calculated volume of oil from the new A/C compressor: 160 mL (5 1/3 fl·oz, 5.6 lmp·oz) − Volume of removed A/C compressor = Volume to drain from new A/C compressor.
NOTE: Even if no oil is drained from the removed A/C compressor, don't drain more than 50 mL (1 2/3 fl·oz, 1.8 lmp·oz) from the new A/C compressor.
