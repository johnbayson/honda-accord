Front Pillar - Without Side Curtain Airbags
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  1. Pull out the door opening trim (A) as needed. Pull the upper portion of the front pillar trim (B) back by hand to detach the clips, and pull the trim up, then remove it from the dashboard (C).
  1. Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
