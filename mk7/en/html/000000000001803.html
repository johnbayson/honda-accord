Cargo Area Light Test/Replacement
  1. Disconnect the 2P connectors (A) from the convenience facility garnish (B).
  1. Remove the lens (A) from the convenience facility garnish.
    BULB:
    5 W

  1. Check for continuity between the No. 1 and No. 2 terminals. There should be continuity. If there is no continuity, check the bulb. If the bulb is OK, replace the cargo area light.
