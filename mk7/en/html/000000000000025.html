Master Cylinder Disassembly
NOTE: The illustrations show LHD model with VSA.
  1. Remove the rod seal from the master cylinder.
  1. Remove the circlip (A) while pushing in the secondary piston (B).
  1. Remove the reservoir (C).
  1. For VSA type, remove the stop pin (A) while pushing in the secondary piston (B).
  1. Remove the piston guide (A), secondary cup (ABS type) or O-ring (VSA type) (B), retainer (C), secondary piston (D) and primary piston (E).
  1. Remove the reservoir seal (A) from the reservoir cap (B).
  1. Remove the strainer(C) and grommets (D) from the reservoir (E).
    NOTE: When reservoir and master cylinder body are separated, replace the grommets with new ones.
