DTC Troubleshooting: 41-2x, 41-3x, 41-8x, 41-Bx
DTC 41-2x (41-20 to 41-29, 41-2A to 41-2F),41-3x (41-30 to 41-39, 41-3A to 41-3F),41-8x(41-80 to 41-89, 41-8A to 41-8F),41-Bx (41-B0 to 41-B9, 41-BA to 41-BF):
Internal Failure of the Left Front Impact Sensor
  1. Erase the DTC memory.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and check that the SRS indicator comes on for about 6 seconds and then goes off.
Does the SRS indicator stay on and is DTC 41-2x, 41-3x, 41-8x,or 41-Bx indicated?
YES -
Replace the left front impact sensor. If the DTC returns, replace the SRS unit.■
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Go to Troubleshooting Intermittent Failures. If another DTC is indicated, go to the DTC troubleshooting details.
