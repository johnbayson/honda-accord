A/T Idler Gear/Idler Gear Shaft Replacement
Special Tools Required

Handle driver
 07749-0010000

Driver attachment, 32 x 35 mm
 07746-0010100
  1. Remove the snap ring from the idler gear/idler shaft assembly.
  1. Remove the idler gear shaft (A) from the idler gear (B) with the special tools and a press.
  1. Replace the idler gear or idler gear shaft, and attach the idler gear shaft to the idler gear.
  1. Install the idler gear shaft (A) in the idler gear (B) with the special tools and a press.
  1. Install the snap ring.
