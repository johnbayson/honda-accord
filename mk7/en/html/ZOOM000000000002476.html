1. Remove the three bolts securing the shift cable holder (A), then remove the shift cable cover (B).
  1. Remove the spring clip (C) and control pin (D), then separate the shift cable (E) from the control lever (F).
  1. Insert the new shift cable through the grommet hole. Do not bend the shift cable excessively.
  1. Install the shift cable bracket on the body, then install the grommet.
  1. Verify that the transmission is in the R position at the control lever.
  1. Attach the shift cable end to the control lever, then insert the control pin into the control lever through the shift cable end, and secure the control pin with the spring clip.
  1. Install the shift cable cover, then secure the shift cable holder to the shift cable cover with the two bolts.
  1. Install the heat shield.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and verify that the R position indicator comes on.
