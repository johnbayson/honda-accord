Perform the gauge control module input test. ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Remove the gauge control module.
  1. Disconnect the gauge control module 30P connector.
  1. Disconnect the VSA control unit 47P connector.
  1. If equipped with the Honda navigation system, disconnect the navigation unit 20P connector.
  1. Jump the SCS line with the HDS.
  1. Disconnect ECM/PCM connector E (31P).
  1. Check for continuity between ECM/PCM connector terminals E11, E24 and body ground individually.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the gauge control module, the VSA control unit, the navigation unit and the ECM/PCM (E11, E24), then go to Step 17 .
NO -
Go to Step 13 .
